This is a charming town house, different and unique. The first floor, which is accessed either from the entrance hall, or also from the kitchen itself, has 5 bedrooms and 2 full bathrooms, one of them with a dressing room.
Finally, from the first floor there is access to a small terrace and from there you go up to the roof which has a 90 m2 terrace. Due to its size and design it is the ideal area for rest and relaxation.
