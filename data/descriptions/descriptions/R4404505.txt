Southerly facing two bedroom town house located right at the heart of the picturesque urbanisation of La Heredia.

This Property  is distributed over two floors: The ground floor, elevated above street level, consists of a hallway and 2 en-suite bedrooms. Both bedrooms are a good size, complete with fitted wardrobes, air-conditioning and plenty of natural light.
