A beautiful, modern, 2 bedroom apartment in the ever popular and very well kept complex of Princess Park in Sitio de Calahonda, located in the upper and sought after area of Calahonda, Mijas Costa on the Costa del Sol.
The property consists of a lounge/diner, an independent kitchen, 2 double bedrooms, 2 bathrooms (1 en suite) all with fitted wardrobes and the main bedroom has patios doors with direct access to the terrace area, the large covered terrace is South West facing with views towards the sea.
Marble floors and double glazing throughout and patio doors which lead out to the large luminous terrace, the kitchen has a separate utility area.
Air conditioning, hot and cold and Persian blinds on all the bedroom windows.
The community areas are beautifully maintained with lovely gardens and 4 community pools.
There's a private underground garage space and guest parking throughout the urbanisation.
Ideal property as a permanent or holiday home.
Princess Park is a gated community with 24-h security control. On site video porter and it's only a few minutes walk/drive, to the shops and restaurants. The beaches of Calahonda, Cabopino, Riviera del Sol, Miraflores, Torrenueva and La Cala de Mijas are only a short drive away and Miraflores Golf, La Siesta Golf in Calahonda, Cabopino Golf and Calanova Golf are close by.
Ideally located between Fuengirola and Marbella at only 20 minutes to the famous Puerto Banus and less than a 30 minute drive from the Malaga international airport
