Large, bright, south-east facing apartment in Majestic Hills, within walking distance of the beach with three ensuite bedrooms and pleasant views of the mountains, gardens and the sea.

Majestic Hills is located in Casares Costa, within walking distance of the beach and all amenities and within an easy drive of Estepona and Sotogrande.

The property has three double bedrooms, each with its own ensuite bathroom; including the principal bedroom suite which has access to the main terrace and has double basins and jacuzzi bathtub.  The second bedroom has a small private terrace also.  

The apartment has a very large lounge with feature open fireplace and French doors out onto the huge terrace. There are sea views from the lounge, terrace, and two of the bedrooms. The apartment has extensive storage, including wardrobes in every bedroom.  There is an extra-large kitchen which is fully fitted with Bosch appliances, including dishwasher and tumble dryer in a separate utility room.  There is also a guest lavatory.

The apartment is well appointed with marble floors throughout, air-conditioning and underfloor heating in the bathrooms. The property will be sold alongside an underground parking space and store room.

Majestic Hills is a very well presented community with two swimming pools and spectacular tropical gardens.
