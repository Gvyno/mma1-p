Beautiful renovated 3 bed semi detached house next to the Chaparral Golf Club, near La Cala.
On the entrance level you will find a dressing room and entrance to a private garage.

On the second floor, there is a living room with access to a private terrace with a jacuzzi and BBQ area, one newly renovated modern bathroom, and one good-sized bedroom. The kitchen is fully equipped with Bosch appliances and Silestone countertops. On the third floor there are two bedrooms with a good size wardrobes, a bathroom and two terraces with views over the mountains and glimts of the sea.

The well maintained urbanization is quiet and has swimming pools, sauna, basketball court, tennis and paddle courts. The community offers a comfortable environment to live and do activities.

Nearby is the Bosque del Chaparral where you can enjoy pleasant walks in nature and the beach is only 10 minutes away.  Ideal area to enjoy the sea, golf and the forest. 
It's about 10 minutes drive to Fuengirola and the Miramar shopping mall.
