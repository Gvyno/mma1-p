Detached Villa on 1 floor located in Pinos de Alhaurin on a large plot.

The property is mainly distributed as follow:
Entrance hall, fully equipped kitchen with separate utility room and toilet, spacious living room with dining area and fireplace with direct access to the main terrace, master bedroom with dressing area and on suite bathroom, 3 double bedrooms, full bathroom. All the bedrooms have fitted wardrobes and AC hot/cold.
In the basement, there is approx. 84m2 with a Bodega.
Outside: Driveway for several cars, swimming pool of 54m2, BBQ area, toilet, utility room, approx. 200m2 of land (on the side of the property) dedicated for fruit trees and olive trees.
There is an extra 12.000L water deposit and solar panels for hot water. Electricity and water are connected from the townhall.
