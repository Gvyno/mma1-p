This attractive villa is situated in one of Marbella's premier residential estates, Cascada de Camojan in Sierra Blanca, with 24 hour manned security and comprehensive video surveillance. A charming villa, built to a good standard and offering complete privacy to its residents. The expansive rooms have high ceilings, good quality materials and all the bedrooms offer ensuite bathrooms. The villa sits on a plot of  1750 meters with private swimming pool and landscaped gardens. Perfect for either a holiday home or full time living, the property also offers great potential for new owners to put their mark on it.
