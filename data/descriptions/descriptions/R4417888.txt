Modern new apartment with sunny terrace in a private and exclusive residential complex with pool. 

The flat has a bright living area with open, fully equipped kitchen and access to the south-facing terrace. From here there is a lateral view of the beautiful sea.  

The master bedroom has access to the front garden. Here you can already enjoy the morning sun. The second bedroom has a beautiful view into the garden. 

The main bathroom has a bathtub with shower. The second bathroom has a walk-in shower. 

Within a few minutes' walk you can reach various shopping facilities for daily needs, restaurants and the beautiful beach. 

The amenities include air conditioning with heat pump (aerothermal system) as well as double-glazed windows, a parking space in the underground garage and a cellar room. The flat is offered furnished.
