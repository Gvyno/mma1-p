Welcome to this amazing duplex penthouse in La Mesana, on San Pedro beach!

The apartment is situated in La Mesana, a small development of only 40 properties located just 200m from San Pedro beach and the amazing restaurants. Gated community with fantastic gardens and swimming pool.

This fantastic penthouse offers a spacious entrance leading to the living room which, in turn, leads onto a south west facing terrace. The large kitchen is fully equipped with space for a dining/kitchen table and leads to a separate utility/laundry area. 

There is one bedroom en-suite as well as a guest toiler on the entrance level. On the upper level you will find the master bedroom with walk-in closet and en-suite bathroom. From the master bedroom you can access the huge roof terrace with space for lounge, dining, sunbeds and an outdoor kitchen. You can also access this roof terrace from the down stairs terrace.

Fantastic views over the mature gardens and La Concha mountain.

Garage space and a big storage room included in the price.
