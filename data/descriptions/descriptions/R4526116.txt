Meisho Hills presents an acclaimed, exclusive residential enclave located within the highly regarded gated community of Sierra Blanca in Marbella. Comprising 58 opulent townhouses, this development affords awe-inspiring vistas that stretch from Marbella and Puerto Banus to Gibraltar and Africa, establishing Meisho Hills as a premier destination on the Costa del Sol.

This exceptional property has been meticulously crafted without compromise, emphasizing modern comfort and cutting-edge design while harnessing the advantages of contemporary architecture. The residence boasts a meticulously curated selection of materials, seamlessly blending wood, stone, and marble, and incorporates state-of-the-art conveniences, including intelligent home systems that can be effortlessly controlled via an iPad, underfloor heating, capacious and chic fitted wardrobes, air conditioning, and a robust alarm system. The interior has been tastefully furnished with carefully chosen designer pieces.

The primary level introduces a luminous living area with expansive glass doors, a guest lavatory, and a utility space adjoined to a streamlined, minimalist kitchen featuring a stylish central island/bar and premium appliances, all with access to the enchanting main terrace. Ascending the modern staircase with metal railings leads to the second level, where you will find the generous master suite with an en-suite bathroom and a private terrace. Additionally, this level offers another bedroom with an attached bathroom and access to an expansive south-facing terrace. The basement level provides direct entry to the private garage, accommodating two vehicles and offering ample storage.

Meisho Hills stands as a meticulously maintained, fully secured community with 24-hour security, providing access to two expansive outdoor swimming pools and three spa areas that include fitness facilities, indoor swimming pools, saunas, Turkish baths, and tranquil relaxation spaces. Nestled at the base of the mountains and enveloped by the natural beauty of Sierra Blanca, including renowned hiking trails, this development seamlessly blends seclusion with the convenience of proximity to key locales like Malaga airport and Marbella.
