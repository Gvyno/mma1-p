Beautiful apartment in Valgrande, located in Sotogrande Alto, next to the Valderrama Golf Club, on the first floor of a 2-story building. Situated in one of the best buildings in the prestigious Valgrande development, it offers 24-hour security, a communal pool, beautiful gardens, paddle and tennis courts, a spa, gym, and a golf practice area.

The apartment comprises an entrance hall, a fully equipped kitchen with a laundry area, and a 70m2 terrace. There's a spacious living/dining room with access to a large terrace with beautiful garden views, 3 double ensuite bedrooms, and 1 guest toilet. It features underfloor heating, air conditioning (hot/cold), 2 parking spaces, and 2 storage rooms.

Valgrande is considered one of the best developments in Sotogrande, known for its tranquility and extensive green areas. It boasts three outdoor pools, a gym, a spa with jacuzzi, sauna, and changing rooms, paddle and tennis courts, and a putting green. The development is gated and has 24-hour security.
