Large townhouse with great access in Casarabonela.

Boasting plently of outside space, this two storey townhouse would make a wonderful family home.

You enter into a grande living room with a snug to the left that could be a dining room and the kitchen to the rear. A set of stairs leads you up to 2 double bedrooms.

There are also stairs from the interior Andalucian patio to a large storeroom a further double bedroom and a large roof terrace. The property does require some updating but has all the traditional character there to make your self at home in Casarabonela.
