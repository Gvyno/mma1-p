Casa Cenote presents itself as a luxurious villa, exuding the ambiance of a beach resort, nestled in a serene Nueva Andalucia neighborhood, within walking distance of restaurants and bars. The villa features an expansive infinity pool, sandy areas, and a pool reminiscent of a natural cave with a cascading waterfall, all set within a Bali-inspired atmosphere. This remarkable villa accommodates up to 12 guests and is brimming with delightful surprises, from laid-back chill zones to a built-in jacuzzi, a paradisiacal pool, a fully equipped gym and spa adorned with bamboo accents, a wine cellar, a cinema room, a bar, a pool table, and even a basketball court.
