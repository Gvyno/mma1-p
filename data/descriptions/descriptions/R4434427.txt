Cosy townhouse in Benahavís centre.

This home is a proper Spanish village house located right in the centre of Benahavís and only a few steps away from all the amenities, bars, restaurants, shops and facilities.

The property is a 1-bedroom townhouse laid out on 3 levels. It consists of an open-plan kitchen and living room on the main level. The bedroom, bathroom and dressing area are on the first floor and a good-sized rooftop terrace where you can enjoy the sun right in the centre of Benahavís.
