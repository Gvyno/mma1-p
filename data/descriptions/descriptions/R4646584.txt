Modern brand-new apartment in a residential complex of 90 2- and 3-bedroom apartments situated in a privileged location to the south-west of Marbella, in Nueva Andalucía. 

Situated on the fourth floor of the building, the property comprises: 2 bedrooms, one of them en suite and one with separate bathroom; fully fitted and equipped kitchen; living/dining room with access to a covered terrace to which one of the bedrooms also has access.

The area is family-friendly and very quiet, yet it is located next to supermarkets, schools, sports facilities, children's playgrounds… The apartments are spacious and bright, perfect for families who wish to live close to some of the best golf courses, the beach and Puerto Banús. The complex is fully gated and boasts a large swimming pool, solarium and chill out area.
