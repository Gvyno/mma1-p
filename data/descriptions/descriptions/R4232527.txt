Beautiful Andalusian style villa!!

There are two villas, the main one measuring 410m2 divided into 7 large bedrooms, 4 bathrooms, a large kitchen with a utility room and a living-dining room with a fireplace.

On the plot with its separate entrance there is another 140m2 villa consisting of an entrance porch, living room, large kitchen, 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms, it has air conditioning, storage room, large patio for vehicles.

The villa has incredible views over the Guadalhorce valley. The gardens have a large swimming pool, outdoor dining area and barbecue area.
