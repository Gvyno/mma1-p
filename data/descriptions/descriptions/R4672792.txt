Modern fully refurbished apartment in a lovely complex known as Selwo Hills, Estepona. Urbanisation is gated and there are large swimming pool, tropical gardens and gym. 

Apartment is on the first floor with 4 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms. In the salon there is a spacious living-dining room connected to a large terrace with south facing views out to the Sea and gardens. A fully equipped kitchen with laundry room. Other features include air conditioning, fitted wardrobes, marble floors, underfloor heating shutters on the terrace, two storage rooms and three underground parkings.

It's a nice family friendly urbanisation in a quiet area on the New Golden Mile between Marbella and Estepona, within few minutes driving to the beach, restaurants, shops and other amenities.
