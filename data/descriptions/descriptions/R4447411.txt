This charming 1-bedroom apartment is located in the picturesque urbanization of Las Colinas del Faro, in the beautiful town of Mijas, on the Costa del Sol. With an elevated location, it offers stunning panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea and the surrounding mountains.

Upon entering the apartment, you will be greeted by open spaces and modern decor. The living room is bright and welcoming, with large windows allowing in plenty of natural light. From the living room, you can access a large terrace, perfect for enjoying the sea views and relaxing outdoors. This terrace is an ideal place to dine outdoors or simply enjoy the warm climate of the Costa del Sol.
