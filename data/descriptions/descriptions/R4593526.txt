Nueva Andalucia , Marbella
South west facing  2 bedroom, 2 bathroom apartment on the first floor of a very nice and well established residential urbanization within a short walking distance to the famous Puerto Banus (with all its exclusive amenities) and the sandy Mistral / Rodeo beach. The apartment is bright and has a south-west facing terrace overlooking the swimming pool and the manicured community  gardens. This complex also features 2 padel courts.
Parking and small storage unit included in the price
The beach and Puerto Banus are within a 12-15 minute walk from the development.
Opposite this complex you will find a tennis club and 250 metres away the famous Real Club de padel with one of the most impresive gyms in Costa del Sol
