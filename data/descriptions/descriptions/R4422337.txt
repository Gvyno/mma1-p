This luxurious four-bedroom townhouse in the centre of Calahonda offers a blend of modern comfort and convenience. Spread across four levels, it boasts spacious living areas and high-quality Bosch appliances in the open-plan kitchen. The ground floor seamlessly connects to an expansive terrace and a private garden, perfect for outdoor living.
Ascending the feature floating staircase leads to the top floor, where the master bedroom suite and two guest bedrooms reside, along with access to the rooftop solarium, offering panoramic views. The lower floor hosts two additional large rooms and two private parking spaces, ensuring ample space for residents.
Key features include Climalit double glazing, electric shutters in the bedrooms, and underfloor heating in all bathrooms, enhancing comfort and energy efficiency. Moreover, the property is part of a luxurious community spread over 3,000m2 of landscaped gardens. Residents can enjoy amenities such as a large communal pool, sun deck, onsite security, concierge service, CCTV system, fully equipped gymnasium, and underground parking with two additional basement rooms.
With its central location, residents have easy access to essential amenities and are just a short distance from the picturesque sandy beach. This townhouse epitomizes luxury living in Calahonda, offering both tranquillity and convenience within reach.
