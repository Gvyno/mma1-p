3 bedroom, 2 bathroom middle floor apartment with breath-taking sea views.
This spacious three bedroom, 2 bathroom apartment is set within the Campos del Mar complex.
Situated on the upper slopes of Calahonda with panoramic sea & mountain views and within 10 minutes' walk of local bars and restaurants. 5 minutes' drive to local sandy beaches and supermarkets.

Only 15 minute drive to either Marbella or Fuengirola along the coast.

This modern apartment is nicely furnished and equipped to a very high standard with air conditioning throughout.
