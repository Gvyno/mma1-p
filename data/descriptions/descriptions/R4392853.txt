Rural House of 4000m2 of land all deeded

In Jubrique, 30 km from Estepona, with incredible views of the entire Sierra Bermeja. Spectacular gastronomy, nature, hiking trails and very close to the coast.

The house has 82 m2, and the plot 4000m2.

It is located at the entrance of the town, a 3-minute walk from all the services that Jubrique offers. It has direct access from the road that reaches Jubrique and is completely paved. It has a parking area with a capacity for 4-5 vehicles and a recreation area, barbecue and solarium with spectacular views.

The house is completely renovated and furnished. It has a kitchen open to the living room with a fireplace, 3 bedrooms and a bathroom with a shower, and all with fitted wardrobes.

The house is self-sufficient at the energy level as it has photovoltaic panels and gas supply. The water is from the municipal network and has a septic tank.
It has olive trees, almond trees, plum trees, figs and chestnut trees.
