Charming detached bungalow in a quiet residential area, close to both Fuengirola and Mijas Pueblo.  The property consists of a spacious lounge, separate dining kitchen, four double bedrooms, a bathroom and shower room.  Outside there is a utility room, storage room and private, single car garage.  Most of the external areas are terrace with the option to park multiple cars and there are two orange trees.  The main terrace area has a large toldo to provide shade in the summer and the garden area with flowers and bushes always looks beautiful when in flower.  The property has been rewired throughout by the present owner and also had solar panels fitted to the roof for economical electricity.  This would make a fantastic family home for anyone currently or planning to live in Mijas permanently.
