Los Arquerous Golf & Country Club is a prestigious golf and country club located in Benahavis, it is known for its picturesque setting, offering stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea and surrounding landscapes. 

The club features an 18 hole golf course designed by Seve Ballesteros, known for its challenging layout and well maintained greens.  Besides golf, Los Arquerous offers a range of top-notch amenities for its members and guests.  These include a clubhouse with dining options, a pro shop, practice facilities and professional coaching.  The country club also has tennis courts, fitness centre and bowling alley making it an ideal destination for sports enthusiasts and those seeking a luxurious lifestyle.
