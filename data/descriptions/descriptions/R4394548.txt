This unique property located near Pizarra boasts an old oil mill, sitting on a vast expanse of land. The mill, which was in operation until the 1950s, has been preserved and retains all of its original machinery, including grinding stones, a press, a boiler, and a well. The cellar, complete with jars, adds to the historical charm of the property.

The mill is situated on a sprawling olive grove, covering an impressive 65,088 square meters of land. The lower area of the farm is dedicated to a productive lemon orchard, creating a picturesque  setting. The house is in need of total renovation.

The mill itself offers approximately 370 square meters of living space, with ample room for various uses. This unique and historic property is a rare find and presents a multitude of opportunities for its new owner. Whether you're looking for a serene countryside retreat or a unique investment opportunity, this Old oil mill near Pizarra is a must-see.
