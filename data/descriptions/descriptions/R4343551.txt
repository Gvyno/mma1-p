A very large and spacious townhouse in the popular Monte Paraiso complex in lower Calahonda. Refurbished and immaculately presented throughout, this lovely home offers flexible living space with 2 bedrooms upstairs, 1 bedroom on the ground floor and a large self contained apartment downstairs with bedroom and bathroom. There is a large terrace off the lounge offering afternoon sun as well as 2 terraces off each bedroom upstairs and one off the apartment downstairs. The community has beautiful gardens and pool shared between only 11 houses. It is walking distance to all of the amenities that Calahonda has to offer as well as the beach.
