Large four bedroom house with off street parking, private garden and large lounge and sea views from the first floor.

The property is distributed on two levels with a spacious lounge on the ground floor, as well as a separate kitchen, guest lavatory and one bedroom, which is currently used as an office.  There is also a separate utility room.

The ground floor terrace is spacious and quite private.

The first floor of the property has three bedrooms, including the principal bedroom suite which has a large ensuite bathroom.  There are two guest bedrooms, and a family bathroom.  Two of the bedrooms have access to the small upstairs terrace, which has wonderful views.

There is off street parking for one car, and the community is gated with a large communal swimming pool.

Viewings are recommended.
