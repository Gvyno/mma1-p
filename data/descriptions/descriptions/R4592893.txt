Wonderful one bedroom duplex apartment with lovely sea views in Calahonda.
Located in a very well maintained and popular beachfront complex, this property is ideal as a holiday home or for investment purposes.
It comprises a modern open-plan kitchen, a large living/dining area, 2 bathrooms and 1 bedroom that opens to a large terrace with lovely partial sea views.
The owners of the property/guests have access to the main outdoor pool, tennis courts, and a GYM with spa (both the latter at an additional cost).
