Looking for a property that is only a few steps away from the beach? Then this Beach House in urbanisation Aldea beach in Manilva is something for you!

Situated in a first line beach urbanization, in one the best and most green areas of Manilva. 

When you enter the bright and spacious house you feel already home, nicely decorated and lots of light coming trough the big glass windows. On the main floor the house has a very big lounge and dining area,
separate toilet and a semi open fitted kitchen. From the living area you can go directly to the terrace and lovely garden. When you go up the stairs to the first floor you will find a large master bedroom with en suite bathroom and a large guest bedroom with en suite bathroom. 

The house comes with a private parking space. The community has extensive landscaped gardens, big new swimming pool and kids pool, tennis and basketball court.  

The beach in front of this urbanization is wide and has 3 beach bars/restaurants in walking distance, also the walking path brings you up to Castillo and La Duquesa Port or to the other side to Punta Chullera. 
Manilva is located at the southern western edge of the province of Malaga next to Sotogrande. The town is split into two parts, the seaside resort and the charming white-washed village.
