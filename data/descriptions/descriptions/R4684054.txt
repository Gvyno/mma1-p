This is a great opportunity to purchase a very spacious townhouse distributed over three floors with a large roof top terrace enjoying superb countryside and mountain views. The house is located in the cente of Alhaurin el Grande town close to Plaza Baja and is in need of some updating. On entering the property you have a large entrance hall, lounge, kitchen, bathroom and an enclosed patio, on the first floor are three good sized bedrooms, the second bathroom and a small terrace area with steps leading up to a very big roof top terrace which overlooks the town and has stunning views across the countryside to the mountains.
This spacious home is within walking distance of everything the countryside town of Alhaurin el Grande has to offer including shops, fantastic restaurants, schools and sports facilities, it is just 20 minutes to Malaga and would be ideal for a family living permanently or as a holiday home. There are parking spaces nearby the house.
