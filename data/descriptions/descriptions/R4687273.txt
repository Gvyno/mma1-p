Welcome to the pleasant and reputable Puerto Blanco and this three bedroom apartment. This bright and cozy accommodation is ideal both for those looking for a permanent home and for those who want a holiday home. Located in an area that houses a wide range of amenities such as bars, restaurants, shops and Estepona harbour, this apartment meets the needs of a diverse crowd. Next to the property you will also find an extensive padel facility complete with gym and restaurant.

A shared pool area is available to all residents, which further enhances the already appealing experience of this residence. The apartment consists of three bedrooms and two bathrooms as well as a nice and easy-to-use living room and a functional kitchen. In addition, it offers a bright and inviting terrace with generous space for furniture, accessible from both the living room and the kitchen. Here the sun can be enjoyed from afternoon to evening.

The price also includes a garage space and a storage room
