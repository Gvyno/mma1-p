Wonderful residential land in Cerros del Aguila Mijas. 

Uses: Multi-family housing, single-family, detached and semi-detached. 
Excellent opportunity to build an apartment complex (PB + 3 heights). 
If a plot project were to be carried out for a single-family housing project, the conditions would be as follows:
- For a plot between 600 - 1,200 m², only PB+1 could be built with maximum occupancy of 55.00%.
- For a plot between 1.200 - 2.400 m², only PB+2 could be built with a maximum occupancy of 50,00%.

40min from Malaga, 30 min from Marbella, 13 min from Fuengirola.
