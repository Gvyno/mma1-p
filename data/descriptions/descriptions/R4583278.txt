Huge plot with sea views in La Quinta Golf . Investment opportunity 
Flat land situated in a very private street in the lower side of La Quinta Golf, very close to Los Naranjos Golf and La Quinta golf courses. By the normative there is a 29% of an allowed built area, means 500m2 that can be built above the land level plus a basement and terraces, in total a project can be around 800-900m2. It offers amazing open sea views from the ground level and a lot of space for a garden and swimming pool area.  An attractive investment opportunity to build a luxury villa for resale or great option for family home.
