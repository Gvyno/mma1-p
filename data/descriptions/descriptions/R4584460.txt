DUPLEX PENTHOUSE IN THE HEART OF NEW ANDALUCIA

Spectacular four bedroom duplex penthouse located in the privileged area of Nueva Andalucia, Marbella with East and West orientation. This property has 171,49 m2 built, 83,80 m2 of terraces and is distributed in two levels. On the main floor we find a spacious living room with fully fitted American style kitchen with access to a large terrace with panoramic views to the mountain and gardens. We can also find another smaller terrace, a laundry room with guest toilet, 2 bedrooms and 1 bathroom. The first floor has two large bedrooms and a bathroom. One of the bedrooms offers a private terrace. All bedrooms have air conditioning. 
This high quality duplex-penthouse is located on the second and last floor of the building (it does not have an elevator) and is part of the urbanisation La Maestranza which offers swimming pools, gardens and communal areas as well as a private garage. 
The duplex-penthouse is only a few minutes walking distance to shops, bars, restaurants, supermarkets... Less than 5 minutes drive to Puerto Banus, 3 minutes drive to Centro Plaza shopping centre, 5 minutes drive to Aloha College, 5 minutes drive to golf courses, 15 minutes drive to Marbella centre and 20 minutes walk to the beach.
