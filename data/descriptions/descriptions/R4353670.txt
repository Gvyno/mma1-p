This spacious townhouse is located in a secure and well taken care of urbanization in Nagueles, close to the Golden Mile and a short drive to the beach and Marbella center.
The complex has a nice garden, two swimming pools and a paddel court.
On the main floor there is a nice living dining area with chimney, a terrace, a good size kitchen with workroom and a guest toilet.
The first floor consists of 2 bedrooms, one of them with a bathroom with jacuzzi en-suite and terrace, and another bathroom.
The top floor a bedroom with en suite bathroom and a solarium.
The basement of the townhouse consists of a big room, which can be used as guest apartment, playroom or office and a garage with space for two cars and direct access to the house.
