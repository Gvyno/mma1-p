Stunning contemporary flat located on the 12th floor in an exclusive area of Malaga, Perchel Sur.

Facing south, this residence boasts a bright living room opening onto a large terrace, providing comfortable outdoor seating and dining areas with magnificent views of the sea and port. The separate and generously-sized kitchen is fully equipped with high-quality appliances. The sleeping quarters consist of three bedrooms, one of which has direct access to the terrace. The bedrooms are spacious and include walk-in sliding closets. Additionally, there are 2 complete bathrooms, one of which is en-suite and the other one is shared, plus a guest toilet.

This property has been completely renovated to the highest standards. Designed with a modern aesthetic in light colors, it features wooden floors and impeccable finishes.

Ideally situated in the very centre of Malaga, the location offers the convenience of having all amenities practically at your doorstep, including commercial centers, shops, all types of services, as well as close proximity to the beach promenade, historic centre, train and bus stations.

Included with the property is a parking space in the underground garage of the building.
