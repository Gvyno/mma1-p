Perfectly located penthouse apartment with incredible rooftop solarium with 360-degree views of the sea, golf and mountains.

Of course the apartment has a spacious lounge and a fully equipped kitchen, as well as a utility and laundry room.

The property is perfect for family holidays as well as permanent living as short term rentals are restricted.
