Best unit in Las Flores Residenciales, a magnificent complex of 17 terraced houses located in Los Pacos.

Las Flores is divided into two complexes; Complex I consists of 8 houses, and Complex II consists of 9 houses. Both are surrounded by community gardens, and the complex has a lovely communal pool.

This townhouse has three bedrooms, three bathrooms, one of which is en-suite, a spacious living/dining room, a fully fitted kitchen, two large terraces, a solarium, two private garage spaces and a lot of privacy.

It is within walking distance of Spanish and International schools, sports facilities (tennis, padel tennis), a supermarket, shops, cafes, restaurants, a dog playground and only 500m to the nearest bakery.

Los Pacos is becoming increasingly popular, particularly for people with young families.

There is a frequent bus service to Fuengirola town centre. The beach is only 10 minutes away, and Málaga airport is 15 minutes by car.
