Located within the renowned Club Sierra complex, this charming elevated townhouse features three very large terraces, all facing the south! The three bedrooms are conveniently situated upon entry, while the upper level hosts a living room with a fireplace, a dining area, and a kitchen. Additionally, a separate glass-enclosed living space is perfectly designed to capture the winter sun. Each level offers breathtaking views, and an open solarium provides an ideal spot for relaxation and sunbathing.

Though this property requires some renovation, it presents an excellent opportunity for creating a splendid holiday retreat or a comfortable year-round residence. The community has stunning tropical gardens and water features, along with a spacious elevated pool area and convenient on-site restaurant.

The property is located a mere 10 minute drive to Puerto Banus, 5 minute drive to Puente Romano and 3 minutes to Aloha.
