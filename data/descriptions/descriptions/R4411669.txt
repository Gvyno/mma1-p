This 2 bedroom apartment is newly renovated and is located in an urbanization by the sea: Bahia de La Plata, on the new Golden Mile, between San Pedro and Estepona. The apartment is south-facing and has a beautiful view of the urbanization and the sea. The apartment has 2 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, 1 guest toilet, living-dining area with integrated kitchen, which leads to the large terrace. There is a parking lot and storage room to the apartment. The urbanization has 24 hours of security service, swimming pools, indoor pool and direct access to the beach promenade.
