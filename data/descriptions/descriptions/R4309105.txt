A great opportunity to buy a 4-bedroom Villa with an independent 2-bedroom guest house!

Located in a beautiful rural location just outside Alora, on the way to El Chorro and the famous walk of "El caminito del Rey".

The spacious master bedroom is light and bright with double windows and a vaulted ceiling. The other two bedrooms both have independent access to the first-floor terrace, with stairs leading down to the garden below. From this terrace you have great views over the valley of the sun.
