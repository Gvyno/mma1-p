This amazing contemporary villa is located front-line Las Brisas Golf (Nueva Andalucia) and ideally set at the end of a private cul-de-sac.
It has a modern design with high-quality finishes, open plan layout and large rooms, ample outdoor entertaining area and infinity pool from where you can enjoy views over the golf court.
It's a plot of 1549 m2, with 6 bedrooms and 7 bathrooms; it includes an art SPA with an indoor pool, sauna and Turkish bath, an indoor gym, a cinema room and entertainment space with a customised bar, wine bodega and pool table.
