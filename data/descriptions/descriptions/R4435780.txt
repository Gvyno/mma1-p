Fantastic three bedroom, south facing townhouse located in Riviera Beach, Estepona. This residential gated community has communal gardens and swimming pool and is located close to the beach, shops, restaurants and amenities. The property on the entrance level has an open plan living and dining area with built in fireplace and access to a private patio; fully fitted open plan kitchen; and a guest toilet. The first floor has the master suite; two guest bedrooms; and a guest bathroom. The next level leads to the solarium with views to the swimming pool and sea. Other features include hot and cold air conditioning, fitted wardrobes and a car port for one car.
