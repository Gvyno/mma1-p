Welcome to this charming townhouse located on the main street, The Strip, in the heart of Calahonda. This prime location offers the perfect blend of coastal living and urban convenience, with all your daily needs just a stone's throw away. From bars and restaurants to shopping, everything you want is within easy reach.

Spread over three floors, this terraced house provides a spacious and well-organized living space. The ground floor welcomes you with a comfortable living room with kitchen, a practical guest toilet, a generous dining room and a large terrace.

Ascending to the first floor, you will find two expansive bedrooms, each with its own private balcony. The newly renovated bathroom on this floor adds a touch of modern luxury to your living experience.

The journey continues to the top floor where a spacious bedroom awaits, complete with en-suite bathroom for added privacy. Connected to this bedroom is a roof terrace which offers a fantastic view of the sea and provides plenty of space for sun loungers. This terrace will become your favorite place, ideal for relaxing and enjoying the coastal beauty.
