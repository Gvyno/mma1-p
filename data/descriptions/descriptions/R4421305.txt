Cozy town house located in a quiet area of Alhaurin with easy parking and close to all services.
The house is in a very good state of conservation and is distributed over two floors. It has an entrance from two streets. On the ground floor we have a large living room from where you access a beautiful Andalusian style patio. Completely renovated and equipped kitchen with direct access to the mentioned patio, where there is also a laundry room and full bathroom. Separate dining room and double bedroom that could be converted into a garage. At the top there is a large hall from which you can access three double bedrooms, two of them with direct access to an outside balcony. From here we go out to a small terrace with a storage room and access to a large roof terrace with beautiful views of the town and the Sierra de Alhaurin. Your visit is recommended.
