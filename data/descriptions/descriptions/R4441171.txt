***Close to the Beaches of Casares*** Cortijos de la Bahia, Casares Costa is a gated urbanisation of townhouses situated a short distance from the beaches, fantastic golf courses and local amenities. The urbanisation has plenty of gardens and benefits from two communal pool areas. This 3 bedroom, 2 bathroom townhouse is spacious throughout. The entrance leads to an open plan living area with a kitchen. The stairs ascend to the middle floor with a choice of 2 bedrooms with ample wardrobe storage and a family bathroom. The stairs ascend further to the master suite with an en-suite bathroom and the door leading to the roof terrace - perfect for sunbathing! This property is ideal for permanent living or coastal holidays as it is only a short walk to beaches of Casares. It is a blank canvas - perfect for those wanting to put their own stamp on a home in the sun. Viewing is highly recommended.
