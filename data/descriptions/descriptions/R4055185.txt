This amazing villa is nestled in the hills of the very attractive urbanization Campo Mijas, with panoramic views of the green surroundings to the sea. Away from all the crowds, yet within easy access of all amenities this wonderful area has on offer, only a 7 minute drive from the beaches and Fuengirola centre. The villa is built in typical Andalusian style and renovated in 2019. Built to high standards and perfectly combining modern design with a Nordic touch.

When entering this beautiful house to the first level, the modern decoration harmoniously melt together with a panoramic view over Fuengirola and the Mediterranean Sea. You instantly feel at ease. The great topic of this house is that the living area and pool deck are all on same level offering indoor and outdoor living space merging seamlessly. The pool is fitted with an electric cover and  heat pump to extend the swimming-season year-round.

The fully equipped kitchen presents itself with a dining area, utility room, guest toilet and direct access to the terrace. The three bedrooms are positioned on the next floor, all spacious and bright and with great views. The master bedroom has an en suite. The two guest rooms share the guest bathroom.

Going down the stairs you will find a good size gym, a patio area and a big garden where children can enjoy playing. Please book your visit to see this perfect family home with your own eyes.
