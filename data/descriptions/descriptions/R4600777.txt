The Edge is an spectacular residential development in which the prestigious architect Rafael de La-Hoz has removed any visual barriers between the sea and the homes. Nestled on the ground floor, this exquisite apartment boasts a prime location right on the beach, offering unparalleled access to sun, sea, and sand. With two generously-sized bedrooms and two luxurious bathrooms, this residence is the epitome of comfort and style. 

As you step into the bright and airy living space, you'll be greeted by the soothing sounds of the nearby waves. The open-concept design seamlessly connects the living and dining areas, creating a welcoming atmosphere for entertaining or simply unwinding after a day of seaside adventures.

The well-appointed kitchen features modern appliances and sleek countertops, providing the perfect setting for culinary delights. 

The two bedrooms are havens of relaxation, each offering a tranquil retreat with ample space and natural light. Wake up to the gentle sound of waves and step out onto your private patio to savor your morning coffee with the salty breeze caressing your face.

With two elegantly designed bathrooms, your comfort is paramount. 

Step outside onto your private terrace, where the beach is just a stone's throw away. The lush garden surroundings create a serene outdoor space, perfect for al fresco dining, sunset cocktails, or simply basking in the warmth of the sun.

The urbanisation has a big outdoor pool, inside swimming pool, Health & Social club with co-working space, paddle court, gym and Spa.
