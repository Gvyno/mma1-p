Incredible two bedroom, south facing apartment in the sought after gated community of La Trinidad, on Marbella´s Golden Mile. This stunning apartment is located in one of the best blocks in the community with wonderful sea views. La Trinidad offers residents facilities such 24hr gated security; well kept gardens; communal swimming pools; an indoor heated pool; gym; sauna; seasonal café; as well as being a short walk to the beach and surrounded by local amenities. The property consists of an open plan living and dining area with access to a private terrace with magnificent sea views and views of the pool; fully fitted kitchen with separate laundry area; two bedrooms with en suite bathrooms and access to the terrace; and a guest toilet. Other features include hot and cold air conditioning, marble floors, private parking, storeroom and electric blinds.
