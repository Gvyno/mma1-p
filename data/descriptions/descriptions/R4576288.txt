Beautiful family house located in the heart of the town of Fuente de Piedra. This stunning townhouse has a ground area of 220m2 and a living area of 180m2, in addition to enjoying a 55m2 patio and a 22m2 garage that can easily accommodate two vehicles.

Spread over two floors, the first floor comprises a spacious and bright living room, a dining room, a fitted kitchen, a pantry, two bedrooms, and a bathroom.
From the kitchen, you can access a large 55m2 tiled patio, which has an access to the street as well as to the garage.

On the upper floor, you will find 5 bedrooms and a bathroom.

The house is sold unfurnished, making it ideal for a large family or for conversion into a guesthouse.

Ideally located near the city center and close to all shops and amenities, it's just an hour away from Malaga airport
