Great south facing corner semi-detached house, unfurnished, located in a private urbanization on the golf course of Cabopino. 
The house is divided into three floors, on the top floor is the master bedroom, very spacious, with on suite bathroom and a large terrace with beautiful sea views, on the main floor is the kitchen, very bright, fully equipped and with laundry area, living room with fireplace with access to another terrace also with beautiful views and a guest toilet, on the first floor is a storage room and 2 bedrooms with a bathroom to share connected by another terrace and garden.
The house has a fireplace, air conditioning hot and cold, alarm system and 2 uncovered parking spaces.
The orientation is south and has lots of light, plus fantastic views of the sea and the urbanization. Located on the golf course of Cabopino, a few minutes from the port of Cabopino and restaurants and services.
