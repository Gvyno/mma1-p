A spacious country property with beautiful panoramic views of the Monda Valley is what makes this home so special. It is perfect if you are looking for privacy, peace and quiet and is just a 10 minute drive to the village of Monda. 

The house comprises 5 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms in total and the distribution is as follows:

The ground floor is the main living area with 3 bedrooms (one is ensuite), 2 bathrooms, spacious and bright living-dining room with log burner and a lovely corner kitchen with amazing views. The living room also has access via patio doors onto the terrace.

The top floor is accessed via exterior stairs and comprises a very large roof terrace with incredible views plus 2 independent studio apartments, each with their own shower room.

The lower terrace boasts a lovely pergola with sitting area, private pool, barbecue, and garden with breathtaking views.
