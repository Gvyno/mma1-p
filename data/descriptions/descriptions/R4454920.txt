Spectacular villa with panoramic sea views in Mijas.

Experience the breathtaking beauty of this magnificent villa, offering panoramic views of the sea in Mijas. Nestled in a prime location, this detached villa provides convenient access to the motorway, just a short 20-minute drive from the airport, and a mere 25 minutes from Marbella. Moreover, the stunning beaches are within easy reach, with Fuengirola only 5 km away.
This villa seamlessly blends traditional Andalusian style on the outside with a modern and contemporary interior. As you step through the spacious entrance, you'll be greeted with direct access to the living room and sliding doors that open onto a terrace offering unparalleled sea views stretching along the coast, including the iconic Gibraltar. The large kitchen, equipped with top-of-the-line Boffi and Gaggenau appliances, boasts a skylight and provides access to another terrace and dining area. On this floor, you'll also discover a wine room and a gym. The master suite, complete with an ensuite bathroom featuring a Jacuzzi, a balcony, and direct garden access, is also found on this level.
The property features a generous garden, two outdoor kitchens, multiple terraces with panoramic vistas, and even a charming rustic guesthouse situated in front of the pool. The guesthouse includes two bedrooms, two bathrooms, and a delightful bar area with a barbecue, making it a perfect haven for guests and entertaining.
