1,677 m2 building located on a 5,128 m2 plot.

The building is made up of 3 independent houses, a hostel, a restaurant and garages.

Restaurant with a large lounge with large windows that give a panoramic view of the town and the Strait of Gibraltar. With capacity for 200 diners in the interior room, in the bar area where there is another room with capacity for 24 diners, at the entrance there is a cozy porch with capacity for 60 diners.

The hostel is fully operational, it has 8 rooms each with its own bathroom. At the entrance to the rooms there is a wide corridor with space for a rest area. Cleaning room and laundry.

Each house has independent access, with parking at the door.

House 2: 2 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, living-dining room, equipped kitchen, porch at the entrance.

House 3: 2 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, kitchen open to the living room and dining room, completely renovated.

Large garage area with capacity for 4 vehicles.
