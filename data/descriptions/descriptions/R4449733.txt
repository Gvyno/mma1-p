Stunning and luxurious detached villa in the peaceful and natural surroundings of Monte Mayor, a hillside estate of lavish villas protected by 24 hour security within the Benahavis municipality.   

This exquisite home was designed by the current owners and comprises of a sweeping driveway leading to a double garage and the main entrance doors of the property. The galleried landing on the upper level offers superb views to the sea across the triple height living area, together with access to 3 bedroom suites, including a lavish master suite with spacious bathroom and walk-in wardrobe. All three bedrooms enjoy access to the large terrace with stunning views of the sea and surrounding landscape.

On the lower level there is a large living room with areas set aside for dining and a snug/TV lounge. Also on this level is a modern, fully-fitted kitchen with breakfast dining area, plus a utility room. On the other side of this level is a further bedroom with en-suite bathroom, a guest toilet and an impressive cinema room.

The extensive terraces lead to the garden with large swimming pool, together with a sauna, shower room and storage area, which could be easily converted to a gym.

Features of the property include extensive use of travertine marble, air conditioning and under-floor heating throughout.
