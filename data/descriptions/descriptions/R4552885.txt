This beautiful three bedroom apartment is located in a very desirable area of the La Quinta Golf Resort, nestled on a beautiful hillside facing the golf valley to the east. This project of just 14 three-bedroom apartments and penthouses offers a high degree of privacy, which is hard to find among other developments in this region.
The apartment has a spacious living room that opens towards a spacious terrace. There is a master bedroom with adjoining bathroom and 2 bedrooms that share a second bathroom.

The apartment includes a storage room and a parking space in the underground garage with pre-installation for a connection to charge electric vehicles.
The property is in a completely secured area with a private access to ensure maximum safety and peace of mind for the residents. Within the complex there are several green areas and a beautiful swimming pool with sun terrace and toilets.
