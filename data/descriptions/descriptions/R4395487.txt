Welcome to Villa Vitelli, an exquisite Andalusian mansion spanning 838m2, nestled within a lush 5000m2 garden plot. This captivating villa has 8 spacious double bedrooms, 8 bathrooms, with 5 en-suites.

As you approach the grand entrance, the high roofline give you a palatial first impression. Step inside to a vaulted ceiling foyer leading to an extended lounge and nearby kitchen, complemented by an independent dining room. The wrap-around terrace connects these spaces, creating a perfect social setting. Enjoy picturesque golf and sea views from the terrace, along with beautifully landscaped gardens.

The ground floor also offers a study, cloakroom, and en-suite bedroom. Upstairs, discover four more bedrooms, including a lavish master suite with its en-suite bathroom and stunning garden and partial sea views.

Descending to the lower floor, find a games room, study, and three more en-suite bedrooms, each with natural light and private terraces. 

The 90m2 pool and mature gardens set on a large plot offer ample space for relaxation and entertainment.
