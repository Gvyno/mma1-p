This spacious 5 bedroom corner townhouse is located just a 5 minute drive to Max Beach and all amenities in Riviera del Sol, Mijas Costa. It consists of 5 floors:

MAIN FLOOR 
From the entrance of the house stairs down will lead you to the bright living room with open plan kitchen. This floor has access to a spacious terrace overlooking the communal gardens and swimming pool.

FIRST FLOOR
On the first floor you will find a bedroom, a bathroom and the large master bedroom with access to an ensuite bathroom and to a private terrace.

SECOND FLOOR
On the second floor you will find the third bedroom, also with an ensuite bathroom and access to a private terrace.

DOWNSTAIRS -1
If you take the stairs down from the entrance, you will first find a utility room with guest toilet. 

DOWNSTAIRS -2
Take the stairs down again and you will find a large area which the current owner converted into a 2 bedroom guest apartment with a small kitchenette and bathroom. This floor can have many purposes, from storage to a music or cinema room.

OUTSIDE
The house has terraces on every level and to the side of the house you will find a private garden with direct access to the communal swimming pool.

FACILITIES
The house comes with fibre optic internet, air conditioning (hot and cold), an alarm system and 2 private parking spaces behind the gate.
