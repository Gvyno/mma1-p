Bright penthouse with solarium and sea views located in the lower part of Calahonda, a stone's throw from La Luna beach. The apartment consists of a beautiful living room with fireplace with access to a large south-facing terrace with sea views, a semi-open kitchen, an bathroom with shower and window for the natural ventilation, a bedroom with a built-in wardrobe that also has direct access to the terrace. The entire apartment has marble floors, blinds on both patio windows and a barbecue on the terrace. In addition, there is a 52m2 solarium accessible from outside the apartment that can be used to build a small independent studio since the drain pipes are already there for it.
The Urbanization offers a large community pool and parking areas outside the building. You can walk to the beach and all the shops, bars and restaurants in the area. Also located next door is the bus stop that will take you to Marbella or Fuengirola, both of which are about 10 minutes away by car. Malaga international airport is only about 25mn away.
