Large 4-bedroom townhouse in Los Pacos, Fuengirola

On a quiet street in the popular residential area of Los Pacos, which belongs to the municipality of Fuengirola, this townhouse with four floors, 3 terraces and a large garage with space for two cars and plenty of storage room, perfect for a large family. 
At the front you are greeted by a large patio on two floors, perfect for enjoying with friends and family. On the entrance level there is a living room with dining room, kitchen, and hallway with guest toilet. From the kitchen you come out onto the second patio which also has a laundry room.
Upstairs there are 3 bedrooms with a bathroom and a guest toilet, and on the top floor is the master bedroom with an en-suite bathroom and a private terrace with morning sun. 
Downstairs is the large basement with garage and a lot of space for bicycles or motorcycles and more.
Nearby are schools, shops, cafes, and restaurants. The beach can be reached in just a 5-minute drive.
