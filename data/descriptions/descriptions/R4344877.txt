Lovely semi-detached house with very good dimensions, located a few metres from the Hotel Puente Romano and the beach. It consists of a total built area of 450 m2. The ground floor is distributed in entrance hall, guest toilet, large fully fitted and equipped kitchen, large living dining room with fireplace and access to a south facing porch. Nice swimming pool inside the community. The first floor consists of 2 bedrooms with terraces and 2 bathrooms en suite. On the second floor there is a large bedroom with bathroom en suite and solarium with beautiful views to the urbanization and La Concha de Marbella. In the basement there is a private garage as well as a large area for a gym, cinema room etc.
