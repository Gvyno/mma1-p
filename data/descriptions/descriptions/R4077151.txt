Discover the comfort in this 3 bedroom apartment located in the prestigious urbanization of Marbella Real, in the heart of Marbella's Golden Mile. This community offers exquisitely maintained tropical gardens, a children's playground, five communal swimming pools, gym, sauna and paddle tennis courts.

The apartment is within walking distance to some of Marbella's best beaches, as well as restaurants, stores and a wide variety of leisure amenities. The spacious living-dining room connects to a lovely terrace that overlooks the lush garden and pool. The kitchen is fully equipped, and the master bedroom not only has an en-suite bathroom but also access to a private terrace. The remaining two bedrooms share a bathroom.

The property features additional amenities such as fitted closets, window blinds, air conditioning hot/cold and a private subway parking space. This apartment in Marbella Real offers a perfect combination of modern amenities and a privileged location, guaranteeing a comfortable and luxurious living experience.
