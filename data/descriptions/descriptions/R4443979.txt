It's a perfect retreat, whether you're soaking in the hot tub, stargazing at night, or simply savouring the tranquil beauty of your surroundings. This floor also has an additional bedroom with an ensuite WC, which can easily be converted to a shower room.

This townhouse is more than just a residence; it's an authentic experience, a harmonious blend of modern living and traditional charm. Discover the comfort and tranquillity of Alhaurín El Grande living at its finest.
