This spacious and bright duplex apartment is located in an established and quiet urbanization in Las Lomas del Marbella Club on the Golden Mile.
The property is situated in a gated complex with 24-hour security service and beautiful communal gardens with three pools, squash court, sauna and meeting room.
The apartment comprises a large dining and living area with access to the terrace, 4 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, one of them en suite, open kitchen and a guest toilet. 
On the upper floor you have another terrace with nice views of the garden and mountain and towards the sea.
A garage space and storage room are included in the price.
