This apartment is located on the ground floor in the desirable Las Cañadas area of Las Lagunas de Mijas, conveniently close to El Corte Inglés. Boasting 115 square meters of living space, it comprises of four generously sized and luminous bedrooms. The bathroom has been fully renovated and features a bathtub. The kitchen is spacious and fully equipped, with direct access to a private and tranquil interior patio. The entrance room leads to a spacious living/dining area spanning over 45m2. This property is situated in an ideal location, surrounded by supermarkets and with quick access to the highway. The beach is a leisurely 14-minute walk away.
