Beautiful penthouse flat in the magnificent complex Puebla Agatha in La Reserva de Marbella with magnificent sea views.
The flat has two bedrooms, two bathrooms, fully fitted kitchen, spacious and bright living and dining room, south-west facing terrace. 
Sold furnished. Very well kept complex with two swimming pool areas, children's pool, barbecue area, communal gardens and surrounded by typical Andalusian squares.
This exclusive urbanization is located in the municipality of Marbella, one of the most privileged enclaves of the Costa del Sol and a reference point for world tourism. Its mild and temperate climate, its magnificent beaches and the golf courses that surround it are some of the reasons that place Marbella at the forefront of foreign investment in Spain.
Furthermore, this natural paradise also has one of the best marinas in Europe, as well as the most prestigious international schools and excellent motorway and dual carriageway communications, as it is only 12 km from Marbella and 30 minutes from Malaga International Airport.
