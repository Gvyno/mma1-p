This apartment is in a very enviable position on the main boulevard of La Cala de Mijas. It is on the 1st floor. It is spacious and distributed with the kitchen to your right and then the lounge and dining area looking over the plaza. The terrace which ran the length of the apartment before has been enclosed. The bathroom and the 3  bedrooms are along the corridor. There is a lift in the building and there is easy access for people with walking difficulties. Supermarkets, pharmacy and all other facilities are on your doorstep and the beach a literal stone's throw away. Public transport and great links to the other main centres of the Costa del Sol accessed by the A7.
