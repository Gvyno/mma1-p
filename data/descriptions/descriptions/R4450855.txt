Well-appointed 3-bedroom duplex penthouse situated near the New Golden Mile. 

Walking distance to La Resina golf and country club with a first-class restaurant and clubhouse. Just a five-minute drive to the beach with all the amenities ranging from bars, restaurants, and supermarkets and a new paddle tennis club opening late 2023/2024.

From the kitchen and lounge, you have access to a massive wrap-around terrace with stunning views, the guest bedroom has a further private terrace with the same stunning views. 

On the next level, you have the master suite with a full- sized bay window leading to another large terrace with panoramic views of the sea, mountains, and gardens. 

Two parking spaces completes the picture along with the communal pool. 

If you are looking for a large penthouse apartment with wonderful views and ample outside space this opportunity cannot be missed.
