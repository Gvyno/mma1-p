A stunning 4 bedroom detached villa offering privacy, flexible accommodation in a quiet location in Elvira Hills.

Sitting on a generous plot of just over 1000m2 this 340m2 villa needs to be seen to appreciate the charm. Upon entrance to the impressive hall staircase, the flow of the downstairs is perfect for seamless living and entertaining.

A generous living room, with vaulted ceiling, leads on to a terrace overlooking the pool and gardens.

The dining and kitchen bother offer terrace space.  The kitchen, once updated, will offer a generous open plan kitchen/ breakfast room. A bedroom suite, complete with dressing room, is also on the ground floor.

Upstairs opens up either side of the sweeping staircase offering 2 master suites both with vaulted ceilings.

From the house entrance, arriving in the lower ground floor offers a 4th bedroom suite along with a generous multi use room with natural light and air circulation. Completed with a sweeping drive and covered double parking.
