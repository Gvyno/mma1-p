Charming two-bedroom apartment with spectacular sea views, Sitio de Calahonda, Costa del Sol!

Beautiful two-bedroom and one bathroom apartment is located in the peaceful area of Sitio de Calahonda. The west facing balcony offers stunning sea views and you can enjoy sunshine until late in the evening. Living room has a cosy fireplace. The kitchen is good sized and practical. It is fully equipped and opens partly to the living room. Both bedrooms are reasonably sized as well. The apartment has a classical style and it is sold fully furnished. The community has a parking space and there is a shared storage room for scooters for example.

There is an outside parking area for the cars. The community of Mirador de Calahonda is very well kept. The houses are surrounded by green areas and there is a lovely garden around the swimming pool. The famous restaurant Dos Leones is located in the community area. For tennis and golf lovers there are La Siesta Golf and Club de Sol tennis club just around the corner. Padel fields and a sports club are also nearby. There are several restaurants, shops and all types of services within a couple of kilometres radius.
