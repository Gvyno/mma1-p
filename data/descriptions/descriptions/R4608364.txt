***Beautiful Boutique Community*** Villas Granada Golf is a beautiful boutique community of 7 homes with pretty communal gardens and pool area. Location is within the El Hacho area of Duquesa and is a short distance from local shops, marina and the beaches. This 3 bedroom, 2 bathroom home offers wonderful accommodation internally and externally. The entrance leads to a spacious living area with patio doors to the south east facing terrace area and next to the swimming pool. There is a fully equipped kitchen with a front aspect and a guest toilet. The marble stairs ascends to the upper floor with 3 double bedrooms with wardrobe storage and a family shower room. The master bedroom benefits from a private terrace and a full en-suite bathroom. This is a wonderful home from home in a pretty commmunity and a must see.
