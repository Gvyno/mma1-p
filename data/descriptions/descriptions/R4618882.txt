Stunning penthouse apartment with large private solarium perfect for relaxing in the sun! 

As you enter the property, you find a spacious lounge, a kitchen with a pass-through arch window overlooking the quaint terrace facing the gorgeous landscaped gardens, golf course and swimming pool areas. The terrace has a spiral staircase leading up to the vast private solarium. 

There is a hall leading to the bedrooms; both are very spacious, with fitted wardrobes and the master bedroom has a beautiful ensuite bathroom. The bedrooms share a second terrace with excellent views and large windows that allow a bright and airy feel. 

Located on the third floor, the property has fantastic views to the golf course as well as the tropical landscaped gardens and pool areas. There are hot and cold air-conditioning units throughout the property. 

The complex has several swimming pools, communal underground parking plus there is also ample street parking.
