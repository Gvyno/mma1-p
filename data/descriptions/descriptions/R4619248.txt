Nueva Andalucía is a stunning area located between Puerto Banús and the Golf Valley in Marbella, Spain. It is a popular destination for tourists and locals alike, offering a wide range of activities and amenities. One of the main attractions of Nueva Andalucía is its four top golf courses, which attract golf enthusiasts from all over the world. In addition to golf, the area also boasts paddle and tennis clubs, perfect for those who enjoy racquet sports. For families, there are several international schools in the area, providing excellent education for children of all ages. Nueva Andalucía is also home to some of the best hotels, restaurants, and bars in Marbella. Visitors can enjoy a variety of cuisines, from traditional Spanish tapas to international fusion dishes. There are also several markets in the area, selling everything from fresh produce to handmade crafts. For those who enjoy a bit of nightlife, the Marbella Casino is located in Nueva Andalucía, offering a range of gaming options and entertainment. In terms of housing, it offers a diverse range of options, from luxury apartments to rustic and contemporary villas. The area is known for its gated communities, offering a high level of security and privacy. Overall, Nueva Andalucía is a fantastic destination for those looking for a luxurious and active lifestyle.
