Great townhouse within walking distance to all amenities, the centre of La Cala de Mijas and the beach.

The property is divided into several levels, with the following distribution: main floor offers a spacious renovated open concept kitchen. Separate utility room. Living-dining room with exit to large west facing terrace. Guest toilet. This level is the heart of the home, very bright and cosy.

On the first floor are the three bedrooms. The master bedroom is ensuite, with the two secondary bedrooms sharing a full bathroom. Upstairs is the solarium, with beautiful sea views as well as space for a barbecue. The large basement, with space for several vehicles, is currently used as an office, storage area and gym.

The quiet and international community has a swimming pool and communal areas for the enjoyment of the owners.
