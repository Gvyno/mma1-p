Set in the residential area of Buenavista, this modern villa offers fantastic sea views. The contemporary design includes a double-height ceiling in the living area, creating a wonderful light and open space. The living area with a modern, fully fitted kitchen, complete with island, leads to the  spacious terrace area with saltwater pool.  There are a total of three bedrooms, all with en-suite bathrooms. One of the bedroom is on the ground floor, the main bedroom with walk-in wardrobe and en-suite bathroom as well as another guest bedroom are on the upper floor. There is a large basement area, which can be customised to include another living area, bedroom, gym or cinema room. Buenavista is a beautiful residential community with easy access to the coastal highway, just 20 minutes from Marbella and Malaga airport, and just a short drive to the beaches of Benalmadena and Fuengirola.

Completion expected quarter 3/2025
