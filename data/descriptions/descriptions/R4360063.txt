Welcome to this stunning 4-bedroom Andalusian-style villa, nestled in the tranquil location of Valle Romano just west of Estepona. Built in 2008, this exquisite property offers a blend of elegance, comfort, and breathtaking views of the surrounding golf course and sparkling sea.

Situated in a lovely upcoming international neighborhood, surrounded by modern newly built villas, this villa offers a peaceful retreat while still being within a short drive to Estepona center and all amenities. Its convenient location provides easy access to the vibrant city life, yet ensures privacy and serenity for a perfect balance.

The villa boasts a well-planned distribution, featuring a spacious open kitchen/living area that creates an inviting ambiance for gatherings and relaxation. The living area extends seamlessly to a large covered terrace, equipped with retractable glass windows, allowing you to enjoy the beautiful surroundings all year round.

For those seeking panoramic views, the large rooftop terrace is a true highlight, offering a breathtaking vantage point to admire the mesmerizing vistas of the golf course and sea.

The basement of the villa hosts a guest apartment complete with a kitchen and laundry room, providing privacy and comfort for visiting guests or extended family members. Additionally, the property offers a private garage with a large storage room, ensuring ample space for your belongings. The gated driveway can accommodate 2 to 3 cars, providing convenience and security.

To complete the luxurious experience, a private pool awaits you in the charming mature garden, offering a serene oasis where you can unwind and soak up the sunshine.

This Andalusian-style villa captures the essence of Mediterranean living, combining elegance, tranquility, and stunning views in an ideal location.
