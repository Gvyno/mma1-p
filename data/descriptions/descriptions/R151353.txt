Detached villa near cumbres del Rodeo in Nueva Andalucia, only a short drive to Puerto Banus and the town of San Pedro.
The property has had some renovation and offers 3 bedrooms on the ground floor, one of which has an ensuite bathroom, fully equipped kitchen and a comfortable living room with large covered terrace overlooking the garden & pool, ideal for alfresco dining.
 The garden is easily maintained with a heated saltwater swimming pool, and there is a large driveway leading to a double garage.
