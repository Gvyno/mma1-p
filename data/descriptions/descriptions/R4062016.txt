Bright spacious apartment located next to the new Hotel El Encinar de Sotogrande. The apartment offers a fitted kitchen, a large living and dining area with an open fireplace, three really large bedrooms with fitted wardrobes. The master bedroom has its own bathroom ensuite and the two other bedrooms have a bathroom to share. From the living and dining area, glass doors are leading to the spacious balcony with an east orientation from where you enjoy a beautiful view over the green gardens and the Mediterranean sea. The apartment offers several built-in wardrobes.

The urbanization "Tenis" is one of the first projects lifted by the founder of Sotogrande.
