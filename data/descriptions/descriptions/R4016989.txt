The property is arranged over four levels with extensive entertaining spaces, both inside and outside.  The kitchen is furnished with the best quality furniture and high-end Miele and Siemens appliances.

The oversize master bedroom suite has an elevated bed, private lounge, a huge walk in closet and a large ensuite bathroom with feature bathtub, walk-in shower and double basins.

There is an impressive exterior pool with a separate children's' pool and extensive terraces.  There is also a solarium with jacuzzi.

There are six bedroom suites in total, including the master suite.

Highlights include a private gymnasium, cinema room, a games room with a bar and pool table, and underground garage for two or three cars.

There is a private elevator to every level and a state of the art security system throughout.
