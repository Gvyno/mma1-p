Duplex Penthouse with Sea Views! ‼

Excellent penthouse with 2 bedrooms and 2 complete bathrooms, featuring multiple terraces, integrated kitchen with the living room. The living room is equipped with LED lighting for special evenings. Located in the Paraiso area, just 100 meters from the highway, providing easy access to both Puerto Banus (8 min) and Estepona (15 min). Close to shops, supermarkets, bars, restaurants, tennis clubs, and within walking distance to the beach.
