A one of a kind villa in construction surrounded by the breathtaking sea views

Introducing an exquisite example of eco-friendly luxury living. Located amidst lush greenery and boasting a stunning contemporary design, this sustainable villa redefines elegance while championing environmental responsibility. Every aspect of this property has been thoughtfully crafted to minimize its ecological footprint without compromising on design or style.

The heart of the villa is undoubtedly its expansive living area, boasting floor-to-ceiling glass walls that provide uninterrupted panoramic views of the coastline, the African coastline, Gibraltar and La Reserva golf course. 

Nestled in the hills, this villa is an architectural marvel that seamlessly blends into its natural surroundings. Constructed using durable, renewable materials, such as CLT wood with similar strength to concrete and earthquake durable, the property  reduces environmental impact but also infuses the home with a warm, rustic charm. The urbanisation is secure as gated and manned 24/7. The property is located on a quiet cul de sac and is a short drive to the Sotogrande school, close to famous golf clubs such as La Reserva and Valderrama. Also, Sotogrande port and the surrounding beaches is a short 10 minute drive away.

As you step inside Villa Verde, you're greeted by an abundance of natural light, thanks to vast floor to ceiling energy-efficient windows and strategically placed skylight wndows that invite the outdoors in. The open-concept layout creates a seamless flow between the living spaces, allowing for a sense of spaciousness and connectivity with nature.

Villa Verde's commitment to sustainability extends to its intelligent design features. Natural ventilation systems, including strategically placed windows and adjustable louvers, allow for passive cooling, reducing the need for air conditioning. The villa's insulation is made from recycled materials, ensuring optimal temperature regulation year-round, while energy-efficient appliances and fixtures further reduce energy consumption.

Stepping outside onto the villa's expansive terrace, you are greeted by a dazzling oasis of relaxation. A large infinity pool beckons you to take a refreshing dip while gazing out at the glistening sea stretching as far as the eye can see. Surrounding the pool, plush gardens inviting you to bask in the warm sunshine or curl up with a good book under the shade of a parasol.
