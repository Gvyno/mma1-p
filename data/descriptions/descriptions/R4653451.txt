Dining area with a large table for 8 people, lounge area with a small bar and direct access to the terrace and the swimming pool. There is an outside bathroom, barbecue area and plenty of space to put sunbeds. On that same floor there is 1 bedroom with a bathroom and a toilet.

On the upper floor there are 4 perfectly distributed bedrooms with their bathrooms and dressing rooms.

There is parking for 2 cars.
