Country house located on the outskirts of Alhaurin el Grande on a 5,800m2 plot planted mainly with citrus trees. With water from the town hall and the canal, completely fenced, outdoor parking space, warehouse, and a private pool. The house needs to be partially renovated and currently consists of a spacious covered porch from where you access the house which consists of a living room with fireplace, kitchen, two bedrooms and two bathrooms. An exceptional location with good access and only 10 minutes driving to town.
