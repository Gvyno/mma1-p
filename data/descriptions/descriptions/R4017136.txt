The project consists of 20 apartments distributed into 5 buildings on a 8.000 m2 plot of prime land. All blocks hold 4 apartments, each groundfloor having a private garden overlooking the elaborate garden landscape.
The 6.000 m2 communal area features a large pool, sun deck and designer gardens, making for idyllic surroundings in the
most pleasant of environments.
Epic Marbella symbolizes the utmost quality standard in fusion with functional and creative high-end luxury residential living.
Floor plans are conceived with an emphasis on spacious open areas,
maximum floor to ceiling height level, as well as a premium on large
format windows making for plentiful natural light throughout.
Basements will have from 106 to 154 m2 allowing to comfortably have up to 4 bedrooms in total as well as the possibility of adding a home cinema, gym, entertainment or second kitchen.
One of the various amenities that EPIC buyers will enjoy is the installation of a fully equipped wellness SPA, gymnasium offering the latest equipment, heated indoor pool, clubhouse/meeting area for events/celebrations, etc. All of the above mentioned facilities will be implemented inside Epic grounds and naturally will be for exclusive use by residents only.
