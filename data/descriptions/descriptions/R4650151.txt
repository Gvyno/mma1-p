Amazing townhouse located in Aloha Lake in Nueva Andalucia.
 Distributed on two levels, the ground floor has a cozy living room with fireplace and a large exit to the garden ideal for enjoying the outdoors and garden views, an open kitchen and a guest toilet. On the first floor there are three bedrooms, the master bedroom with en-suite bathroom and the other two with a common bathroom offering comfort and privacy. A large garage with an extra space converted into a music studio. The urbanisation itself offers beautiful communal gardens and a swimming pool to enjoy on sunny days, plus the location in Nueva Andalucia is excellent, with easy access to golf courses, restaurants, shops and the beach.
