- LOVELY VILLA IN SIERRA BLANCA -

Classic style 4 bedroom villa modernly decorated with high quality furniture. It has a wonderful hall that leads to a spacious living room surrounded by large windows and where the dining room is located, the kitchen open to the fully equipped living room and with independent access to the terrace.
