Nice finca of over 18,000 m2 with a house of 97m2 on top.
This nice house has a spacious living room with fireplace and on the side a closed Andalusian kitchen.
In the living room there is a staircase to the upper floor with two bedrooms, the master bedroom has fitted wardrobes and air conditioning. There is also a bathroom with shower.
At the front is a very large covered terrace with BBQ.
At the back outside there is also a large barn, with a laundry room with toilet, which can be converted to extra accommodation.
On the plot is a large chicken run and a carport for 2 cars.

There are already several kinds of fruit trees placed and another piece of land is prepared to place 300 mango trees on it.
The land is irrigated and there is a water storage of 30,000 litres.
The access is good and the last part needs some improvement.
The whole house needs some modernisation.
