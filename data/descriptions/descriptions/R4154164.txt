Traditional house in Tolox with the Sierra de las Nieves behind.

The property is divided into three floors, on the second floor we find living room, living room, kitchen and a small toilet, on the second floor, two bedrooms, one double, one single and 1 bathroom with bathtub.
The top floor has another double bedroom and a large terrace with laundry. Bright and with beautiful mountain views.

This property offers many possibilities as it is very well located, overlooking the village of Tolox, with great places to walk, its spa and the Torrecilla peak.
