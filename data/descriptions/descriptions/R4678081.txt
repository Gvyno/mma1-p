The roof solarium enjoys plenty of outdoor space and wonderful sea views! The property consists of an open-plan, kitchen and living area, leading out to the terrace and roof solarium. Puerto Banús is a short 5 minutes drive away. This fabulous townhouse is perfect for a holiday home, permanent residence or rental property.
