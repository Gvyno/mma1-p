***Sea Views & Walking Distance to the Beach*** A wonderfully spacious 3 bedroom duplex property in the lovely, established urbanisation of Mirador del Mar on the Casares Costa. The property opens to a light & airy hallway leading to an open plan living area and patio doors to the large terrace with views of the coast. On this floor, there is a double bedroom with ample storage, a large shower room and a lovely galley kitchen with a utility area. In the hallway by the marble stairs is a good sized storage room. On the upper floor, there are a further 2 bedrooms and 2 full size bathrooms. The master bedroom has a 10 M2 terrace area with spectacular coastal views. The property is in good order throughout but needs some modernisation and viewing is highly recommended.
