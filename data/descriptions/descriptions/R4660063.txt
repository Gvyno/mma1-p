Ideally located 7 bedroom villa in La Cala, boasting sea and golf views. The property built in 1982 is only a few minutes' walk to all amenities that La Cala village has to offer and the beach. Located on the ground floor is the entrance hall, large fully fitted kitchen with breakfast bar and utility room and bright and spacious lounge diner with feature fireplace. Patio windows to the side of the lounge / diner leading to a large south east facing terrace. There are three good sized bedrooms all with fitted wardrobes. The master bedroom has en-suite bathroom and there is also a guest bathroom. The lower level has a further four bedrooms and two bathrooms, large living area, small kitchen and utility room. Access to the lower terraces, lawned gardens and pool. There is a covered BBQ area and shaded pool lounge areas. The property has air-conditioning throughout and garage.
