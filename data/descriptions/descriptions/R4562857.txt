Casares del Mar is located on the coast of Casares, just by the beach. The location gives the properties proximity with a private access to the beach and spectacular first line sea views.  
This first floor newly renovated apartment keeps a very high standard has a lot of extras such as; Glass curtains on the terrace. A smart-home system for both the new individual air condition "cold / warm" and a new water boiler. 

The apartment is sold fully furnished. 
The living room and dining area has large sliding glass-doors that let a lot of light into the room as well as they lead to the large private terrace. 
There is a fully equipped very bright kitchen with all appliances included. 
The apartment offers 2-bedrooms with fitted wardrobes and 2 bathrooms of which master bedroom has direct access to the terrace.
Master bathroom is on-suite and equipped with both a bathtub with hydro massage and a shower. 
The apartment has an underground garage space and a storage room. There is also possible to purchase another parking spot. 
The urbanization is a gated community and has recently been reformed, in both the interiors and the shared areas. There is a lovely garden and 2 large outdoor swimming pools. 
The location is exceptional, between Marbella and Sotogrande. Here you can take advantage of the tranquillity of the area & beach and quickly access places with all amenities, shopping, bars and restaurants. 
There are less than 5 minutes' drive to several golf courses (Casares, Finca Cortesin, Dona Julia, La Duquesa and Estepona golf)
There are 4 min drive to Duquesa Port and less than10 minutes to the centre of Estepona. 
Perfect as a permanent home, a holiday-home or for rentals.
