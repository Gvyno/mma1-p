Nestled deep within the countryside this is a perfect spot to embrace nature's tranquility.  This property feels like a country retreat offering a genuine escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.  Located off the grid, this idyllic haven is a sanctuary for those seeking solace and connection.

Property Details:

Tucked away in the heart of the mountains this hidden gem is accessed by both concrete road and track meandering down to the property giving you a sense of seclusion and adventure.  Here you can embrace the simplicity of off-grid living, the property is powered by solar panels.  Water is currently provided from the local hamlet of Jorox.  Town Water has already been connected to the end of the plot and should be connected with the next year.

A welcoming terrace sits to the side of the property this is one of the most lovely spots to sit and relax and enjoy the tranquility of the land... to be honest you are pretty much spoilt for choice here!

Entering into the kitchen to the left is the spacious living room with french doors framing those lovely views.  The main house has three bedrooms and a family bathroom, the master bedroom also has the added luxury of an ensuite bathroom.  The family bathroom is located on the ground floor as is what could be a utility room, this is currently utilised as a ´tool room´.

On the lower floor will find the fourth bedroom with bathroom.

Whether you're an outdoor enthusiast or simply seeking peace and reflection, this property offers a range of activities to suit your desires. Hiking trails wind through the forest, leading to hidden waterfalls and breathtaking viewpoints. Birdwatching, photography, and meditation are popular pastimes in this tranquil setting.
