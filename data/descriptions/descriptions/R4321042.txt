Nestled amidst sprawling olive groves, in the countryside of beautiful Casarabonela.

Property Details:

Relaxing on the terrace of this finca is a lovely spot to enjoy the lifestyle this property provides.   The fully fitted kitchen leads from this terrace, a seating area that people gravitate towards and a perfect spot to entertain.  The arched wall in the lounge is a lovely touch with fireplace (pellet burner) to provide the winter heat.  There are three double bedrooms and a family bathroom.  The master bedrooms has an ensuite bathroom and patio doors leading to the pool area which enjoys beautiful vistas of the mountains and countryside. There is also bar outside that has both water and electricity connections. 

To the left of the property is a 40m2 self contained casita, the owners constructed this for friends and family but it could also be a rental property. The casita includes a shower room for guests to enjoy. 

On the small hill to the side of the property you will find a very large storage room that could also be used as a worksop. The owners previous had a couple of Shetland Ponies and their paddock is also located here. There are two water deposits here.

The property is connected to town water.
