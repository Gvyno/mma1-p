Completely remodeled and brand-new townhouse situated in a central yet serene location in Las Lagunas, with convenient amenities just steps away and close proximity to the Fuengirola beach.

Spanning across four floors, this residence offers ample space. The entry level features an open-plan kitchen, a generously sized dining and living area with a private patio, and a guest bathroom with a shower. A staircase leads to the basement, a sizable open area suitable for various purposes such as a games room, TV room, children's play area, or even a home gym.
On the first floor, three spacious double bedrooms share a bathroom with a shower. 
The top floor houses a fourth bedroom with an integrated bathroom and access to a spacious south-west facing terrace.
