Penthouse with incredible views of the Mediterranean, located in one of the most popular urbanizations in the lower Riviera del Sol.
The apartment consists of 3 substantial bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, one of which is en-suite. Kitchen with an open solution to the dining area and the living room. From the living room you come out onto the lovely terrace with a fantastic view of the wire farm and the Mediterranean Sea. The terrace has plenty of space for both dinners and sunbathing on hot days. The terrace has also been equipped with glass curtains that protect against wind on windy days.

The area has a large, beautiful garden with a pool, tennis court and barbecue area, as well as plenty of parking for the residents.
Everything you could possibly need within walking distance, shops, restaurants, gym, paddle courses and of course the beach.
