A fabulous duplex penthouse/elevated townhouse located in a beachfront urbanisation.  The perfect setting to enjoy the Mediterranean lifestyle. The property boasts a large patio, ideal for dining al fresco, with an outdoor kitchen area and ample space to entertain.  

You access the house via a flight of stairs.  As you walk in through the main door you come to the living dining area,  The living/ dining room is split level with a fabulous wood burning stove.  Walk straight out the glass balcony doors to the large patio.  From here there are views of the gardens and communal pool.  On this level you also have a fully fitted kitchen with a small breakfast bar and a cloakroom.  

The second floor is home to three good-sized bedrooms and two bathrooms.  The main bedroom is ensuite.  There are two terraces.  Walk out of the main bedroom and from here , you have your private terrace with fabulous garden, pool and sea views.

The beach here is spectacular—roll out of bed and literally walk along a path down to a gate that leads directly to a promenade and one of the most beautiful beaches in Manilva.   Walk along the promenade to the neighboring village of El Castillo, renowned for its fish restaurants, and onto Duquesa Marina, with numerous restaurants and cafés.  

The urbanisation itself has lush gardens, communal pools, padel tennis and a tennis court and the propertty comes with allocated parking in the communal zone.   Very close by is a &#039;Manilva Beach&#039; beach bar where you can order some delicious fresh fish and enjoy a Tinto de Verano.
