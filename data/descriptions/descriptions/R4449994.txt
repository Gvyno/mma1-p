This very well presented bright first floor corner unit located in the popular gated development of Cumbres Del Rodeo in Nueva Andalucia offers stunning mountain and sea views  as well as towards Gibraltar and within a short distance of  amenities including the beach, variety of golf courses and Puerto Banus. Accommodation comprises of spacious lounge & dining area which leads out onto both the covered and uncovered terrace with glass curtains which is idea for all-year-round living. There's a fully equipped kitchen with separated utility room, guest toilet, three bedrooms one of which is ensuite and access to the terrace and two good sized guest bedrooms sharing a family bathroom with walk-in shower. Further features include under-floor heating in the bathrooms which were renovated two years ago an underground parking space with lift access, communal swimming pool and well-maintained gardens.  A perfect all year round home as well as a for holiday living.
