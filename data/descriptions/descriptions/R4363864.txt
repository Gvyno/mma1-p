Discover elegance and comfort in this exceptional flat, fully furnished and meticulously equipped to meet your every need. Each bedroom and the living room are air-conditioned to ensure your comfort at all times.

Located in the secure Señorio Aloha complex, which offers 24 hour security. Situated at the entrance of the prestigious Golf Valley, this property is ideal for both living and rental purposes. A short stroll takes you to the famous Puerto Banús Marina, home to luxurious yachts, a selection of high-end restaurants, exotic bars and cutting-edge fashion boutiques.

From the private terrace you can enjoy magnificent views of the sea, the mountains and much of Puerto Banús. The complex's beautifully landscaped gardens provide a serene and verdant setting. The development also boasts three refreshing swimming pools, two of which are specially designed for children.

This exclusive flat is situated in an enviable location, right next to the iconic Puerto Banús bullring. Just a minute's walk away, you can immerse yourself in the hustle and bustle of the popular Saturday morning street market. Less than five minutes' walk away, the vibrant heart of cosmopolitan Puerto Banús awaits, known for its outstanding beach facilities and a wide range of water activities such as jet ski hire. Just 5 km away, you'll find the charm of Marbella, with its exclusive shops, shopping streets and the famous Picasso Park, which is worth a visit for both shopping opportunities and simple curiosity.
