This superb elevated ground floor apartment is located in Los Pinos Los Arqueros and enjoys really good views of the Mediterranean sea. The property has two bedrooms one with an ensuite, a separate fitted kitchen with a utility room and a spacious and bright lounge and dining area. The property has a lovely covered terrace with some tropical plants and overlooks the communal gardens. Included in the price is an underground parking space and storage room. 

Los Pinos is one of the original complexes within Los Arqueros and has seventy two apartments and penthouses and two communal pools one of which is heated for year round use.

Los Arqueros is a wonderful golf resort in Benahavis with restaurants and other sports facilities within the resort and a 24 hour security service. Located just a ten minute drive from San Pedro de Alcantara and Puerto Banus and just a few minutes from La Quinta golf.
