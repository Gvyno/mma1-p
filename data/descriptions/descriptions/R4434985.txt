A beautiful family villa in the heart of Benamara.  A light and bright, sunny detached villa located on the edge of the parkland of Benamara.  The garden has direct access to the stunning communal gardens and pool and is south facing so enjoys all day sunshine.

The property has a large living and dining room with a fireplace for the cooler, winter months and a newly refurbished, open-plan kitchen.   There is also a separate study area.   The lounge leads to a wrap around terrace and private garden on two sides.  Upstairs there are 3 good sized bedrooms, all with fitted wardrobes.  The master bedroom has an ensuite bathroom and a large terrace with panoramic, south facing views down to the sea.

The villa is situated on a quiet, residential street and has a car port for 2 cars.

It really is a beautiful villa, ideal for full time living or for those who want a larger holiday home.  Viewing highly recommended.
