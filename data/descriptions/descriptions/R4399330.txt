Nestled within a charming complex in the area of La Quinta, this penthouse boasts a sleek, contemporary design seamlessly integrated into its natural surrounding, and with sea views!

This penthouse boasts three bedrooms, each with an en-suite bathroom, and its south-southwest orientation ensures an abundance of natural light streaming through thoughtfully designed, oversized windows. Additionally, this penthouse features an expansive terrace-solarium with sweeping panoramic views, complemented by an extra charming private garden area.

The kitchen is equipped with top-of-the-line appliances, ensuring culinary enthusiasts have everything they need at their fingertips. Convenience abounds with amenities such as a laundry room, central air conditioning, underfloor heating in the bathrooms, two parking spaces, and a storage room.

The communal spaces are a testament to sustainable landscaping, with native plants that minimize water consumption. Residents can unwind by the communal swimming pool, surrounded by a sun-soaked terrace. For those dedicated to maintaining their fitness regimen, there's a gym, SPA, and indoor pool, all valuable additions to the complex. Security and peace of mind are guaranteed with gated access to this modern community.
