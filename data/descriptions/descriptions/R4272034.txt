Ideal apartment for investors in Sabinillas!

This 1-bedroom apartment is ideally located, behind the Repsol gas station. The kitchen is fully equipped with appliances and laundry room. The bathroom is a family bathroom complete with bathtub.

The property has marble floors and an electric water heater and is ideal if you want to live next to the sea and in a town with all kinds of services.

The urbanization is located in the town, on one of the most beautiful beaches on the Costa del Sol. It also has a community pool and a large green area. The Sabinillas promenade goes from Buenas Noches to La Chullera, making it ideal for long walks.

It is just a few minutes walk from the town's sports complex with multiple tennis courts, paddle tennis courts, a sports pool, a sauna and spa area, as well as a number of different sports and aerobics classes for children and adults. Given its central location, there are several restaurants and bars nearby, and a large Lidl store is located just across the street.
