PROPERTY OF THE MONTH ...TOWNHOUSE NEAR MIJAS PUEBLO ...
Situated in the very popular urbanisation of Mijas La Nueva is this south facing town house with views to the green and leafy gardens and surrounding countryside,
The property has been thoroughly modernised and has air-conditioning throughout. The urbanisation has beautifully well-kept gardens, 2 swimming pools and tennis courts. 
Water is included in the community fees.
The pretty village of  Mijas Pueblo is a 20 minute walk away and a local bus service is close by.THIS PROPERTY HAS TO BE VIEWED to fully appreciate the location and wonderful community it offers.
