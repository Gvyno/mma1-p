Located on a good size plot, the property is distributed over 3 levels and features a private lift. 
On the ground level you will find a huge garage for 7 cars, living room, laundry area, 2 bedrooms upstairs and a spa area with jacuzzi and hydromassage shower. Inside the spa there is also a bedroom, 2 dressing rooms and a bathroom. 
The first floor consists of a spacious living-dining room with access to a large covered porch with sitting area facing out to the pool, fully-equipped separate kitchen with exit to the outside and a bathroom. 
The second level features 3 bedrooms and a complete separate bathroom.
The third level is occupied by a large master bedroom with a private bathroom, dressing room and access to the uncovered terrace.
Outside you have a fabulous garden with saltwater swimming pool, yard and barbecue area. 
The property boasts a prime location within only 5 minutes' walk to the beach and is surrounded by all kinds of services and amenities. Torrequebrada Golf is close by.
Perfect holiday or permanent home.
