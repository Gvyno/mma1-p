West facing two bedroom townhouse set on the stunning urbanisation of Zahara de Istán, overlooking the Istán lake. Less than a ten-minute drive from Marbella, Puerto Banús and every amenity such as commercial and sports centres, restaurants, schools and more. 

From the complex there are magnificent panoramic views to the wooded valley and the Mediterranean Sea. The community offers immaculate lush gardens, a club house and a refreshing swimming pool.

Distributed over two floors, the property comprises, on the ground floor of: entrance hall, living room with fireplace, split level dining room, fully fitted open plan kitchen with breakfast seating and access to a tiled patio with a built in barbecue, one bathroom. 

On the upper floor, there is the master bedroom with en suite bathroom with hydro massage bath and private balcony with breathtaking views towards the lake, second bedroom with private balcony and one bathroom. It offers the possibility to build a basement and a third bedroom on the first floor.
