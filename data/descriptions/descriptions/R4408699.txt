Nestled in the picturesque river area of of Pizarra, this very rural finca offers a tranquil escape amidst nature's beauty. Situated on a sprawling hilly plot spanning an 5868 square meters, this property presents an opportunity for those seeking a very peaceful lifestyle.. With its idyllic setting a haven for nature lovers. Approached on a long 20 minute driving on a track through a river bed. 

This finca has a living area with the kitchen in need of fitting, two bedrooms and a bathroom. One of the highlights of this property is the presence of two wells, providing a reliable source of water. There is also a large deposit tank to store water.

While the property currently has an unfinished pool, it presents an exciting opportunity for the new owner to design and create their  swimming oasis surrounded by the breathtaking views of the hills and the untouched natural landscape.

For those who appreciate sustainable living, this finca offers the convenience of an electricity connection. Please note the access is a river bed track. Not near schools or transport or amenities. The land is mostly hilly.
