This charming villa is presented as an unrivaled jewel in an exclusive urbanization of semi-detached villas. Access is guaranteed by a separate electric gate and private security, providing residents with an oasis of tranquility and privacy. The kitchen, equipped with high-end Siemens appliances and elegant granite countertops, is a culinary lover's delight.
The generous living areas open onto a covered terrace and private garden, harmoniously connecting with the exceptional common areas. These include subtropical gardens, a cozy picnic area with pergola, and two impressive swimming pools: one outdoor with a lifeguard during the summer, and a heated saltwater pool to enjoy all year round, both surrounded by sun loungers and parasols.
Light floods the four bedrooms, each with built-in wardrobes and double-glazed windows. The hot/cold central air conditioning, the fireplace in the living room and the marble floors throughout the house offer comfort in all seasons. The master bedroom has a luxurious en-suite bathroom and a large 40-meter terrace with stunning mountain and sea views.
Security is a priority, with a blind main door, an alarm connected to the central station and bars on the windows. Additionally, privacy is guaranteed with a lockbox enabled. With two bathrooms and a toilet, comfort is combined with elegance in every corner of this property.
Located in the peaceful area of ​​Riviera/Calahonda, just 20 minutes from Malaga international airport and equidistant between Fuengirola and Marbella, this villa is just a few minutes from the best beaches on the coast. The proximity to shopping centers, schools, pharmacies and a variety of cafes and restaurants, as well as the popular Diana Park, highlights the convenience of this enclave.
For sports lovers, the offer is plentiful with tennis clubs, paddle tennis, bowling and renowned golf courses within walking distance. In short, this property is not only an exceptional residence, but also an invitation to live the Mediterranean lifestyle at its finest.
