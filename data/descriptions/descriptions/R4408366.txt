Fantastic four bedroom & three bathroom duplex penthouse located in Bahía del Velerín has been fully refurbished. All rooms have been carefully furnished and feature many luxurious details such as fitted wardrobes, walk- in showers, a freestanding bathtub, a Bang & Olufsen sound system and a modern fireplace. The impressive roof terrace offers you unobstructed sea views over the Mediterranean and is equipped with a dining, wellness and chill out area. The property is in walking distance to several good restaurants as well as the new Laguna Village and has direct access to the newly built coastal path and the sea. The community has a fantastic pool area with a snack bar, tropical gardens and 24/7 security. The property comes with an underground parking and a storage room.
