Sunny duplex penthouse with three bedrooms and three bathrooms located in a private urbanization with swimming pool and communal garden areas in Nueva Andalucía, a few minutes by car to the beach, very close to the Plaza Shopping Center and Puerto Banús. This property is located on the attic floor of a Mediterranean-style building with only two floors that does not have an elevator. The house is distributed over two floors, on the ground floor we find a large hall, an independent equipped kitchen with a separate laundry room, a living room with a dining area through which you can access the large main terrace, 40 m² with views of the sea and the mountains, as well as to the pool; a bright exterior bedroom and a separate bathroom. On the second floor we find two other bedrooms, the main one en suite with a dressing area and terrace. Given its large surface area, it has many possibilities to expand terraces, rooms, etc. to adapt it to the client's needs. Marble floors, central air conditioning.
