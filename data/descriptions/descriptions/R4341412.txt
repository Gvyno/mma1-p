Splendid 5-bedroom villa situated a mere 5-minute drive from Pedregalejo Beach. Set on an extensive 800 m2 plot, this property is distributed over 4 levels, offering an impressive and spacious layout. 

The basement accommodates a wine cellar, a dressing room, and a storage area. On the ground floor, you will find a spacious living room, a cozy seating area, a separate kitchen with adjacent laundry room, a guest/office room and a bathroom with shower. Both the living area and the kitchen offer direct access to the porch, garden and the inviting swimming pool with jacuzzi. The outdoor space also boasts a delightful small orchard, a built-in barbecue, a traditional clay oven and a charming chill-out area.

Ascending to the first floor, you'll discover 4 bedrooms with sea views and 3 bathrooms, including one en suite. The master bedroom connects to a terrace, while two other bedrooms open out to balconies. Finally, the attic is occupied by a bedroom with its own private terrace, providing breathtaking sea views.

Situated in an established neighborhood, this villa enjoys proximity to all essential services and amenities, with convenient access to the Mediterranean motorway. The beach is a mere 5-minute drive away, the historic centre is within 10 minutes' drive and Malaga international airport can be reached in 15 minutes by car.
