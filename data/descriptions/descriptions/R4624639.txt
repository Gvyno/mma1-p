Beautiful luxury ground floor apartament in Casares Costa, in gated Albatros urbanization. 

It has 152m2 and 56m2 of terrace with private garden and sea views. It consists of 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, one ensuite and one with shower, fully fitted kitchen with utility room, living-dining room very spacious and with lots of light thanks to the large windows. Living room is connected to the terrace and private garden. It has sea and mountain views. The terrace has a lot of options because of its big size. 
The whole flat is equipped with central air conditioning. There is one parking place and storage included in the price. 
Albatross is a gated urbanization and offers a privileged location in Casares Costa. The beach is only 2 min away by car, famous golf course Finca Cortesin is 2 min away, Sabinillas is 5 min away, offering a great variety of services, shops and restaurants. Estepona is 10min, Marbella 30 min, Sotogrande 15min.
