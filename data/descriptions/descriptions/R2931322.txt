Beautiful dream villa of 561m2 on a 6640m2 plot with avocado tree plantations.

This three-storey villa is distributed as follows:
On the first floor it has 3 large bedrooms, one en suite with a large jacuzzi and a hydromassage shower,
an extra bathroom and a separate toilet.

A modern fitted kitchen with dining area and a fireplace and a small storage room.

Under the umbrella, it has a great room with a round window that provides 360 degree spectacular views.
