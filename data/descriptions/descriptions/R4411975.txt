Excellent opportunity to live in a semi-detached house in an urbanization on Avenida de Bel-Air, between San Pedro and Estepona.
The house with a private patio and terrace is located in a gated, quiet, well-maintained urbanization with garden areas and a community pool that can be used all year round.
The house has 127 m2, distributed over 2 levels: ground floor and first floor.
On the ground floor we find the hall, a completely renovated and equipped independent kitchen, a complete bathroom with a shower, a cozy and spacious living room with air conditioning and direct access to a private patio with barbecue.
On the first floor is the rest area consisting of 3 bedrooms with built-in wardrobes, terrace and bathroom with bathtub.
Covered parking on the street at the entrance to the urbanization.
Local amenities are within walking distance, including supermarkets, pharmacies, bank, bus stop, nurseries. The beach is 5 minutes by car, just 10 minutes driving from Estepona and San Pedro.
