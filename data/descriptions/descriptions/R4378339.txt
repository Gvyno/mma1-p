A magnificent reformed townhouse in the heart of the Alhaurin el Grande, just below the iconic townhall. This exceptional property offers a unique opportunity to own a townhouse with panoramic views from its delightful roof terrace and a quaint small patio all designed with the utmost attention to detail.

As you step into this stunning townhouse, you will immediately be captivated by the modern aesthetics that are destined to create an inviting living space. Spread across three well-appointed bedrooms and two bathrooms, this townhouse offers ample space for a family or a discerning individual seeking a blend of sophistication and convenience. 

The open plan lounge and kitchen are thoughtfully designed to encourage a harmonious living experience. As you step into the living area, the glass doors open up to a delightful patio, seamlessly integrating the indoor and outdoor spaces, allowing for easy entertainment and relaxation.

Moving to the top floor, a true highlight of this property awaits - a large roof terrace offering breathtaking views of the picturesque valley surrounding Alhaurin el Grande. Whether you're starting your day with a cup of coffee or unwinding in the evening with a glass of wine, this rooftop oasis promises to be the perfect setting for unforgettable moments.

The property also boasts a small garden, thoughtfully located off the kitchen area, providing a private and tranquil space to enjoy al-fresco dining.
