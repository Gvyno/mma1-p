Sotogrande is one of the most attractive, glamorous, and versatile coastal destinations in Spain. Its appeal lies not only in its stunning location but also in its well-planned urban development and exclusivity. It stands as a nearly unparalleled residential community—tranquil, secure, and brimming with a wide array of both sporting and social activities. The diverse range of high-level sports events fosters a highly selective and vibrant social scene.
Sotogrande boasts broad avenues, a marina, beach clubs, a multitude of services, an international school, banks, and shops, all while offering a diverse range of activities.
For golf enthusiasts, Sotogrande is one of Europe's premier golf destinations. Its 2,000 hectares house nine golf courses, including the renowned Valderrama Golf Club, a former Ryder Cup venue. Additionally, the Sotogrande Golf Academy provides exceptional facilities for golfers of all levels.
Polo is another standout sport in Sotogrande, with the Sotogrande Polo Club being one of the world's finest. The club offers excellent facilities for enjoying this sport, hosting annual polo events that are qualifiers for the Polo World Cup.
Sotogrande's luxurious marina, the Real Club Marítimo, provides opportunities for sailing and water sports of all kinds.
