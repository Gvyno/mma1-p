Magnificent apartment in the heart of Nueva Andalucia, located on the second floor of a quiet urbanization with own parking for car and free motorcycle area, communal pool and green areas inside the enclosure. With a variety of services such as supermarkets, schools, pharmacies, restaurants and five minutes from Puerto Banus or San Pedro Alcantara.

The entrance to the apartment communicates directly with a spacious living room very bright and comfortable, with access to a large terrace. In this we will find a fully equipped kitchen with breakfast bar and laundry.

Through the same living room we access a hallway that leads to three bedrooms and a bathroom, which have fitted closets and windows to the outside. The master bedroom also has a small dressing room connected to the bedroom, a bathroom and two windows communicating with the terrace.

It is an ideal apartment to live or for investors, located in one of the best areas of the Costa del Sol.
