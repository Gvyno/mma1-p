AREA
La Quinta in Benahavís is a truly enchanting area on the Costa del Sol, combining exceptional natural beauty with a lifestyle of luxury and convenience.

Located in the hills surrounding Marbella, La Quinta offers stunning panoramic views of the Mediterranean, the golf course and the surrounding mountains.

This place is especially known for its exclusive golf course, which attracts golfers from all over the world thanks to its challenging layout and spectacular views. In addition to golf, the area offers a wide range of outdoor activities, such as hiking and mountain biking, thanks to its natural environment.

La Quinta is also just a few minutes drive from the luxury beaches, gourmet restaurants and high-end shops of Puerto Banús and Marbella, making it a strategic location to enjoy both tranquility and sophistication.

In short, La Quinta is a paradisiacal corner of the Costa del Sol that combines natural beauty, first-class golf and proximity to urban amenities, making it a desired location for both living and vacationing.
