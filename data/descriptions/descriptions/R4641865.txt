Spectacular apartment, completely renovated with modern style, located only 5 minutes drive from one of the best beaches in Marbella.

The master bedroom has a dressing room, through which you access to a private bathroom with a shower.
The entire apartment has been renovated with high quality materials and a contemporary style.
Enjoy this apartment as a first or second residence in a quiet urbanization with a short drive to the beach and all the services.
