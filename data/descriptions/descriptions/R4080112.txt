Semi-Detached Villa in Alhaurín de la Torre

Property Details:

Entering into the property, the open plan living and dining room is located to your left, the fully fitted kitchen is right in front of it and the cloakroom and stairs to the bedrooms are to your right. The living room has a large picture window making the most of the amazing views. There is also a balcony off the kitchen looking out over the town and distant mountains. 

Downstairs are three double bedrooms and a family bathroom. The third bedroom has access down to the studio apartment with kitchen, open plan living area and bathroom. From here are double doors out to a large terrace and pool area.

Outdoor area:

Although there is plenty of local street parking, the property also has it's own private gated parking spot. The swimming pool is located at the lower level of the property.

Views:

This three storey home boasts some of the most fabulous views in town, far reaching towards Malaga bay. Speaking of Málaga, this property is located a 15minute drive to the major city.

Access:

All road access.
