Beautiful four bedroom southwest facing villa in the gated Village Las Lomas de Magna Marbella, 24h security service, two large community pools and tropical gardens. The villa is located close to all services, supermarkets, shops, restaurants, schools, golf courses and only five minutes by car from the sea. 

This property consists of three floors. 
The main floor is distributed in an entrance hall, a spacious living room with direct access to the terrace. The terrace includes tropical gardens with spaces to eat and a chill out area; a bright bedroom with en suite bathroom, a large kitchen, and a guest toilet. The upper floor has two bedrooms with en suite bathrooms and a sunny terrace with partial sea views. The basement consists of the bedroom with bathroom and a garage with enough space for two cars. 

The house also has its own elevator.
