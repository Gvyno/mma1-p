Andalusian style villa built on one level in a very desirable residential area of Elviria. Excellent reform project. Possibility to build an extra floor above or extend existing villa. Mainly flat plot with south - southwest sea views and pool which has steps to the side going down to a lower garden area and a large storage / pool machinery room that can also be transformed into a gym / multi-use room.
