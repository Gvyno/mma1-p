An immaculate four bedroom duplex penthouse in Alicate Playa, an exclusive gated and secure beachside development, offering its residents 24hr security, lush tropical gardens, communal swimming pool and direct access to the beach.

The accommodation comprises of an entrance hall, fully fitted and equipped kitchen with separate laundry room, combined lounge and dining room with direct access to the covered terrace, three guest bedrooms, bathroom, guest cloakroom.

The property is within a short drive to amenities, numerous golf courses and a five minute drive to Marbella centre.
