Traditional country house with private pool located just outside of Alhaurín El Grande. 

Distributed over two storeys, the house benefits from two independent living areas. On the ground floor is a light and airy conservatory area. Inside there are four bedrooms, a living-dining room with fireplace, bathroom and fitted kitchen.

Stairs lead up to a separate living space, featuring another large bedroom with en-suite, a kitchenette and another living room, which is perfect for those who need self-contained accommodation for guests. From here, there is access to a wrap-around covered terrace. A small staircase then leads up to a roof terrace, offering views to the countryside. 

Outside there is a large garage suitable for two cars and it has a WC. The pool area is completely private and surrounded by a grass lawn and palm trees. The property benefits from both well water and mains water and it is just is a 5 minute drive to town and 10 minutes to Alhaurín de la Torre.
