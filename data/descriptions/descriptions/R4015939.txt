Beautifull finca with 360 degrees views towards the sea and the mountains of Mijas. Located on top of a hill you couldn't get more panoramic views than this.

The house consists of 2 levels with on pool level the living area, kitchen and separate toilet. When you go down with the stairs you have 2 bedrooms and an ensuite bathroom. Downstairs is now used as a separate 1 bedroom apartment with an independent kitchen given onto a terrace. The plot is 2514 square meters and has a big private pool, enough space to park 4 cars. Furthermore the garden has plenty of fruit trees and a wine yard. There's also a garage to put away gardening tools.

The property is very accessible through a paved road.

10 minutes away from the beaches of La Cala de Mijas and Fuengirola. 2 minutes drive from la Cala Golf.
