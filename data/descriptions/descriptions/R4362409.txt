AREA

The center of Marbella is a charming place that perfectly combines sophistication and Andalusian charm. Surrounded by pedestrian streets lined with elegant shops, high-quality restaurants and cozy cafes, the center is the vibrant heart of the city. Here, visitors can enjoy a mix of exclusive commerce and authentic Spanish culture.

The promenade is another outstanding attraction in Marbella. Stretching for kilometers along the coast, it offers impressive views of the Mediterranean Sea and is perfect for long walks or relaxing moments in front of the sea. Full of beach bars, bars and terraces, it is the ideal place to taste traditional seafood dishes and enjoy the sea breeze.

The historic center of Marbella is a true treasure. Its narrow cobbled streets, whitewashed houses with flowery balconies and the emblematic Plaza de los Naranjos are a testament to the city's Moorish past. Here, visitors can browse artisan shops, discover historic churches, and enjoy authentic Andalusian architecture.

In summary, the center of Marbella, the promenade and the old town are three fascinating areas that offer a unique combination of sophistication, natural beauty and traditional charm. Whether enjoying the exclusive shops in the center, strolling along the promenade or getting lost in the picturesque streets of the old town, visitors will be immersed in the authentic essence of Marbella.
