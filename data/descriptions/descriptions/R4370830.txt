The house is located in one of the greenest and best-connected areas of the Costa del Sol. It is located on a plot with abundant consolidated trees and has been completely renovated (kitchen, bathrooms, floors, coverings, doors and windows, installations). .
The ground floor consists of a living room with fireplace and a dining room that open onto a porch, kitchen, dining room, two bedrooms, one of them en-suite with a dressing room, and two bathrooms.
On the upper floor there are two bedrooms with a private bathroom and access to the terrace.
In the garden there is a 12 m long swimming pool and a beach bar with a lounge and a bathroom.
Other: Private outdoor parking for 2 cars, underfloor heating and air conditioning.
