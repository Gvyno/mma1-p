This property is right in the port of Sotogrande, Plaza de los Naranjos.
It is an apartment with 2 bedrooms, both with bathroom en suite, spacious, bright and all exterior.
The living room and one of the bedrooms open onto a large terrace with views of the sea and the port of Sotogrande which gives it enormous charm.

The Sotogrande Port concentrates the largest restaurants offer in this luxury development. It is a place full of life throughout the year in which tranquility is not lacking either, given the distribution of its buildings along charming squares with fountains and abundant vegetation.

The beach is 200 meters away.

The apartment has air conditioning, built-in wardrobes in their respective bedrooms, wooden shutters, a 20 m2 terrace with sea views, marble floors and bathrooms, an equipped kitchen and a building with an elevator.

Sotogrande is 40 km from Marbella, 18 km from Gibraltar (with airport) and 80 km from Malaga (with international airport).

The port of Sotogrande is undoubtedly the most sought after area of ​​this luxury resort.
