Bright ground floor apartment in Aloha, Nueva Andalucia. This home offers 2 spacious bedrooms, one with its own bathroom. The second bedroom has its own terrace overlooking the "La Concha"-mountain and this bedroom shares one bathroom with guests.

The community has two swimming pools and the property can be accessed through a lift which makes it great for people with limited mobility.

This is a great investment opportunity and value for money, as it holds a turist rental license and is located right next to Aloha Golf Club and the international school "Aloha College".
