Urban plot with basic project for 16 homes and 32 parking spaces.

Each house is distributed over 2 floors: first floor and ground floor with private pool
- 3 bedrooms
- 4 bathrooms
- terraces
- 2 parking spaces at street level

Located in Torreblanca at C/ Los Madroños 40, Fuengirola, Málaga, the plot is outstretched with a northwest orientation.

The southern boundary has a straight layout and adjoins an adjacent plot, the eastern boundary has a winding path and is the land qualification limit between the UAS and EE types, and the western and northern boundaries adjoin Los Madroños street, with a continuous path. Access to the homes is from the west section of Calle los Madroños.

It has a steep downward slope from the road to the east boundary. The PGOU of the municipality of Fuengirola, approved on January 9, 1987 and partially adapted to the Law of Urban Planning of Andalusia, is applicable.
