Delightful frontline golf townhouse situated in Cabopino. The property features 4 well-appointed bedrooms, two of them with its own en-suite bathroom. The master bedroom offers a private terrace overlooking the golf course, where you can wake up to the tranquil sounds of nature and enjoy a cup of coffee in the morning.

With its prime location on the golf course, this townhouse is a paradise for golf enthusiasts. Cabopino Golf offers a challenging 18-hole course with stunning sea views, making it a dream destination for any golf lover.

In addition to the golf amenities, the property is conveniently situated near the picturesque Cabopino Marina, where you can find a variety of restaurants, bars, and shops. The beautiful sandy beaches of Cabopino are also just a short stroll away, perfect for enjoying the Mediterranean sun and sea.
