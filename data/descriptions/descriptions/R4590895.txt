Luxurious detached villa with 3 bedrooms and 3.5 bathrooms featuring a private pool, garden, and mountain views, located in an upscale development offering all kinds of amenities in Benahavís.

This villa is situated in the exclusive Parque Botanico urbanization, nestled between Marbella and Estepona. The development boasts hotel-quality amenities: a vast outdoor pool, children's pool, heated indoor pool, gym, spa area with sauna, children's play areas, tennis, paddle, and squash courts, 24/7 security, and beautifully maintained gardens.

The villa, set on a 400 sqm plot, has its own private pool with stunning mountain views. It features a secluded garden with ample seating, both covered and open terraces, and plenty of space for outdoor dining or barbecues. There's even an outdoor bar area perfect for entertaining, numerous trees, and a hammock for the ultimate summer nap!

This property is ideal for those who value tranquility, enjoy serene landscapes, and for families who love the outdoor lifestyle.

With two separate entrances, the villa is perfectly suited for guests/family visits or as a vacation rental.

The home includes a main living room with a separate kitchen, which could easily be transformed into an open plan. The master bedroom is on the upper floor, with two additional bedrooms on the ground floor.

Built to a very high quality standard, the property features cold/warm air conditioning, marble floors, etc.

The house has its own private garage with ample storage space.

The surroundings offer beautiful walking trails with a small river, ideal for families with children or pets who enjoy nature.

Parque Botanico is a spectacular enclave located less than 10 minutes by car from the beach and the renowned 'Tikitano by Besaya' and Laguna Beach Clubs. The area includes various amenities such as excellent schools, seven golf courses, restaurants, shops, and more, in the nearby towns of San Pedro, Marbella, and Estepona, an hour from Málaga Airport.
