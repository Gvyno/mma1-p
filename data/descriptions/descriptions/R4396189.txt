This beautiful villa enjoys a splendid location in the hills of La Quinta, Benahavis, with only a few minutes drive away from San Pedro Alcantara and a short 10 minutes to Puerto Banus. The close proximity to amenities and the beach makes this property ideal for rental opportunities. 

Located in a safe and family orientated urbanization, the villa comprises an open-plan kitchen and living room with a stunning sea view. The premium quality Scandinavian designed furniture and kitchen brings a cosy and homely feeling to the ground floor. The living room is directly connected to the terrace, a lovely space to entertain your guests. taking full advantage of the stunning sea views all the way towards the African coast.
