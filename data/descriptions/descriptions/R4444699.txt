This two storey villa enjoys breathtaking countryside views from its elevated position and is distributed over two floors with a large garage underneath the house. Steps lead up to the first storey of the house where you have a large bright lounge with floor to ceiling windows, a good sized kitchen and a bedroom. Upstairs are three more bedrooms one is the principal bedroom with an ensuite bathroom and a private terrace looking out across the countryside. The first level opens onto a covered terrace, private pool and an easily maintained garden.
This superb villa is close to shops and restaurants and a shopping centre, the towns of both Coin and Alhaurin el Grande and just 20 minutes from Malaga airport. This property would make an excellent family home for both permanent living or holidays.
