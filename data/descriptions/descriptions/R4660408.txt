New to market this 1st floor apartment situated in the community Altos de la Quinta only a short distance from the exclusive and emerging Real de la Quinta and the 5 Star Westin Hotel and Golf Resort.
This light apartment benefits from a spacious lounge and dining room area leading to a stunning terrace. This large terrace offers breathtaking views towards Marbella, the Mediterranean and La Quinta Golf.
There is a fully fitted kitchen with all appliances and separate utility room and generously sized master bedroom complete with ensuite and private access to the terrace. The apartment includes a second double bedroom with fitted wardrobes and guest bathroom. 
The apartment comes with an allocated parking place very conveniently located right in front of the portal entrance only a few metres from the apartment.
For the enjoyment of residents is a large community swimming pool nestled amongst tropical gardens.
The commercial centre Monte Halcones is a short drive away with its useful amenities such as pharmacy and supermarket.  Also nearby is the famous golf valley of Nueva Andalucia and Puerto Banus.
The community also has a security guard between 10pm and 6am.
This property would make a fantastic holiday home or for permanent living due to being in a peaceful area but also close to everything the coastline has to offer.
