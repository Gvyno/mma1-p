A superb quality ground floor property with huge west facing terrace plus some lovely modernisation and upgrades. The interior is very well appointed, with a generous lounge diner, fully fitted kitchen, 2 double bedrooms plus a 3rd bedroom which is currently used as an office. You have a full en-suite bathroom and a guest shower room plus this apartment has centrally controlled hot and cold air conditioning throughout plus an underground garage space and storage room.  Owners also have the use of the on-site amenities which include lovely communal gardens, 2 communal swimming pools, 2 children's pools, paddle court, kids park and a sports area.  There is mains gas for the hot water plus very reasonable running costs makes this a very economical property to own and run.
