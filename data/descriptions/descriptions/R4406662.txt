This impeccably presented,  3-bedroom ground floor apartment, facing south, is nestled in one of Riviera del Sol's premier frontline golf complexes. Boasting a spacious layout, the property encompasses a generously sized lounge, a fully equipped kitchen, 3 double bedrooms, 2 full bathrooms (1 en suite), a guest toilet, and a substantial 38 m2 terrace with all day sunshine. The terrace features fitted retractable glass curtains, rendering it usable year-round.

The expansive living area provides seamless access to the terrace and showcases a recently fitted open-plan kitchen with a separate utility room. Each of the 3 bedrooms is adorned with fitted wardrobes, electric blinds and both bathrooms are finished in marble with walk in showers.

Following a comprehensive refurbishment, the apartment showcases polished marble floors throughout, complemented by sun blinds, an entry phone, alarm system, and hot and cold air conditioning for ultimate comfort. Nestled within meticulously maintained gardens, the complex offers an outdoor pool and communal seating areas that provide breathtaking sea and golf views.

The property includes an underground parking space and a spacious storage room. Conveniently located, the complex is a mere 1-minute walk from shops, restaurants, and bars, while the beaches are within a 10-15 minute stroll. Malaga airport is easily accessible, just a 25-minute drive away.
