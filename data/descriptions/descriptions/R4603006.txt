Immaculate beautiful middle floor apartment in Altos de La Quinta. Sea, mountain and valley views.
This well maintained and popular urbanisation is located in the heights of La Quinta, a short walk to the 5 star hotel Westin, the La Quinta golf course and a small convenience store. 5-10 minutes drive also to other services, supermarkets and San Pedro de Alcantera and Puerto Banus.
The urbanisation is gated and has a large pool area with one large pool and one for children.
The 25m2 terrace faces southeast and has sun awnings.
There are 2 bedrooms and two bathrooms (one en-suite) and mosquito nets on all sliding doors and in the laundry room.
The kitchen has relatively new appliances, including induction top. The separate laundry room has also new washer and dryer (both Bosch).
There is an elevator in the building.
One indicated private parking inside the urbanisation.
