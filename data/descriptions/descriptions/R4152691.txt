Beautiful Andalusian style villa located within a small gated community in La Quinta, Benahavis. 

South-west facing, the villa is located in a very peaceful area offering full privacy and has views to the green valley. This property with 416m2 living area, divided over 3 levels, comprises  on the ground floor an entrance hall leading into a spacious living room that connects to the terrace. Further on there is a guest bedroom with ensuite bathroom and fully fitted kitchen with laundry area. 

The master bedroom with ensuite bathroom occupies the complete upper level, designed for complete privacy.

The 200m2 basement with natural light contains 2 bedrooms which share 1 bathroom, a garage and a large multifunctional room which can be used as a gym, cinema room, wine cellar or even an extra bedroom. Possibilities are endless. 

Perfect property if you're looking for peace and quiet but then again being only a few minutes drive away from the center of San Pedro and Puerto Banus.
