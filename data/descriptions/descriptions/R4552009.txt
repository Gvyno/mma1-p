Completely renovated townhouse in a family-friendly Regidor Hills gated community, alongside the Guadalmina River in the charming village of Benahavis. Surround yourself with a modern yet warm interior the moment you enter the bright living room, kitchen, and cozy terrace. You will enjoy cooking in an open kitchen with modern appliances, superb-quality porcelain countertops, and a cooking peninsula.

Bathrooms have walk-in showers and a calm ambiance. On the top floor, you will find yourself in a stunning and vivid bedroom surrounded by two terraces with terrific mountain views. The game/cinema room in the basement offers an extra space to relax and entertain your family and guests. A private underground garage for your car(s) and lots of storage space complete the house which makes it an ideal family or vacation home. 2 community pools are perfect to enjoy yourself during hot summer days.

This family home is in a quiet residential area, surrounded by mountains, and within walking distance to all amenities. Schools, kindergarten, a huge outdoor sports complex, and nature walks are just around the corner. Enjoy sought-after village life in one of the restaurants, cafes bars, and other amenities. San Pedro, Marbella, and Estepona are only a short drive away. Experience a perfect balance between nature and a modern lifestyle.
