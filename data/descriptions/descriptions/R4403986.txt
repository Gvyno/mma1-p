Fantastic flat in the centre of Estepona

Located in a residential area north of Estepona, we find this spacious flat of 107m2. 

The property, built in 2004, is distributed in a spacious living-dining room with a 5m2 balcony, a fully fitted kitchen with utility room, the master bedroom with en-suite bathroom and two other bedrooms sharing a second bathroom.  

The property is sold without parking space, although there is the possibility of acquiring one or  renting it. 

The location is unbeatable, as it has all types of services within walking distance. It has the luxury of being a quiet residential area and at the same time having the town centre and the beach next to it.
