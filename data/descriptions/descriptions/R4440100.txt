Spacious 10,000m2 Finca with a 650m2 villa, unique in its environment due to its proximity to Estepona, the beach and its privacy.

Located at the entrance of Estepona, 5 min by car and a 15 min walk to the beach. In the center of the farm is the 650m2 villa with the following characteristics. 
One-storey villa with 3 bedrooms and their ensuite bathrooms. Kitchen, living room, entrance, toilet, open terrace and sunroom. Garage with capacity for 7 cars and possibility of extension for 9 cars.  Service bedroom, bathroom and tool room. South facing with views to the sea, pool, Gazebo and plot of fruit trees. Solid anti-seismic construction, guaranteed by Architect and plans The Finca is fully self sufficient with 2 water wells, one of its own and one shared with 6 neighbours. 
Both wells are authorised and certified with their pertinent permissions for usage up to date. The private well has its own underground cistern storage with a capacity to store  225 cubic square metres  and automatic filling for the irrigation of the plot and own consumption for the villa. Solar panels for the hot water Hot and cold air in all rooms and  fireplace.

The plot consists of the following:
200 orange trees (each one produces 150kg of fruit)
20 Avocados
55 olive trees with their own olive oil production
Other varied fruit trees, Pears, Custard apples, Mangos, Plums, Figs and Quince.
