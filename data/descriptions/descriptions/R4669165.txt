This beautiful townhouse is for sale in a private urbanization with a community pool and garden.
The house itself consists of 3 floors and a roof terrace, including the basement, which is currently being used as a laundry room and storage room.
The ground floor has a large hall that on one side leads to the large living/dining room with direct access to a terrace, on the other hand to the fully equipped and independent kitchen, an office and a guest toilet.
Via a construction staircase you reach the first floor, where there are 3 double and exterior bedrooms, the main one with an en-suite bathroom and an additional shared bathroom. From here you access the solarium.
It has a total of 255 m2 including terraces and roof terrace, 216 useful m2. Nice sea views and views over community garden. 2 parking spaces in front of the property.
Close to all kinds of shops and restaurants, so as to the beach which is about 10 min by car.
