A stunning four bedroom apartment located in a gated community offering its residents 24hr security, lush communal gardens with swimming pool. Within a short drive to the five star Puente Romano hotel, Marbella centre and Puerto Banus as well as walking distance to coffee shops, restaurants and a Sports Club.

Luxuriously decorated throughout and built to an extremely high standard, the accommodation comprises of an entrance hall leading to a spacious lounge with fireplace and direct access to the terrace, a recently renovated, modern fully fitted and equipped kitchen with breakfast area, four bedrooms all with ample wardrobe space, the master ensuite has access to the main terrace with sea views. The property includes hot & cold air conditioning, under floor heating in the bathrooms, domotic system, a large storeroom and 2 parking.
