Apartment for sale in the lovely town of Tolox.

The house has got 90 meters distributed in a hall, separate kitchen, quite spacious living-dining room, toilet, full bathroom and three bedrooms, it also has a wonderful terrace from where you can see the entire town, storage room and garage.

Tolox is a town located in the heart of the Guadalhorce Valley, and it is a natural reserve with the designation of Sierra de Las Nieves National Park; also recently listed as one of the magical towns of Spain.

In addition, Tolox is known, among other things, for its Natural Spa specialized in respiratory therapies, where people from different communities come to prevent and treat diseases related to the respiratory system, having great fame in this specialty.

Its unique festivals such as Dia de los Polvos and Las Mozas, and the transmission and conservation of customs, gastronomy and popular arts make this town a unique place, which you will always carry in your heart.
