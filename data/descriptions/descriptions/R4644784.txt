This well mantained and bright duplex is located in Marbella Center, second line of Playa de la Fontanilla, a few minutes walk from the Old Town, close to all public amenities, bars, restaurants and the well-known Complex Plaza del Mar (shopping and Wellness Center, with a see view). 

The property consists in 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, both en suite, and a guest toilet located on the low level of the property. The entrance is on the first floor, which composes of a living room with fireplace and a kitchen with a small dinning corner, both connected through a large window. Large windows, from ceiling to floor, have also all the rooms, some of them with directly exit to the terrace which opens a stunning see view, swimming pool and community garden view. It is a pleasure to have your coffee on this  spacious terrace or simply to enjoy the afternoon sun.

The apartment is part of the most famous urbanisation in Marbella town - a modern and closed urbanisation with community pool, fitness center, sauna, beautiful gardins and security 24h.

In the price are included a storage room and an underground parking place, with direct access, via an elevator, to the floor where the apartment is placed.
