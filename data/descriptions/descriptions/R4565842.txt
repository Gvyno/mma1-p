Nestled in the prestigious Los Flamingos golf resort in Benahavis, this contemporary 6-bedroom villa redefines modern luxurious living. Positioned at the forefront of the golf course, the property enjoys an enviable location within one of the most exclusive developments on the Costa del Sol. The gated community of Los Flamingos comprises 9 distinct developments, offering residents breathtaking panoramic views stretching from the Andalusian hills and mountains to the sea, all the way to Africa and the Strait of Gibraltar.

Situated in a tranquil valley, this villa provides a peaceful retreat without compromising on luxury. The location offers easy access to an array of amenities, with the sea and beaches less than 3 km away, and the vibrant attractions of Puerto Banus, San Pedro, Nueva Andalucia's Golf Valley, Estepona's New Golden Mile, Marbella, and the gastronomic village of Benahavis just a short drive away.

The property is surrounded by three 18-hole golf courses, the Michael Campbell Golf Academy, and the opulent Anantara Villa Padierna Palace Resort, known for its Tuscan palace design and luxurious amenities, including a spa, wellness center, multiple restaurants, private beach club, and tennis club.

Recently completed, the sustainable and organic architectural project stands on a generous plot of 2,577 m², offering a house of 722 m² distributed over three levels connected by a lift. The villa, designed with a focus on sustainability, boasts an elegant and harmonious integration with its surroundings. Its modern design incorporates natural materials, innovative technology, and premium-quality finishes.

With a south/east orientation, the villa presents captivating views of the Italian-style landscape, sea, mountains, and golf course. The floor-to-ceiling windows create a seamless connection between the interior and the picturesque surroundings, fostering a constant dialogue with nature. The neutral color scheme, contemporary lines, and open-plan layout contribute to a refreshing, luminous, and airy living space.

This villa stands as a testament to luxurious, modern living amidst the beauty of the Costa del Sol.
