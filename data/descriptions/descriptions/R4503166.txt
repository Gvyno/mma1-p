Corner Townhouse with stunning sea and mountain view. Great location in lower Calahonda! 
Only 600m to the lovely Beach of Calahonda. Easy walking to lots of Restaurants, Shops, Supermarkets, Pharmacy and much more! You can also find fun activities for the whole family within 
 walking distance:
- New Fitness & Padel Club "Oxygen" and Restaurant "Heaven" have just opened! 
- Siesta Golf Club & Restaurant, open 365 days a year!
- Tennis Club "Club del Sol", one of the most popular Tennis Clubs in Costa del Sol! Play Padel, Squash, Gym and Tennis!
- Luna Beach, known for nice food and drinks!
- Cabopino Harbour have a beautiful wide sandy beach, nice Restaurants, live music and great drinks at "Andy's Beach Club". 
- Max Beach Club & Restaurant holds amazing events, serving nice food & drinks! 

The house consists of 2 spacious bedrooms, 2 bathrooms and a living room, with an open kitchen. The kitchen is in its original state, offering you the chance to personalize it according to your preferences! There are 2 terraces on the main level and 1 rooftop terrace with the most amazing view! On a clear day you can see the beautiful mountains of Africa and Gibraltar. 

The community is healthy, gated, very well kept, and has a big pool. 

The location makes it a great holiday home and investment! Many of the houses in the community do holiday rentals. The house has a Tourist License.

Parking: Several parking spots are dedicated to the community. Free parking on the street in front of the complex.
