This characteristic, white two storey townhouse located on a corner has a nice view over the Mediterranean Sea and the surrounding area. The townhouse is located in a calm and enclosed urbanisation, overlooking the pool and garden. The urbanisation's facilities include a tennis court.
The property is situated 300 meters from the beach and walking distance to local amenities and restaurants. 500 meters walking distance from the property to the train.
The first floor includes a kitchen with a four ring hob, oven and dishwasher. Livingroom and a toilet and a living room with two sliding terrace doors that fully opens leading out to the large terrace.
A typical Andalusian mosaic staircase is leading up to the second floor including two bedrooms with fitted wardrobes with access to terraces as well as a toilet with shower cabin.
A large store room is situated under the main terrace.
