Wonderful semi-detached house with private swimming pool and south orientation situated in a well known and consolidated urbanization with golf course and club house with restaurant.
The house is divided into two levels; on the main floor there is a spacious living room with fireplace that communicates with the dining area and also from where you can access to a very spacious porch, the garden and the swimming pool. On this level there is also a fully equipped kitchen with access to the garden and a guest toilet. 
Upstairs there are three en-suite bedrooms. The master bedroom also has a dressing room and terrace. 
The house enjoys hot and cold air conditioning, electric blinds and double glazing. 
Outside, within the plot, there is a covered parking space.
Great family home to live in all year round and enjoy holiday periods.
