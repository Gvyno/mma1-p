THE AREA

Benahavís, a charming town located on the Costa del Sol, stands out for its exquisite gastronomic offer, making it a renowned culinary destination. Surrounded by mountains and lush nature, it offers an idyllic setting for outdoor lovers. Its world-class golf courses attract fans from all over the world.

This picturesque town, strategically located near Marbella and Puerto Banús, enjoys excellent accessibility and connectivity. Its proximity to the golden beaches of the Mediterranean adds appeal to its tourism offering, providing visitors with a complete experience that combines the serenity of nature with the luxury and vitality of the surrounding areas.

Benahavís stands as an oasis of tranquility, where its cobblestone streets and traditional Andalusian architecture invite you to stroll and enjoy an authentic Spanish atmosphere. With a lively nightlife and a varied leisure offering, this town captivates residents and tourists alike, establishing itself as a unique destination that combines the sophistication of the Costa del Sol with the authenticity of Spanish culture.
