Wide and bright Townhouse in the well known Urbanization of Bel-Air, with South orientation and with sea views from the first floor and full of character. 
It is a very quiet urbanization, well maintained and very close, 5 minutes walk from all services (bank, pharmacy, supermarkets as Lild, Mercadona & supeco, bus stop, bars restaurants, etc), at just 8 min driving to Estepona and 5 min to Puerto Banus.
WALKING DISTANCE TO THE BEACH.
The house has 3 floors:
Ground Floor- Walk in through the lovely antique door and you will find a guest WC on the left and an open plan equipped kitchen on the right. Then a dining/living room with fireplace and a gorgeous large private terrace. Wood burner and chimney in the lounge. There are also one large double cupboard for storage and a small one under the stairs for more storage space. 
First Floor- 3 bedrooms with fitted wardrobes, master bedroom with bathroom en suite where you enjoy lovely views to the well kept gardens brimming with flowers, the large pool and green area and on to the sea. The other two bedrooms share a bathroom.
Second Floor- Two solarium terraces, one facing south with views of the sea and the garden and the other terrace to the north with mountain views.
A/C hot and cold with separate splits in each room. Electrical shutters and mosquito nets, lots of outdoor parking space just in front at the entrance door level.
