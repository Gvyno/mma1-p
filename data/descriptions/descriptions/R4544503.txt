Welcome to this nice townhouse with three terraces, one of which is a roof terrace with views of the surrounding areas and the sea. In the immediate area there are two large supermarkets, a pharmacy, a bank and restaurants.

The ground floor consists of a nice open plan kitchen. A spacious living room and a guest toilet. From the living room there is an exit to a terrace which also has direct access to the pool.
On the first floor there are three bedrooms and two bathrooms, one of them en-suite. The bedrooms have built-in wardrobes. One of the bedrooms has a connecting terrace.
On the second floor is the roof terrace. There you can enjoy the sun from morning to evening.

Air conditioning cold/hot. Parking spaces are available in the urbanization.
