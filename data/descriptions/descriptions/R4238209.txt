The most modern and beautiful villa in the area of Torrenueva, Mijas Costa, with stunning views to the coast and the sea. Originally built in 1983 and completely renovated in 2012, the whole house now has underfloor heating and heating by solar panels. Built over two floors with the following accomodation: Lower floor: living room with fireplace and TV room, kitchen-dining room, two bedrooms and three bathrooms. 1st floor: two large bedrooms and two bathrooms plus an office. The villa enjoys a large and well kept garden, a sea water swimming pool. Garage parking.
