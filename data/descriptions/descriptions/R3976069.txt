THE AREA

Nueva Andalucía, in Marbella, is a charming enclave that combines natural splendor with urban sophistication. Recognized for its world-class golf courses and luxury villas, this area also provides access to high-quality international schools, making it an attractive option for families. Residents enjoy proximity to the beach, the opulence of Puerto Banús, and a variety of entertainment options. Additionally, the presence of international schools adds extra convenience for those seeking a global education for their children. Nueva Andalucía presents itself as a comprehensive destination that blends luxury, convenience, and a rich cultural offering on the beautiful Costa del Sol.
