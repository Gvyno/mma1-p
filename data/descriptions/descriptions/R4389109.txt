Welcome an exquisite 838 m2 Andalusian mansion, set within a 5000 m2 lushly landscaped plot. This captivating villa has 8 large double bedrooms, 8 bathrooms and 5 en-suite.

As you approach the grand entrance, the high ceiling line exudes a palatial opulence. Enter into a vaulted ceiling foyer that leads to an extended living room and nearby kitchen, complete with a separate dining room. The wraparound terrace connects these spaces, creating a perfect social environment. Enjoy picturesque golf and sea views from the terrace, along with beautifully landscaped gardens.

The ground floor also offers a study, cloakroom and en-suite bedroom. Upstairs, discover four more bedrooms, including a luxurious master suite with its ensuite bathroom and stunning garden and partial sea views.

Descending to the ground floor, you will find a games room, a study and three more en-suite bedrooms, each with natural light and private terraces. The 90m2 pool and mature gardens offer ample space for relaxation and entertaining. Villa Vitelli is an epitome of luxury living.
