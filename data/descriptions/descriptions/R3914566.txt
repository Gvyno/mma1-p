Bargain. Beautiful irrigated land located just 4 kms from the city of Ronda. On the land there are 2 streams with good water flow. It has a total area of 274 hectares where there is a house in ruins. Composed mostly of scrubland. There are 82 hectares of pasture, 137 hectares of scrub and 55 hectares of holm oaks. Ideal for livestock, agricultural, photoelectric and eolic generation projects. According to municipal ordinance, the minimum plot is 10,000 m2, but 25,000 m2 are necessary to register in the property registry office.
