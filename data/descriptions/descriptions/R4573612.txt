Contemporary development located in the heart of The Golden Mile, just before Sierra Blanca, one of the most exclusive areas in Marbella. Imara stands out for its modern design, high quality materials and ample spaces. This duplex penthouse has a homely and cozy feel to it with a contemporary-modern decor. Due to the south-west facing position, the apartment enjoys sun all day. The property consists of 3 bedrooms all with en-suite bathrooms, 2 large south facing terraces with impressive panoramic sea and mountain views, a spacious living/ dining area and an open plan fully equipped kitchen with breakfast area. The property comes with 2 parking spaces and features hot and cold air conditioning, underfloor heating throughout, quality wooden floors, large walk in wardrobes, fireplace in the living room, walk-in shower, electric blinds, etc. The community offers 24 h security, communal gardens, pools and saunas. Imara is just two minutes by car from Marbella centre and the beach and 5 minutes from Puerto Banus.
