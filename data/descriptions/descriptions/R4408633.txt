Nestled within the heart of the enchanting village of Tolox, this magnificent historical townhouse stands as a testament to the enduring beauty of the past.

Property Details:

With its stately presence, rich heritage, and an abundance of original features, this grand residence offers a glimpse into a bygone era with many original features.  This imposing townhouse graces the charming village streets of Tolox with its grandeur. The facade is a study in timeless architectural elegance, featuring, ornate wrought-iron railings, the property is presented in excellent condition.

The ground floor welcomes you with high ceilings, original ceramic floors, and intricately detailed plaster moldings. The salon boasts a  fireplace that exudes old-world charm and traditional cabinetary. 

Throughout the residence, you'll discover a treasure trove of original features, including gracefully arched doorways, hand-carved wooden banisters, and vintage mosaic-tiled floors
