This fully remodelled 3 bedroom and 2 bathroom ground floor apartment is located on a first line luxury complex on the New Golden Mile between Marbella and Estepona. The apartment has a modern interior with an open designer kitchen with the best German appliances and a very spacious living and dining room. The impressive interior features many luxurious details such as tailormade wardrobes, an electric fireplace and a Bang & Olufsen sound system. The beautiful terrace allows you to easily access the communal pools and invites you to enjoy the lush gardens of this high-end community. 

"Torre Bermeja" is a gated complex with 24-hour security which is well known for its mature landscaped gardens and its amazing "5 star" facilities such as 2 outdoor pools, 2 gyms, an indoor pool and 2 saunas. It has easy access to the beach and promenade and several amazing restaurants are within walking distance.

The apartment comes with an underground parking and a storage room.
