Land with an ecological and sustainable farm of 24,800 square meters that is located 2.5 km from Guaro is a charming and respectful place with the environment. It is surrounded by exuberant vegetation and stands out for its various fruit and olive trees. The main focus of this farm is to practice organic agriculture. Fruit trees provide a wide variety of tasty fruits during different times of the year. The farm has a pool and two houses built of mud and stone, these buildings provide a cool environment in summer and warm in winter. In addition, its architectural style harmonizes perfectly with the natural environment, creating a cozy atmosphere in harmony with nature, the dry stream that runs through it offers a calm environment with the natural environment, ideal for those who seek to connect with nature and live sustainably.
