Duplex penthouse situated in Calahonda, Mijas, a five minutes' drive from the Mediterranean. The entrance on the third floor of the building opens up to an entrance hall, a comfortable living room with dining area, two bedrooms, two bathrooms, a fully equipped kitchen with separate laundry room and an enclosed balcony with a glass wall. This floor (including the balcony) measures 123m2. A staircase leads to an almost 70m2 large roof terrace with spectacular views over the coast. On this terrace is a storage room of 16m2, including a lockable area, as well as a sauna, an outdoor shower and a kitchen block with hot and cold running water and built-in barbecue. The complex has limited, secure communal parking space, but the price of the penthouse also includes a private parking. There is also a very spacious and sheltered swimming pool area within the community. The apartment is accessible by elevator, has central heating and air conditioning in all rooms. A perfect holiday home on the Costa del Sol!
