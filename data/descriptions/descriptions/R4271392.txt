Underground parking space in La Cala de Mijas.
This parking space is located in the Urb. Las Terrazas de la Cala de Mijas, 10 minutes walk from the center of La Cala. It has 42 m2 and comes with a storage room of about 6 m2 included in the price. There is room for a car and a motorcycle. The garage is closed, video surveillanced and there is also a consierge.
