This  villa in La Quinta, boasts five bedrooms and a basement level leisure area. As you enter the property, you'll be struck by the impressive high ceilings and immediate views of the Mediterranean Sea and golf course.

On the main floor, there is a bedroom with an en-suite bathroom, providing privacy for guests. Adjacent to this bedroom, you'll find two rooms currently configured as home offices.

Upstairs, the master bedroom features high ceilings and its own en-suite bathroom. Another double bedroom with an en-suite bathroom is also located on this level, both enjoying fantastic views.

The basement level is designed for entertainment, with a cinema room, bar, gym, and pool table. Additionally, there's a separate bedroom with a mini kitchen and bathroom.

Outdoors, the property offers a saltwater heated swimming pool and a fantastic jacuzzi for relaxation. Underfloor heating is available throughout the entire villa for year-round comfort. Access to the roof terrace can be conveniently achieved with the lift, allowing you to appreciate the spectacular views. The lift can take you also to the basement level.

A private garage with space for three cars ensures secure parking. This villa combines modern comfort with breathtaking views, making it an ideal choice for a high-end Mediterranean lifestyle in Benahavis, Marbella.
