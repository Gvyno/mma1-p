El Real de El Campanario - Where Lifestyle  Meets Nature.

Nestled within the exclusive enclave of El Real de El Campanario, this beautiful 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom apartment offers an unparalleled living experience in the midst of a lush tropical paradise. 
A boutique community with  only 4 blocks and 2 ample pool areas, discover the epitome of tranquility, all within the safety of a gated community with 24-hour security.

As you step inside, you're greeted by a bright and spacious living/dining room, bathed in natural light, creating an inviting atmosphere for relaxation and entertainment. 
From the living area you step out onto the expansive, partly covered terrace that overlooks the enchanting tropical gardens, swaying palm trees, and the glistening pool. 
This south-facing oasis is your private retreat, perfect for savoring sunsets and hosting gatherings with friends and family.

The main bedroom features an ensuite bathroom with a bathtub, while the second bathroom boasts a convenient shower, both adorned with windows that usher in the soft glow of natural light.

Your culinary adventures await in the fully equipped kitchen, complete with Siemens appliances.

In addition to the generous interiors, this second-floor apartment offers practicality with a spacious laundry area, 1 underground parking space, and additional storage, catering to your every need.

Beyond your doorstep lies the prestigious El Campanario Golf & Country House, an elegant Balinese-style clubhouse.
Imagine a lifestyle where a well-equipped gym, a heated indoor pool, a rejuvenating hairdresser, a restaurant serving culinary delights, and a plethora of sports classes for both kids and adults are all within walking distance.
