This apartment has 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms and underground parking and a storage room. On entry the kitchen is on the left and the lounge and large terrace is on the right. The terrace is very spacious and has lots of shade as well as sun to suit people's preferences. One bedroom shares a terrace with the kitchen and the other has an en suite and an area of built in wardrobes. Great position for the Myramar Commercial Centre and 5 minutes from the beach. Easy access to the links to Marbella and Malaga. The pool and gardens are beautiful and the views over the Sierra de Mijas are stunning.
