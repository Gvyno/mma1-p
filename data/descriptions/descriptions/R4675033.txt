The generously sized lounge and dining area seamlessly lead to an inviting terrace, enhanced by the addition of glass curtains and direct access to the well-kept community gardens.

To the right of the dining area, one finds access to the two comfortably proportioned bedrooms and two full bathrooms, ensuring a practical and well-organized living space.

This apartment stands as an attractive option, whether utilized as an occasional sunshine retreat or seized as a lucrative rental investment opportunity, thanks to its unbeatable location.
