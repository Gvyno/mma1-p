Ground Floor:
The ground floor unveils a harmonious blend of functionality and aesthetics. Second Floor:

Here, you'll be greeted by an integrated BBQ/kitchen area, designed for al fresco dining and culinary delights. An adjacent lounge and dining area, graced by breathtaking views of the coastline and majestic mountains, create an ideal setting for relaxation and entertainment.
