This Frontline golf penthouse offers two good sized bedrooms and will sleep four in comfort; one room is a twin the other a double, both have outside space and terraces. The bathroom is shared between both rooms and offers a shower as well as a bath. The living room and dining space opens onto the terrace which also has steps leading up to another roof terrace with views out to sea. The kitchen is well appointed and has everything you would need for your stay. The apartment is fully air-conditioned and comes with two underground car park paces in the gagage. A lift will take you straight up to the Penthouse.

The penthouse apartment is located within the secluded Valle Romano golf resort, it offers stunning views across the sea towards Gibraltar and also north Africa, the Sierra de las Nieves provides the other backdrop and you can see Gibraltar easily from the terraces. There is also a small shop at the top of the road and a tapas bar, however the Vale Romano Golf Club is just a minutes walk away offering a bar and restuarant. If you are a golf fan this is an ideal location, however it is also great to explore this southern part of Spain. The town of Estepona is about 15 minutes by car, with a famous old town and lovely beach front. Of course north Africa is a popular visit and may be reached by day trip from nearby Tarifa.
