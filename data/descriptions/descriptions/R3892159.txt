This bright Scandinavian style apartment, boasts a lovely covered terrace, with some sea views, leading into the spacious living area with an open plan, fully fitted kitchen with NEFF appliances, 1 bedroom en-suite, a 2nd bedroom and a 2nd spacious guest bathroom, with access to an enclosed patio.

This apartment is ideal for someone not wanting to depend on a car, as the beach, restaurants and amenities are at the doorstep.
