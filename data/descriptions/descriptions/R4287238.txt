A spacious comfortable country house on 10,000 m2 of land.

This nice campo home has from 3 bedrooms. The master bedroom has bathroom en-suite. There is another bathroom. 
The layout of the house is completed by the large living room with open kitchen.
There is a built-up area of about 120 m2.

The property is located in the beautiful mountain area of Las Tres Cruzes, just 2 kilometres from the village of Bellavista. Bellavista has many of the daily necessary amenities such as supermarket, bar, restaurant, bakery, butcher and a primary school.
There is electricity, well and shared community water for irrigation.
Good access.
