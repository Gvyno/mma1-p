Beach side in Costalita

Spectacular townhouse in gated and secure community steps from the beach in Costalita.

It is distributed on the first floor with a large living room with open kitchen to the living room, which gives plenty of light and space. From the living room we access to a nice terrace and private garden, ideal to enjoy the outdoors. On the same floor we find 1 bedroom, bathroom and a terrace with garden. On the second floor there are 2 bedrooms, one with terrace, 2 bathrooms, dressing room.

There is a large communal pool and large gardens well maintained by the community.

Everything is close, schools, stores, 10 minutes from Marbella and Estepona.
