Modern south-west facing duplex situated just a few minutes' drive to the popular Puente Romano Resort with stunning views. This bright and well-planned apartment is distributed over two levels. On the first floor you have an open-plan living space with a top quality Siemens and Bosch kitchen and terrace perfect to see the beautiful sunset, a guest bedroom and a guest toilet. On the second floor there is a large master bedroom with walk-in wardrobe and private terrace. It has a second spacious bedroom with fitted wardrobes and separate bathroom.  

Special features include: underfloor heating (water)  and Daikin Altherma + airzone air conditioning throughout, blinds, Hans-Grohe / Geberit / Roca and Xlight Porcelanosa qualities in the bathrooms.

The apartment is ideally located on the Golden Mile in a gated community with manicured gardens and communal pool. It comes with two parking spaces.
