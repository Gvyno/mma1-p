A beautiful apartment located in Los Aquilaros/Los Arqueros Golf and Country Club resort. It consists of 3 bedrooms with fitted wardrobes, 2 bathrooms (one en-suite), a kitchen with a connected utility room, a spacious living/dining room, 2 terraces, a communal pool, beautiful gardens, an underground parking, lift access and a storeroom. 

The view of the El Higueral golf course, surrounding mountains and the sea, create a panoramic landscape from both terraces and in clear weather even the coast of North Africa can be seen.

The cubhouse of the Los Arqueros G&CC (located just 5 minutes from the apartment) has several restaurants, a bowling alley, paddle & tenniscourts and a fitness centre.
