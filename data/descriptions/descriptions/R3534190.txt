In the heart of the impressive and famous town of Casares, just a fifteen minute drive from the coast is this charming Spanish town house. Just a few minutes walk from the town square, everything the town has to offer, including tapas bars and restaurants, shops, banks, a school and a pharmacy, is within walking distance.

Upon entering the property, the level of the ground floor has the potential to be used as a living room, if you wish, it leads very well to the second level, where the owners have created a "comfortable" living room, which can be Use as a second bedroom, and independent kitchen and a good size.
On the third level is the master bedroom and the independent bathroom with shower.

The fourth and last floor is where the property can be really appreciated, renovated to a bright and well ventilated living room, offers access to the private terrace of the property with stunning views over part of the town and the mountains. There is also a public parking nearby.

A great opportunity to own an authentic Spanish town house.
