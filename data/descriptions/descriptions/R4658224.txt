This 2-bedroom apartment is located in the beautiful area of Los Hidalgos, Manilva. Situated in a nice tranquil complex, this apartment offers a range of features that make it the perfect place to call home.

The apartment boasts a spacious and bright living room with large windows that provide ample natural light and a semi-open kitchen fully equipped with high-quality appliances. 

The apartment features two comfortable bedrooms, and 2 bathrooms, one en-suite. The bedrooms are well-appointed and offer plenty of storage space while the bathrooms are modern and stylish, with high-quality fixtures and fittings.

Additionally, there is a charming covered terrace where you can enjoy stunning views of the vineyards that surround the community and of the sea.
Furniture and underground parking are included in the price. 

In addition to its impressive interior, this apartment also offers a range of extras that enhance the overall living experience. The communal pool is perfect for cooling off on hot summer days, while the communal garden provides a tranquil space to unwind and enjoy the outdoors. 
With its prime location, this apartment is close to the Castillo of Duquesa, as well as to Puerto de la Duquesa, having nearby 2 supermarkets, a gym, some restaurants and of course the town, the beach and the sea, making it convenient for all your needs.
