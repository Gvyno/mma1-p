Building plot in La Victoria neighbourhood

This plot is located right in the centre of Malaga city, in one of the quietest residential areas in the centre of Malaga, the neighbourhood of La Victoria. 
It is also an excellent commercial area with all services easily accessible on foot.

One of the property stands out for the possibility of building an underground car park with 10 parking spaces. It also has a licence to build a building with three floors. It has a plot area of 72m2 and a total buildable area of 144m2.

Undoubtedly, a property with great potential for investors. 
It is located only five minutes walk from the emblematic Plaza de La Merced.
