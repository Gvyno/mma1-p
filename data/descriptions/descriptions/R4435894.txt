On top of the world. This is the feeling you get when you stand in the grounds of Casa Serena, one of the best located villas on the Costa del Sol. With panoramic sea and mountain views from Sierra Nevada in the East to Gibraltar and Africa in the West with nothing but the Mediterranean in between,  it is simply breathtaking.

The main house comprises of all the bedrooms on the ground floor. A large entrance hall greets you which leads into an oval formal lounge and dining room next door. To the right is the large kitchen with room for a dining table, a sitting area / bedroom, master bedroom with huge en-suite and dressing room. To the left is the laundry room, 2 bedrooms with shared bathroom and a large bedroom en-suite at the other end of the villa. All rooms lead out on to the huge covered terrace which has been glazed with glass curtains. 
Upstairs in the tower room is an informal lounge / TV room with access out on to a spacious terrace.

The guest / staff accommodation is housed in a separate building below the garden which comprises of a 2 storey living space with an open plan lounge / kitchen, bedroom and bathroom with access on to its own private courtyard with fountain. 

Outside, the double garage is accessed via giant electric wrought iron gates. The 1700m2 plot has lawn on both sides of the villa. The pool area is terraced and also has a wet bar with stools to sit in the water whilst sipping a cocktail. The elevated garden has the most amazing panoramic views from all corners.
