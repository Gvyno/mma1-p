Located in the area known as Valdeolletas.

Quiet streets with schools, nurseries, the paddle tennis club El Mirador, the Monte Paraíso Country Club....
But very close to the centre of Marbella: less than 5 minutes from the Hotel Don Pepe, down Avenida Cánovas del Castillo and also, less than 5 minutes from La Cañada shopping centre.
