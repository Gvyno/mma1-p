On the top floor is the possibility for a 2 bedroom apartment 100m2, there is a bathroom and two terraces, one of which is a roof terrace. Benahavis is located 7 km inland from the coast and Marbella. Known for its excellent restaurants / bars and hospitality. Malaga airport at 75 km.
