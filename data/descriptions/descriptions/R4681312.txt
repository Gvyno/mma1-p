Beautifully maintained apartment in La Reserva de Marbella, just 15 minutes away from Marbella. La Reserva de Marbella is an established urbanization with charming communal areas, fruit trees, a natural pond with a fountain, and two spacious community pools. The living room features a fireplace and large windows that allow plenty of natural light, as well as a bright dining area.
