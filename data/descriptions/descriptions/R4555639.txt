Semi detached-house with 5 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms in Urb Almendros Golf, Puerto del Almendro, Benahavis.

It has two floors, on the top is the entrance, a toilet for guests, living room with fireplace and stunning views of the mountains and the sea. Kitchen, terrace with dining table, and one bedroom with bathroom insuite.

On the ground floor, there are 4 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, the main one with en suite bathroom and dressing room. All bedrooms have access to the garden.

The house has hot and cold air conditioning with independent control on each floor, underfloor heating in kitchen, living room and bathrooms.

All appliances are Siemens brand.

The urbanization is gated, has a swimming pool, tennis court, security cameras and security gates with opening by controls.

The area is very quiet, it is strategically located with access to Nueva Andalucia, through La Quinta, without having to go down to the A7, it also has access for toll motorway AP7, direction Estepona or Malaga.
