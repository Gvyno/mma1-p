There are a total of 3 bathrooms and a guest toilet, catering to every need. On the first floor, there is also a practical and enclosed laundry room with both a washing machine and a dryer.

The generously sized and bright living room creates an inviting space with a fireplace and direct access to the pleasant covered terrace. The well-equipped kitchen, of high Danish quality in wood with granite countertops, is bright and spacious with a garden door leading to the large terrace.

The villa impresses with beautiful consistent floors and underfloor heating, and for optimal comfort, there is air conditioning in all rooms. This fantastic villa, being sold fully furnished, is truly an experience that must be seen to be appreciated.
