Sitting atop an elevated parcel of land within the Finca Cortesin Resort, this remarkable villa provides sweeping vistas of both the golf course and the sea. The exterior spaces are adorned with meticulously maintained gardens and a splendid pool. The ground level effortlessly merges with the garden, thanks to its floor-to-ceiling glass doors. With refined interiors and distinctive design elements, this villa radiates opulence. The upper-level bedrooms each boast private terraces, highlighting the architectural mastery of Vicens & Ramos throughout.
