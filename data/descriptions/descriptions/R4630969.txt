This stunning designer home is located on the elevated ground floor, within the exclusive La Cala Golf Resort, which offers a range of amenities including a hotel and spa, a cozy bar and restaurant, and several golf courses. It is located in the Grand View urbanization, built in 2019.

The property consists of 2 full bedrooms with built-in wardrobes, 2 full bathrooms and a well-distributed and bright living room. The living room connects to a large terrace of more than 60 square meters, equipped with Lumon glass curtains. From the terrace, you can enjoy mountain views and it is located on the front line of golf.

The home has central air conditioning and a furnished and equipped kitchen, open with high-quality appliances and a custom-made island. The guest bedroom features a versatile wall bed that doubles as an office space, complete with a fully equipped modular unit. The master bedroom has an en-suite bathroom and direct access to the terrace.

Additional features include solar panels for water heating, motorized security blinds on all windows for added privacy and security, hot and cold air conditioning system, and a softening and osmosis water system to ensure the highest quality of water. 

The common areas are in perfect condition and include swimming pools, underground parking space and storage room.
