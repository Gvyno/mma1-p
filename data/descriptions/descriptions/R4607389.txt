San Roque Golf and County Club. Wonderful building plot, set within oak trees and in a private location with a sea view complete with full services and permission to construct a substantial villa of 750m2 plus a pool. The estate has 24 security and landscaping is maintained to highest standards. There are 4 restaurants close by, clubhouse and bar, practice area and driving range,  and 2 championship golf courses. Golf memberships are available.
