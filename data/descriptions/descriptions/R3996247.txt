A homey three bed and three bath townhouse right in the center of Las Lagunas a short walking distance from all amenities. 

The house is distributed over four levels, starting with an ample three car garage on the ground floor with a storage room and a bathroom. The first floor has a large kitchen, living room and a dining area as well as a guest bathroom and a lovely outside area. The second floor has three bedrooms and a bathroom. The master bedroom is very spacious and has a large built in closet. The last level has an ample solarium with a covered area and a large utility/storage room. 

The townhouse is in an excellent condition and there is an option to purchase it furnished. It is an ideal home for a family looking to be close to schools, stores and restaurants.
