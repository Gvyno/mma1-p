Location, orientation, garden for private use, small and quiet community, spacious and bright rooms.. . what else do you need?

Located a 15 minute walk from amenities and the beach in the lower part of Calahonda, this townhouse is located in the center of the highly sought after Urbanization Triana de Calahonda. Enjoy the sun all day. All rooms are south facing and it has a large enclosed garden which is communal and maintained by the community gardener but for personal use only.

On the ground floor, the entrance leads to the bright air-conditioned living-dining room also has a guest toilet and then to the already spacious kitchen which could also be opened to the living-dining room to create a nice large and bright open space with 3 large French doors opening onto the terrace and the garden.
