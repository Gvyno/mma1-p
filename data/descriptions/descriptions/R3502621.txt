This land is located in Cañete La Real, Málaga. 

Land of 460,000 m2. Farm with almond trees, olive trees (about 1500) and pastures. 

Of the total land, about 11.000 meters are "urbanizable" and 800 meters "urbano". 

It is located on both sides of the Camino del Calvario and the Mirador. 
Ideal farm to practice paragliding and risk sports, or organize countryside resort.
