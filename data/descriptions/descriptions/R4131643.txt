This wonderful bright & spacious three bedroom two bathroom middle floor corner apartment situated within one of the best developments in Los Arqueros Golf and Country Club.  The property further benefits from large wrap around terrace with lovely garden and sea views, accessed from the lounge, master bedroom and bedroom two. Also included with the apartment is a large underground parking space and storage room. There are also two large communal pools. You can also the enjoy the many amenities Los Arqueros has to offer including; two restaurant's, fitness centre and sports bar with 10 pin bowling. The lovely town of San Pedro de Alcantara is only a five minute drive away with Puerto Banus and Marbella ten to fifteen minutes away by car.
