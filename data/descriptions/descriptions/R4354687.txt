You enter this beautiful home through an Andalusian style door into a lobby with stairs welcoming you to the 1st floor hallway that leads on to the spacious living and dining area.The fully fitted kitchen and utility room.
From both the kitchen and living room you arrive on the lower terrace with swimming pool. The amazing views from here are undoubtedly the best anyone could wish for, the property overlooks Los Arqueros golf course, the Mediterranean sea, Gibraltar and even the coast of Morocco. Back inside along the hallway on the same floor you will find 2 en-suite bedrooms, both with fitted wardrobes.

The master suite is located on the top floor offering complete privacy with a luxury en-suite bathroom, fitted with a double sink unit, bathtub and standing shower. Off of the master bedroom you will find a superb private sea facing covered terrace with a Jacuzzi spa, 

The property benefits from underfloor heating and Central A/C throughout, as well as super fast fiber optic internet.

The house is just a short walk from the community Bar, Restaurant, butchers and Delicatessen.
The award winning Urbanisation of La Heredia is popular with year round residents and holiday makers alike. In addition to the amenities offered in the community, there is 24 hour security in this gated Urbanisation as well as 24 hour Emergency at home doctor service included for all residents and Guests in the Community.
The beautiful Pueblo of La Heredia is just a few minutes drive to San Pedro, Benahavis, Puerto Banus and Marbella. It is an ideal location for discovering the many tourist delights of Andalucia, including Ski-ing in Sierra Nevada. There are numerous golf courses within a short drive, including Los Arqueros and La Quinta.
From the house you can reach Malaga or Gibraltar airports in 45 minutes via the autopista (AP7), making this a very accessible location.
