Excellent family townhouse, in a very good location, Bel Air (Cancelada).

Close to the tennis club and just five minutes walking to the Mercadona and Aldi. A convenient junction in Cancelada allows you to quickly get to San Pedro, Marbella or Estepona. Quiet gated residential urbanization with good neighbours and a communal pool. Perfect home for a family.
