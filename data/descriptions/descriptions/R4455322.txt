Los Olivos is a small gated community within Los Arqueros Golf & Country Club occupying a superior elevated position. The community is made up of 72 two and three bedroom apartments and penthouses and some very spacious 4 bedroom duplex penthouses. Most of the properties offer magnificent views of the Los Arqueros golf course, surrounding mountains and Mediterranean Sea. 

The apartments all have underground parking and lift access and some also have private storage rooms. Los Olivos boasts two communal swimming pools with separate children's pools set amongst mature manicured gardens where La Zagaleta offers a green backdrop.

Los Arqueros Golf & Country Club is a prestigious golf club located in the municipality of Benahavís, near Marbella, in the province of Malaga, Spain. The club was designed by the legendary Spanish golfer Severiano Ballesteros and opened its doors in 1991. It is known for its beautiful setting in the Sierra de Ronda foothills, offering panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea and the surrounding mountains.

The history of Los Arqueros Golf & Country Club dates back to the late 1980s when Ballesteros, who had achieved great success as a professional golfer, embarked on a career in golf course design. He collaborated with Spanish architect Antonio García Garrido to create a challenging and picturesque golf course in the Costa del Sol region.

The construction of Los Arqueros Golf & Country Club began in 1989, and it was officially inaugurated in 1991. The course covers an area of approximately 60 hectares and features 18 holes spread across a hilly landscape with numerous natural obstacles such as ravines, streams, and lakes. Ballesteros' design aimed to incorporate the natural features of the land while creating a demanding yet enjoyable golfing experience for players of all levels.

Over the years, Los Arqueros Golf & Country Club has gained a reputation as one of the top golf destinations in the Costa del Sol. Its challenging layout, combined with the stunning surroundings, has attracted golf enthusiasts from around the world. The club has hosted various professional tournaments, including events on the European Tour's Challenge Tour.

In addition to the golf course, Los Arqueros Golf & Country Club offers a range of facilities and amenities for its members and visitors. These include LA Green clubhouse with a restaurant LA VIews and bar, a pro shop, practice areas, tennis courts, a gym, Tenpin bowling. The club has a vibrant social scene, organizing events, tournaments, and activities throughout the year.
