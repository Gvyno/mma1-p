This ground-floor apartment with spectacular sea views is located in a secure complex with great views from the spacious terrace. 
The apartment is within walking distance from the centre of Cancelada town and close to several golf courses.

The property consists of a living room with nice furniture, a fully equipped kitchen with dishwasher, microwave, oven, coffee maker and 
washing machine, 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms, one with a bath and the other with a shower.
Air conditioning with hot and cold air throughout the property. The property comes fully furnished with lots of extra's like sunscreen on the terrace, alarm system,...

The highlight of this accommodation is the south-west facing terrace with beautiful sea and mountain views. 
The property has access to communal areas with a fenced pool (open from April to the end of October), a garden and an outdoor shower.

A parking space is available in the complex's underground garage and a storage room.
