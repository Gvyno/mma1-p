Situated in a gated and quiet community, close to the Los Arqueros and El Higueral golf courses, with an indoor heated pool, sauna and an outdoor pool, this 2 bed / 3 bath Duplex Penthouse has sea, mountain & golf  views from both levels.
On the 1st  floor you have the master bedroom with en- suite bathroom, a 2nd bedroom with en- suite
bathroom, the kitchen, a spacious living room with working fireplace, and a terrace with fantastic views.
Both bathrooms have underfloor heating.
On the second level you have an office space, also with en- suite bathroom, connected either via
the staircase to the living room or via an exterior staircase. On that level is also the jacuzzi as well as a barbecue.
Located only a 2 minute drive to  the commercial centre and restaurants of Monte Halcones, 5 minutes
drive from San Pedro, and 10 minutes to the idyllic town of Benahavis, several golf courses are also within
easy reach.
