Here we have a property which has been converted from an old rice mill in San Martin del Tesorillo, in the urbanisation of El Molino. 210M2 with two bedrooms & three bathrooms.
The large and impressive reception has a fireplace with three windows which overlook the campo and an array of trees including orange and avocado
A large fully equipped kitchen with another fireplace which leads out to a private garden / terrace.
As well as a pantry and wash room there is a spacious entry hall with cloakroom and a shower leading off - this could be made into an extra bedroom.
The house has its own covered adjoining parking space.
The urbanization has two private gated entrances, a communal pool, gardens and green areas.
