Proximity to Paradise:
Experience the essence of life in a delightful year-round climate. Embrace your passion for living in this exquisite penthouse, ideally situated in the heart of the "pueblo blanco" (white village). Revel in breathtaking sea and mountain views while enjoying the tranquility that this apartment offers. Plus, a convenient bus service to Fuengirola, renowned for its beaches and connecting train service to Malaga airport and the city center, makes this location even more desirable.
There is also a possibility for purchasing a garage space with the apartment. 

A World of Adventure Awaits:
Perched above the Mediterranean, Mijas Pueblo provides easy access to a diverse range of sights and activities. It's strategically located between Malaga airport (25 minutes by car) and Marbella (25 minutes), with the beach and watersports in Fuengirola just a short drive away (10 minutes). Golf enthusiasts will find numerous courses nearby, and outdoor enthusiasts can explore mountain trails and indulge in an array of activities on the Costa del Sol, including tennis, paragliding, horse riding, motor sports, spas, and even winter skiing in Sierra Nevada (2h10 by car).

Cultural Enrichment Awaits:
For those with an interest in cultural activities, a treasure trove of historical sights awaits, including those from the Moors and Romans. Discover a variety of museums, theaters, and guided tours within easy reach, with Malaga (35 minutes by car), Frigiliana and Nerja (1h00), Gibraltar (1h25), Granada (1h40), Cordoba (1h55), Cadiz (2h15), and Seville (2h30) all within reach.
