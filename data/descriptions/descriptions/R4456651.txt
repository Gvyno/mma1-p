This charming ground floor apartment in Jardines de Calahonda has been partially refurbished to an excellent standard. It has access to the communal garden and a terrace for private use. The flat offers a spacious living/dining room with fireplace, a fully fitted kitchen, two double bedrooms with fitted wardrobes and en suite bathrooms. One of the bedrooms has direct access to the communal garden. It also has communal parking and a private courtyard at the entrance with double gates for extra security, and best of all it is only 800 metres from the beach! The community of Calahonda also has private security.
