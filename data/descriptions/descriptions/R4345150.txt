Private Andalusian style 4-bedroom villa, with garden and private heated pool located in a secluded and peaceful community with private parking.
This lovely villa is perfect for a family either for permanent living or as a holidays home.
The bright spacious lounge with fireplace, leads onto the pool and garden with built in barbecue and loads of sun loungers. Adjoined to the living room there is the dining area, an independent refurbished Scandinavian style large kitchen with high quality materials and high end appliances and a large window looking into the entrance drive-in. In the same level there is a double bedroom with ensuite bathroom. Upstairs there are 3 further bedrooms all with their own bathrooms and the 3 of them have access to a large terrace with sitting areas.

The villa has air conditioning / heating throughout, a garage/storage
Located on the mid-western borders of Calahonda within 15 minutes' walk of the beach, shops, bars, restaurants, and scenic walks around the nearby Cabopino golf. High rental potential, tourist licence on place.
