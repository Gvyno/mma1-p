Frontline golf 2 bedroom ground floor apartment located in Guadalmina Golf. 
It is close to shops and amenities and within walking distance to San Pedro de Alcantara.
The complex has pool and separate children's pool and well maintained gardens.
Included are a garage space and a storage unit.
The property boasts central heating with radiators, a fireplace and air conditioning.
Main bedroom has ensuite marble bathroom and is East facing with a balcony, while the guest bedroom leads out to the main covered terrace.
The lounge/dining is large for such a property. Marble floors throughout.
Good views over the golf course and plenty of afternoon sun.
A separate laundry room is off the kitchen.
A slight refurbishment is recommended to reach the property's full potential. The running costs are very reasonable and the layout is very good too.
Well priced!
