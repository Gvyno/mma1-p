Finca in Casares, in the Celima area, with spectacular views of the mountains.
At the entrance of the property there is a large car park for several cars, a beautiful terrace with Casares stone and the  swimming pool.
Next to the house there is a beautiful covered terrace with a kitchen and a barbecue area, it is an ideal area to have parties, and enjoy the good weather that we have on the Costa del Sol.
The house is built in 2 floors.
At the entrance of the house there is a hall which gives access to a fully equipped kitchen, next to the kitchen is the dining room which has a ceiling with wooden beams and very beautiful views of the Sierra Bermeja, on the same floor is the living room with fireplace and wooden beams on the ceiling.
On the other floor is the master bedroom with wooden beams on the ceiling and fireplace, this bedroom has an ensuite bathroom, on the same floor there are two bedrooms and two bathrooms.
There is an exterior wooden house, which is used as a warehouse and laundry area.
There is an office, independent of the house.
On the plot there are beautiful areas to walk with flowers and fruit trees.
This country property is very private and has a few hundred-year-old trees.
It is located 15 minutes by car from the beach and the city of Estepona, 10 minutes from the town of Casares, 45 minutes from Marbella and 60 minutes from Malaga airport.
