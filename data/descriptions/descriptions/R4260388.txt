The living room is spacious and bright. The 3 bedrooms are spacious and have built-in wardrobes. The living room and 3 bedrooms have direct access to the terrace.
The main bedroom has an ensuite bathroom with jacuzzi, sink, toilet, and bidet. The second bathroom is equipped with a walkin shower, sink, toilet, and bidet. 
The parking is a designated parking within the community.
This well-kept complex has 2 swimming pools and 1 children's pool.
