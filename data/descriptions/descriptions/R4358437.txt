It's been newly refurbished to the highest standards, to create a luxurious contemporary home while maintaining many of its original exterior features.

The entrance hall leads to a substantial lounge and dining room, with full width sliding windows opening to a large terrace overlooking the pool and garden. The pool is heated and has an electric cover.

The modernized kitchen is fully equipped by Bosch. Also on this level are two double bedrooms with en-suite and a guest toilet.
The first floor comprises of a double bedroom master suite with dressing room and private terrace. You also have a second bedroom with an en-suite.

The lower floor has a cinema room, a bedroom, utility room with a kitchen and a bar with a pool table and a two car garage.
The villa is located in a quiet cul-de-sac and you enter through a pedestrian gate, with a secure garage entrance with private gated off street parking.

It is walking distance to shops, restaurants and a short drive to golf courses and Puerto Banus.
