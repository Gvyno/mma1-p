The home was conceived with an open design, incorporating large windows to make the most of natural light throughout the interior space. The integration of an open kitchen in the area that houses the living room and dining room allows for an efficient and comfortable distribution, offering the best organization of space. The apartment has a fully furnished kitchen equipped with SIEMENS appliances, electric blinds and the installation of LED spotlights, guaranteeing a modern and functional environment.

The spacious common areas have been designed with pedestrian paths that flow through beautiful garden areas, creating an environment where you can enjoy a walk and easily access the recreation and leisure areas of the complex. The infinity pool, with its spectacular panoramic view of the sea and the African coasts, becomes the perfect setting to appreciate impressive sunsets that will undoubtedly take your breath away.

A Social Club offers a space to rest and disconnect, either by immersing yourself in reading a book or enjoying with friends in a pleasant environment with wonderful open views. Additionally, a community gym is provided to provide you with the best physical well-being from the comfort of your home.
