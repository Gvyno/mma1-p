Located in an elevated location, it is south facing with views towards the coastline to Gibraltar and Africa. Just a short drive away are the beaches of Manilva, Duquesa Marina and plenty of shops, bars & restaurants. This is a pretty 2 bedroom, 2 bathroom mid-floor south facing apartment. The entrance hall leads to the open plan living and patio doors to the terrace with views of the stunning communal gardens and view towards the Mediterranean. There is a fully equipped kitchen, 2 spacious bedrooms - both with ample wardrobe storage and a family shower room. The main bedroom benefits from an en-suite bathroom and south facing views. The property is being sold furnished with an underground parking bay. A wonderful holiday home and perfect location for those who are active and love wonderful parks and countryside walks, but still close to the amenities and beaches of Duquesa.
