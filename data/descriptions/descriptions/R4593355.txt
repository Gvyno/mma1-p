This recently built house in a rustic area offers a unique opportunity to live in a quiet and natural environment. The property has 5 large bedrooms, 3 of them en suite, providing privacy and comfort. The eat-in kitchen is spacious, ideal for family gatherings, and is complemented by a large living room that includes a modern gas fireplace, perfect for cold nights.

With a total of 4 bathrooms plus 1 toilet, the house is designed to accommodate a large family or to receive guests. The generous plot offers enough space to build a swimming pool and barbecue area, ideal for outdoor entertaining. The highlight of this house is the views of the surrounding vineyards, visible through large windows that provide abundant natural light. Additionally, there are many terraces around the bedrooms, providing private outdoor spaces to relax.

The house also has a large fully equipped laundry area, adding functionality and convenience. The large basement offers flexibility, as it can be transformed into a games area, additional bedrooms, or other uses, thanks to the presence of vents that provide natural light.

Finally, all rooms are equipped with air conditioning with hot and cold options, ensuring a comfortable environment throughout the year. This house combines modernity, space and an exceptional natural environment, making it an ideal residence for those seeking tranquility without giving up contemporary comforts.

Estepona is known for its beautiful beaches, charming old town and vibrant nightlife, making it a highly sought after tourist destination.
