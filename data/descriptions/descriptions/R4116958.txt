Now living by the sea is possible, Las Golondrinas is located at Torrenueva Playa Mijas Costa between Lakala Beach and Florida Mijas beach.
This amazing front line beach ground floor apartment with 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms is a unique opportunity. Gated community with 2 Private parking spaces and a storage.
The Kitchen is modern and open plan into the living/dining area making it sociable.
The apartment overlooks the communal pool and gardens with amazing sea/beach views and direct access to the beach.
Las Golondrinas Torrenueva Playa is vastly becoming one of the top tourist destinations on the Costa del Sol. A stretch of 2.5km of golden sandy beaches, rock pools for scuba diving and fishing as well as summer beach activities for all the family. There are plenty of "Chiringuitos" Beach bars as well as a local market on wednesdays and saturdays within walking distance.
