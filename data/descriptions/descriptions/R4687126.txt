There is also a separate (guest) toilet in the hallway next to the living room. The location is fantastic, close to all amenities, the beach and with easy access to the coastal road connecting Benalmadena with Malaga, Fuengirola, and Marbella. This property is ideal for anyone seeking a permanent residence, holiday home, or investment opportunity. Two garage spaces and a storage room are included in the purchase price.
