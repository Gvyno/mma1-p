Luxury contemporary style townhouse recently built in La Cala de Mijas with south orientation. Only a few minutes walk to the beach, golf, shops and restaurants. Views to the sea and mountains.
Distributed over three levels: on the main level there is a fully fitted American style kitchen, two bathrooms, two bedrooms with fitted wardrobes, a spacious living-dining room, and the main terrace with its small private pool.
On the middle floor are the third bedroom, also with fitted wardrobes and with direct access to the second terrace, the third complete bathroom and the master bedroom with a small dressing room, en suite bathroom and private terrace.
On the top level there is a solarium where you can enjoy beautiful views of the Mediterranean Sea. Included in the price are two parking spaces and a storage room.
Located within a luxurious complex with several swimming pools, one of them heated, and a fully equipped gymnasium.
