This two bedroom apartment is located just a few minutes walk to some of Marbella's finest sandy beaches, flanked with fantastic restaurants close by to include NOSSU, SIROKO, COPACABANA and BONO BEACH CLUB. The property also offers a tennis court, overground and covered parking space along with a storage unit located in the lower level. The property would benefit from some reformation work however beachside East Marbella walking distance to the beach and all amenities this property presents great value. The apartment would make a fantastic holiday let option with the bustling cosmopolitan city of Marbella is just a few minutes drive plus the main highway offering public transport into Marbella also.
