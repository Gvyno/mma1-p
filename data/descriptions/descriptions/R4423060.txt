Bright, sunny 2 bedroom 2 bathroom Elevated Ground Floor apartment in the quiet area of Altos de Calahonda.

 It is Southwest facing and has spectacular sea views to Gibraltar and Africa.


  The apartment consists of a fitted kitchen, large lounge-diner, two double bedrooms with fitted wardrobes, ensuite bathroom and separate shower room.  The lounge and main bedroom lead onto the spacious terrace which has glass curtains.  It is a 10 minute walk to bars and restaurants and a 5 minute drive to shops and the beach.  Situated in the upper part of Calahonda, it has easy access to the toll road to Marbella and Malaga.
