Beautifully presented 2 bed, 2.5 bath townhouse situated in a peaceful Spanish type pueblo at Mijas Golf, with the added luxury of being only 10 minutes drive from the coast (both Fuengirola & La Cala de Mijas can be reached within this time).  It has a generous south facing private terrace to the front of the property where you can enjoy all day sunshine and a quaint courtyard to the rear, accessed via the lounge and overlooked from the kitchen.  There are open views from the main terrace and from the master bedroom terrace above. The property has plenty of character, is tastefully decorated & furnished and has a modern kitchen and bathrooms so you could move in immediately.  On the ground floor you have the lounge/dining room, kitchen and WC, with access to both the front and rear terraces.  Upstairs is the master bedroom with ensuite shower room, bedroom 2, both with built in wardrobes and the guest shower room.  The property is within walking distance of the newly renovated La Zambra Hotel (previously the Byblos hotel).  Outside the property there is community parking.  Viewing is highly recommended.
