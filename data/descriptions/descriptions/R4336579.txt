This property is located in a privileged location in the heart of Malaga, in the historic centre. It has a size of approximately 131 square meters and is distributed in three bedrooms, two bathrooms (one of them en suite), and a spacious living room with open plan kitchen.

The property has been completely refurbished and boasts modern and high quality features. The windows are white lacquered PVC windows of the brand Kommerling, which provides good thermal and acoustic insulation. There is also pre-installation for air conditioning for extra comfort.

The walls of the house are smooth and the hydraulic floors have been restored, which gives it a touch of charm and authenticity. In addition, the beams have been treated with hardener and fungicide to prevent woodworm.

The kitchen has a beautiful worktop and the appliances belong to the Balay Cristal series, which are renowned for their quality and modern design. The bathroom taps are built-in and are from the Imex brand.

In addition, beautiful design wallpaper has been used in the bedrooms and bathrooms, which adds style and personality to these spaces.

In short, this charming home in the heart of Malaga offers a combination of modern and traditional elements, with a privileged location and a high quality renovation in every detail.
