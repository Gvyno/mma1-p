Welcome to this beautiful partly refurbished apartment, situated in Las Colinas de Marbella - Benahavis! 

As you walk in, you'll be welcomed by a bright and airy living area that leads to a big south-facing terrace boasting stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea, mountains and golf course.
The kitchen is fully equipped with high-end appliances and partially open to the lounge. 

The two bedrooms are a serene escape, each with its own bathroom, ample space for a comfortable king-size bed and storage. 

Las Colinas de Marbella is a secure and tranquil urbanization with a car park and outdoor swimming pool. 
This community is situated in an expanding district, only a short drive away from local facilities, golf courses and San Pedro Alcantara.
