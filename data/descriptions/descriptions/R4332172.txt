Los Naranjos Country club is an attractive and popular residential complex, well situated in the heart of Nueva Andalucia's Golf Valley. If you are a golf lover, it does not get much better than this as you are surrounded by the golf courses of Aloha Golf, Los Naranjos and Las Brisas. An excellent property for those looking for a holiday home or permanent residence in the Nueva Andalucia area. This location means you are close to everything you could need such as restaurants, supermarkets, beaches, cafes and international schools.

This is a wonderful townhouse with three bedrooms. As you enter, to the left you have a guest toilet and another door that leads to access to the garage. Straight ahead to the open plan kitchen and living room with is spacious and bright. This area has direct access to the wonderful sunny terrace with convenient stairs that leads down to the community gardens and swimming pool. 

Moving up the stairs to find two bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms. They are both a great size and there is a balcony. 

On the next level there is the third bedroom also a very big size with wardrobes and en-suite bathroom. From here there is access to a spacious terrace with views over the community gardens and pool. 

A fantastic property for sale in an ideal location. The development is designed in the style of a cosy Andalucian village with beautifully maintained gardens, three communal swimming pools (one is heated) and squash/tennis.
