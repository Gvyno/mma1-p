5 bedroom villa, situated in El Lagar - Alhaurín de la Torre. This exclusive villa inspired by a Bavarian palace, is perfect for families looking for a main residence or second home.

The property is located in Alhaurín de la Torre, 10 minutes from the beach, 15 minutes from the centre of Torremolinos and 20 minutes from the centre of Málaga. The property is very well connected with bus stops and the Málaga-Marbella motorway. The airport is only 10 minutes away.

This impressive villa consists of 5 bedrooms and 5 bathrooms, two of them en suite. On the upper floor there is a spacious living room with views, a huge kitchen and pantry. The lower floor has a separate entrance and has a living room with an open plan kitchen and wine cellar. It is located on a plot of more than 1000 square metres, has a swimming pool with retractable cover, barbecue area and covered parking for 2 vehicles. It has a stair lift and pre-installation for a lift. The whole house is perfectly air-conditioned with underfloor heating and central air conditioning and has solar thermal panels.
