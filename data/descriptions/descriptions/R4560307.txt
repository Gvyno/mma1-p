Townhouse in urb. La Cancela de la Quinta in San Pedro de Alcantara (Marbella) consists of first floor, large living room, kitchen and toilet. Upstairs with three bedrooms, two bathrooms, mezzanine with 50 m2 available and basement with garage and storage area. It has a terrace at the entrance and patio/terrace at the back. Air conditioning and solar panels for hot water. Communal areas with garden and large swimming pool.
