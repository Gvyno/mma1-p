This unique traditional townhouse is located in the charming town of Ojen, set back in the hills behind Marbella town. With its 3 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, and 2 en-suite bathrooms, this property offers spacious rooms and is perfect for a family looking for a comfortable home with character. The townhouse is built over 2 floors and also features a roof terrace with panoramic sea views.

Upon entering the property, you will be greeted by a cozy living room with a fireplace, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere. The open plan living dining area is perfect for entertaining guests and enjoying family meals.

The fully fitted rustic style kitchen is equipped with all the necessary appliances and plenty of storage space. The property also boasts a large garage that can accommodate up to 3 cars, as well as a spacious storage room.

One of the highlights of this townhouse is the breathtaking panoramic sea views that can be enjoyed from the roof terrace. This is the perfect spot to relax and unwind while taking in the stunning surroundings.

Located in the town of Ojen, this property is within walking distance to all amenities, including shops, schools, and restaurants. It is also conveniently close to transport links, making it easy to explore the surrounding areas.
