This property is set on the beautiful development of Finca Cortesin. Set just back from the beach, this property offers the most stunning views of the coastline, with a terrace that faces the swimming pool and then opens up towards the stunning sunrises and sunsets that the Mediterranean coast offers.

All of the 4 bedrooms are spacious and light, with fitted wardrobes and plenty of space. The bathrooms are all modern, spacious and well equipped.

The kitchen is modern and offers plenty of work and storage space. Off the kitchen you can find the dining area which is open plan and shares its space with the living room. From here you can access that large terrace overlooking the gardens and the swimming pool. With its large glass terrace windows it permits year round use and allows natural light to flood in.

A private underground garage gives 2 parking spaces, ensuring security. Next to the parking spaces there is also a substantially sized storage room.

This propertys proximity to the port, surrounding golf course, most notably Dona Julia, the beautiful beaches and shopping area make this apartment a must see 'hot' property!
