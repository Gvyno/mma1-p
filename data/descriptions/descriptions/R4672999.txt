Charming villa situated on a quiet dead end street in a very central position of Nueva Andalucia, within walking distance to supermarket, shops, restaurants, bus stop, and the beach.
On the ground floor the villa has a living room with fireplace and a separate dining room and both open up to a covered terrace leading out to an open terrace and the garden.
The garden is mature and there is room to build a pool if required. On the side of the open terrace is a jacuzzi.
On the rest of the ground floor is the kitchen, a bedroom and a bathroom.
On the first floor there are four bedrooms and two bathrooms. There is also a covered terrace with access from two of the bedrooms.
Lovely views of "La Concha" mountain.
The villa has hot and cold air-conditioning on the ground floor and there is outdoor parking.
A property that is great for permanent living or as a holiday home, due to it's central location.
Great potential for holiday rentals.
