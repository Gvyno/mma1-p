Semi-detached house in the urbanisation "La Villa" in La Alcaidesa. 
This property has 4 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms, distributed over two floors. It also has an outdoor space of almost 200m2. 

On the ground floor there is a large fully fitted wooden kitchen with utility room, a bedroom and a guest toilet. Its living-dining room is very spacious and bright thanks to its large window and very cosy in winter due to its fireplace. It has a spectacular outdoor garden where you can enjoy the good weather, ideal for sunbathing or barbecues with family and friends. It also has a charming porch at the entrance of the house.
The first floor contains the master bedroom with access to a terrace with incredible sea views and two double bedrooms. It has a bathroom with bathtub.
The house is equipped with air conditioning, heating and fitted wardrobes. In addition, you will have access to the communal swimming pool and your private parking space.

The urbanization "La Villa" is a residential complex of semi-detached and terraced houses in a privileged area, with a Mediterranean style architecture and a modern design. The development has a communal swimming pool, landscaped gardens and a privileged location, just 2 minutes from Alcaidesa beach and 5 minutes from Alcaidesa Golf and La Hacienda Links Golf Resort. It is also just 15 minutes from Sotogrande and Gibraltar.
