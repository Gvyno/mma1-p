Los Robles de Los Arqueros (Benahavis) properties have been built in the style of a Mediterranean village offering magnificent views over the newly-built 9-hole golf course which will soon be rebranded Puente Romano Golf with 18 holes.

All apartments at Los Robles have high-quality finishes including cream marble flooring, high-spec/luxury designer fitted kitchens, sizeable terraces, beautifully landscaped gardens, adult and children's separate swimming pools, underground garage and storerooms.

Located just minutes away from Marbella, Puerto Banus and the town of San Pedro de Alcantara.

Los Arqueros Golf & Country Club provides much more than just Golf. Consists of other great facilities such as tennis, paddle & squash courts, gym, bowling, driving range, pro shop, restaurant/bar and impressive members lounge areas. Los Arqueros also has a Shopping Village in construction. Never a dull moment in Los Arqueros, always somewhere nearby to keep you entertained!
