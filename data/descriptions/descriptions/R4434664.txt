This great 2 bed 2 bathroom ground floor apartment is situated in an urbanisation, which is one of the best in Puerto Banús. 

It has two swimming pools and extensive tropical garden areas with direct access to the golden sand beaches of Puerto Banús, 

It is gated and benefits from security and has an underground parking space. 

The entrance to this block has impressive double doors  which lead you to the ground floor apartments, there is a lift, although this is not necessary for this apartment, only necessary for the underground parking. 

The apartment benefits from 2 good sized terraces, one that is east facing  thus giving you morning sun, and the other which is west, giving you afternoon and early evening sun. 

The living room is open plan to the kitchen and one of the terraces leads off from this large living area. 

It has 2 large double bedrooms, one with an en suite shower room and the other with a separate shower room. 

The apartment has a  hot and cold air conditioning system.

There is one parking space and built in cupboard storage on the terrace. 

The development is ideally situated, opposite the famous Ocean Club and on the paseo of Puerto Banus. 

It is great for holidays or investment. 

It is very well priced and would benefit from updating, however is perfectly adequate.
