Welcome to this cosy townhouse in Nueva Andalucia, Marbella with lots of potential! Ideally located within walking distance to the Hard Rock Cafe hotel and Puerto Banus.
Upon entering the townhouse, it has an open concept design combining the living room, dining room and the fully fitted kitchen which is half open. The living room has large windows that flood the room with natural light, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere.
The master bedroom has fitted wardrobes for all your storage needs. The second bedroom is equally spacious and comfortable, with a shared bathroom.
One of the highlights of this townhouse is the option to convert the attic into a third bedroom, providing additional space for guests or a growing family. This versatile space can also be used as a home office, gym or media room, allowing you to customise your living arrangements to suit your lifestyle. There are plans for a complete reform, converting it into a 3-bedroom, 2-storey townhouse.
Outside, you will find a private terrace and a lovely garden with communal pool, perfect for enjoying the Mediterranean sun.
