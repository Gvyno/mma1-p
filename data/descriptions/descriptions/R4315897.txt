This six bedroom villa offers plenty of natural light, outdoor entertainment and chill-out areas, as well as a heated swimming pool. The ground floor consists of a spacious living-dining room with direct access to the garden and pool, kitchen, guest toilet, bedroom/office with bathroom en-suite, storage room and large garage. The upper floor has five bedrooms and four bathrooms, with access to the terraces. The roof terrace offers magnificent panoramic views of the golf course and the surrounding nature. This property is located in a quiet area, within walking distance to the beach, restaurants and all kinds of amenities.
