Beautiful 4 bedroom classical style villa with fantastic sea views located in Puerto del Almendros neighbourhood of Benahavis. A short 4 min drive to local amenities in Monte Halcones such as bars, restaurants and supermarkets. The neighbourhood is peaceful and tranquil which is perfect for long term living or to use as a holiday home. Upon entry to the villa you will see the spacious lounge to your left with high beamed ceilings. There is a lovely open fireplace which takes centre stage in this space. Adjoining this room is a lovely dining area and the kitchen. The kitchen leads off onto a wraparound terrace and out to the garden where the pool is located. On this level of the villa is a bedroom with lovely pool views and en-suite bathroom.  On the upper level of the villa are 3 more spacious bedrooms offering fabulous sea views. The basement level of the villa consists of a very spacious garage with space for 4 cars along with a storeroom and a fabulous games chill out room!
