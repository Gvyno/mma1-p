In the prestigious Rosamar Urbanization, next to the Iglesia del Rocío, a charming two-story penthouse stands out, standing out in a privileged location in San Pedro Alcántara. With an area of 165 m2, this residence offers panoramic mountain views with Northwest orientation. Security is guaranteed in this gated community, equipped with security cameras, lush gardens and a refreshing swimming pool.

The ground floor houses a cozy hall, two bedrooms with built-in wardrobes, a full bathroom and a toilet, as well as an open kitchen, a large living room and a relaxing terrace. The upper floor has two bedrooms, one of them with a dressing room and a bathroom with a hydromassage bathtub, next to a large terrace with a 10 m2 storage room that offers impressive mountain views. With amenities such as independent air conditioning on each floor and a spacious parking space, this property fuses elegance and functionality.
