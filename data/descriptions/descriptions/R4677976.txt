Location, locations!
     This ground floor apartment with 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms is situated in the sought after gated Urbanization of Calahonda Park. The beach is only a short walk away and a commercial center with lots of amenities, bars, restaurants and supermarkets right next to the complex. Urbanization has two swimming pools, and well maintained, maturated gardens.  There is a lot of parking space on the gated communal parking.

The apartment has two bathrooms, one with a bathtub and the other with a shower. The semi-open kitchen is renovated and fully equipped.
This apartment has very convenient location. No car needed.
