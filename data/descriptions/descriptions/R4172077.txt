Mediterranean villa with panoramic sea views in Marbella, Spain!

This large property is located on an elevated position in the urbanizacion Lomas de Pazuelo of Rio Real, which offers undisturbed views to the sea.

This unique house was built in a Mediterranean style, and consists of four levels, including a spacious basement of 150m2 with a lot of natural light and space to create an additional room.

When entering the house a spacious living room opens, further a separated dining room, with an access to the covered & uncovered terraces. The kitchen is fully fitted and equipped with Siemens appliances. The guest bathroom and 1 bedroom are also on the first level.

On the second floor, you have the amazing master bedroom with an en-suite bathroom, dressing room and access to the upper terrace, where stunning sea views open to you.

On the third level you have the other 2 bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms, and an additional living room.    

The garden is well-maintained and includes an infinity pool.

About the location:
Rio Real is a lovely residential community built around the Rio Real Golf Course and is situated in the outskirts of Marbella just a few minutes drive away from the center of Marbella and 30 minutes' drive from the International airport of Malaga.
