The guest bathroom boasts a sleek shower cubicle and fresh flooring, while both bedrooms are bathed in natural light and offer generous wardrobe space for storage. The family bathroom has been elegantly upgraded with a luxurious walk-in shower, adding to the apartment's allure and functionality. With its impeccable presentation and thoughtful enhancements, this property epitomizes sophisticated coastal living in the heart of Duquesa.
