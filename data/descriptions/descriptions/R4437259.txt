The main floor houses two well-appointed bedrooms, each boasting en-suite bathrooms for the utmost convenience. The upper floor serves as a private retreat, presenting a master suite complete with an oversized bathroom, an open terrace, and a versatile additional room suitable as an office or extra dressing area, complete with wardrobes.

The lower floor, directly accessible from the street and the garage, features a large living space with a games area and a secondary TV room with a bar. This floor also hosts a sizable bedroom suite and a separate storage and laundry area. Some of the features include include central heating, air conditioning, and electric shutters throughout the villa.

Accessibility is managed with two separate entrances— the upper leading to a car parking area and the lower providing direct access to the garage.
Very attractively priced to the market, this villa represents a superb blend of luxury, convenience, and design, making it a standout in the Marbella property market.
