Welcome to this homely apartment only 10 minutes walk to El Faro beach.

When you enter the apartment you are welcomed by the bright living room which is tastefully decorated, followed by the fantastic terrace where you can enjoy the sun, wonderful meals with the beautiful open view. The kitchen is located next to the living room and is fully equipped with new modern appliances. The home has two bedrooms with built-in wardrobes and two bathrooms, one with shower and one with bathtub. AC is available separately in each room so you can easily adjust the temperature all year round. The furniture is included in the purchase of the home.

The association's pool is located close to the residence, where you can cool off with a swim and relax all day. The surroundings are beautiful and green and here you can hike in the lovely natural environment. The fantastic location means that you can easily move around the area without having to take the car to everything as there are restaurants and shops nearby. It is also easy to get to both Fuengriola center and Marbella with the bus, which is 10 minutes walk away. Not to mention the shortcut to the beach, in just 10 minutes walk you are at one of the Mediterranean's lovely beaches with nice restaurants.
