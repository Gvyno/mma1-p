This cosy and beautifully maintained 3 bed duplex penthouse  in Los Naranjos de Marbella is the perfect property for someone who appreciates its location close to all amenities and walking distance to Puerto Banus.  

The property is very bright due to its south orientation. 
Upon entering the property you step into a spacious living room with working fire place and access to the terrace. The separate kitchen is fully equipped, and has a small laundry terrace. There are two bedrooms on the same level that share a full bathroom.

The upper level level is fully occupied by the master suite with acces to a solarium offering pleasant views of the pool, communal gardens and La concha mountain. 

This property would make an ideal family home or second residence, as well as a great investment due to is high rental potential.
