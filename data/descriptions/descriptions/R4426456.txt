Whether for a home, holiday home, rental investment ....... it is a fantastic opportunity and really is a must see.

The balcony has been closed inn that allows use all year round. Off the living room you have a guest WC, laundry room and a good size kitchen. Up stairs consists of a large bedroom with fitted wardrobes, bathroom and an even larger master bedroom also with fitted wardrobes, an ensuite and balcony.
