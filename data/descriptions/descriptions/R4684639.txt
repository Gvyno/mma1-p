This property has a great location for a holiday home or for living.It is located in a beautiful Andalusian urbanization in Nueva Andaluzia, just 1.5 km from the port of Puerto Banus in Marbella.

The apartment is suitable for clients who do not mind stairs. The property is spread over three floors, each with its own private terrace. From the beautiful wooden atypical door you enter direct into the living room with a fully equipped kitchen and bar counter and an open cute  terrace  while enjoying your morning coffee. Stairs (15 steps) lead to a bedroom with a work area  and  small balcony and an en-suite bathroom with a window to gardens. 
The solarium can be reached by stairs from this floor. The large terrace offers sunbeds, seating for 4 people and a view of the Puerto Banus  and the community garden and pool. On hot days, solarium includes a shower  and also a utility room.

The whole apartment is very unusual and its uniqueness  bring  the right feeling of enjoying  on the Costa del Sol. Alocating  parking  is visible from all windows. just  few steps from apartment. Within the  combination with a few minutes  to Puerto Banus is  a gexcelent choice !
