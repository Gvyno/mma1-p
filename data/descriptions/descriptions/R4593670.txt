A beautiful family home located in a small urbanisation of only 36 houses, set in lush and tropical gardens with spectacular sea views. This end terrace house has off street parking to the front of the property and is distributed over several levels. Upon entering the property you have a cloakroom and stairs that lead down to a newly fitted kitchen and large lounge diner area with fireplace. From this floor you have a nice sized terrace with stunning sea views. If you go down the stairs again you are presented with a practical laundry room and two further bedrooms plus bathroom. Upstairs you have the three main bedrooms on the same level with the master having en-suite and the other two sharing a family bathroom, with the master bedroom enjoying sea views from its Juliette balcony. Up a further level and you are presented with a 40m2 roof terrace with incredible sea views and your very own private bar. The community is immaculately kept and has a communal pool and gym.
