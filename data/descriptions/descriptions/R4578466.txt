The compound is located in a well serviced area in Benalmadena, close to all amenities and only 15 minutes to Malaga airport. The apartment is on the second floor and offers beautiful views of the golf course and mountains. It features a lounge and dining area with access to the east facing balcony where you can enjoy the scenery. The apartment has two bedrooms with built-in wardrobes and two bathrooms, one of which is en-suite. The kitchen is fully equipped and includes a laundry room. The apartment is sold furnished and comes with two underground parking spaces and a storage room. The compound has beautiful outdoor communal areas and a swimming pool.
