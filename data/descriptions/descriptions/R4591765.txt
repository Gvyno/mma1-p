It has a large living-dining room, a fully equipped and semi-closed kitchen, two large bedrooms, the main one with an en-suite bathroom and both with large capacity built-in wardrobes and windows with beautiful views. The second bedroom is right next to the second bathroom. Includes a private parking space and storage room in the community garage.
