Beautiful townhouse in the beach area of San Pedro de Alcantara. On the ground floor we find the entrance hall, living dining room, kitchen, laundry room and a toilet. On the first floor there are 3 bedrooms and two bathrooms. The property also has a basement and garage. Unbeatable location, close to the village, the beach, the promenade and surrounded by all kinds of services and facilities. Ideal for families. Undoubtedly a property worth visiting.
