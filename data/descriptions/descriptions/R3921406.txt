Huge villa of more than 700m2 next to the exclusive area of Retamar and El Higueron in Benalmadena. The house is built on a big plot of 10 000m2 where you enjoy privacy, amazing views to the sea and tranquility. The house is accessed from the top of the plot through a closed gate. We drive down and park either the side of the house or the front porch. The house has a big and well-maintained garden and an extra area where the owner houses a horse. The house is built on 2 levels with living room, social areas, kitchen and 2 double bedrooms en-suite on the first floor. We also find a movie room, a spa with jacuzzi and hammam sauna. From the first floor we also have direct access to the infinity pool facing the ocean. On the upper floor we have 2 big bedrooms with good size bathrooms and their individual terraces facing the sea. 
Under the main house we also have a separated apartment of 2 bedrooms and kitchen, perfect for renting or visiting guests.
