Spacious and bright South-East facing apartment situated in the attractive Urbanization Puente II of Calahonda which has fantastic panoramic sea views. The apartment consists of 3 large bedrooms and 3 bathrooms (2 en suite). It has a nice comfortable layout with a big cloak cupboard in the entrance. The kitchen and the bathrooms have been renovated. The 2 front sliding doors and the awning on the terrace are new. The living/dining room with built-in fireplace leads out to the good-sized covered terrace which also has an enclosed storage space. The kitchen is fully fitted and has a laundry room. The apartment has integrated hot and cold air conditioning.

This quiet gated urbanization offers 2 communal parking areas, elevator and a big communal swimming-pool and gardens. Crossing the street you will find a nice selection of bars and restaurants including the popular Miel y Nata. There is also a bus stop 5 minutes walk away from the apartment that will take you to the bottom of Calahonda and to the Miramar Commercial Centre in Fuengirola.
