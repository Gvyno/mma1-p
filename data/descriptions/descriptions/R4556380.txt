A fantastic opportunity for a charming Puerto de la Duquesa apartment in the heart of La Duquesa. An ideal pied-a-terre for marine side living or vacations.

La Duquesa is a picturesque marina with a large selection of bars and restaurants.  The marina also offers a wide variety of land and sea based activities including several championship golf courses, miles of unspoiled beaches and the Mediterranean Sea.  Beautiful seaside walks along the promenade on either side side of Duquesa lead to the traditional villages of Sabinillas and Castillo. Both have all amenities including a wide selection of tapas bars and restaurants.

Excellent link roads lead to the seaside towns of Estepona, Sotogrande and Marbella with Malaga International airport less than 1 hour away.

This top floor Duquesa apartment is located in a well maintained, low-rise complex with swimming pool and sun bathing terrace.  The complex has security gated entrances whilst this apartment also includes a much-valued, private underground parking space.

The block is served by lifts and stairs with attractive Andalucian styled foyers and landings.

The property itself comprises of a large bedroom with fitted wardrobes, bathroom, a fully fitted kitchen with a utility area and a living room.  The living room opens out to a sunny south west facing terrace, that catches all day sunshine and from here there is also a view to the sea.  In addition, the apartment has air conditioning, marble floors and window blinds.

This is a charming Puerto de la Duquesa apartment in a fine, central location and only a 2 minute walk to the beach and marina. A lovely holiday home with excellent rental potential. Viewing is highly recommended.
