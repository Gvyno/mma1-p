A lovely, quaint 2 bed, 1 bath ground floor apartment in the established beach side community of Benamara Pueblo.  The property consists of a small lounge with open plan galley kitchen, 2 double bedrooms and a bathroom containing a vanity unit, WC and walk in shower.  Outside, the property has a large South West facing terrace, part of which is covered and similar properties in the community have enclosed this space to create more living space or storage.  The expansive communal gardens, under shady pine trees and boasts one of the largest swimming pools in the area and located very close to lots of local amenities, including, shops, bars and restaurants and the beach is only around 5 mins walk.  

Benamara Pueblo is very conveniently located on the New Golden Mile for easy access to Marbella and the world famous Puerto Banus approx. 10-15 mins drive to the East and the "garden of Andalucia" Estepona a similar distance to the West.  There are also bus stops either side of the road, with the West bound one heading towards Estepona easily accessible via a nearby footbridge.  The Benavista commercial centre is also accessible via the footbridge, where there are more shops, bars, restaurants, bowls club, pharmacy and banks.
