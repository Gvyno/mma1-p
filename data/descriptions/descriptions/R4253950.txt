Immaculate 2 bedroom 2 bathroom apartment with open views at Mijas Golf

This is a very spacious and well presented 2 bedroom, 2 bathroom apartment located at Single Homes on the Mijas Golf Estate.  The community is gated with a choice of 3 swimming pools and is a brilliant base for your holiday or full time home.  From here you have the golf course on your door step with nearby local shopping and restaurant facilities close by.   The Miramar Shopping Centre and all the amenities of Fuengirola are just a short car ride away.  The beaches beach bars and the restaurants of the Costa del Sol are all within easy reach.

The community is gated with well maintained gardens and a choice of 3 swimming pools.

This is a second floor apartment with a south westerly aspect and has lift access from the parking garage where a parking space and store room are included in the sale.  

The layout is very spacious and includes an entrance hallway which opens into a combined sitting and dining room which opens directly to the terrace which has space for relaxing and al fresco dining and open views.   There is a fitted kitchen with granite work tops and a range of quality appliances including an oven, hob, dishwasher and fridge/freezer.  There is a separate utility room.

The main bedroom has direct access to the terrace, it has fitted wardrobes and there is an en suite bathroom.    The second bedroom also has wardrobes and has the use of the adjacent family bathroom.

Hot and cold air conditioning is installed and combines with double glazing in hardwood effect quality UPVC windows.
