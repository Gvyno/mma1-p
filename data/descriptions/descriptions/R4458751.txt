Fantastic inland opportunity!

Lastly, the location of this fantastic property allows for privacy, seclusion and tranquility while only being a 10 minute drive from amenities such as restaurants and shops, as well as under an hour drive to the coast and Málaga city. 
This property truly is an ideal opportunity as a full-time or holiday home in the heart of the Andalusian countryside.
