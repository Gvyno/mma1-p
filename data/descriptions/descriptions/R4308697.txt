This pretty finca in the hills of Casarabonela has lots of potential.

Property Details:

You immediately feel the tranquility of this property surrounded by olive and almond groves you can not fail to be wowed by the beauty of the location.  The owners mainly live in the top part of the property,  2 sets of french windows give the lounge a light and airy feel the windows framing those lovely views.  From the lounge you walk directly to the conservatory which is currently being used as dining room and then to the patios and bbq area.  The outdoor space here is very lovely with plenty of room to install an above ground temporary pool or jacuzzi should the new owners wish to do so.   The kitchen and master bedroom with ensuite bathroom are also on this floor as is a cloakroom and utility room.

Downstairs you will find 2 more bedrooms and a guest bathroom along with two storage rooms.  There is also garage to the side of the property.
