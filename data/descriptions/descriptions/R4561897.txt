Discover the charm and convenience of this delightful 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom apartment, nestled in the lively heart of Las Lagunas. Surrounded by a plethora of amenities, including the bustling Carrefour, quaint shops, and diverse restaurants, this residence offers a blend of urban vitality and homely comfort.

Upon entering, a welcoming hallway invites you into a space where every corner tells a story. To the right, the kitchen unfolds, spacious and well-equipped, a perfect setting for both everyday meals and culinary adventures. To your left, the living area, warm and inviting, extends to a lovely terrace. This outdoor haven is a serene spot to unwind, offering a glimpse of the vibrant life below.

The journey through the apartment continues down the hallway to the private quarters. The master bedroom is a cozy retreat, complete with an en-suite bathroom and direct terrace access, marrying comfort with a touch of outdoor tranquility. The second bedroom, serviced by a separate bathroom, offers a peaceful escape, ensuring comfort and privacy for all who reside here.

Beyond the confines of the apartment, the community amenities add another layer of enjoyment. A communal swimming pool provides a refreshing escape on warm days, and the convenience of a private garage is a welcome addition for urban living.

This apartment is more than just a place to live; it's a vibrant urban sanctuary right in the middle of everything. Perfect for those seeking a balanced lifestyle, it combines the buzz of city life with the comforts of home in a wonderfully harmonious way.
