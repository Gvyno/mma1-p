Beautiful 3 bedroom ground floor apartment for sale in Serenity Views, Estepona West.

The open concept kitchen and living areas are bright, and they connect with the outside terraces through large floor-to-ceiling windows. The apartment has a very generous size terrace with south orientation and magnificent open views towards the shared gardens and pool. From here you also see a glimpse of the sea. The apartment has 3 bedrooms, the master bedroom with en-suite bathroom and lots of wardrobe space. The other 2 bedrooms are also of good size and enjoys a bathroom nearby.

The apartment also comes with a private parking space and a storage room in the price.

Serenity Views offers comfortable communal areas, including two outdoor pools, and landscaped gardens. It is fully gated for privacy and security.

Serenity Views enjoys a perfect location at only 700 m to the nearest beach. Estepona and Duquesa's port are at a short distance by car. There are several golf courses to choose from, including the renowned Finca Cortesin Golf Club. It is very well connected to Malaga and Gibraltar's international airports.
