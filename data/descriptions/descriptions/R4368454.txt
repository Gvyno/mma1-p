This 125 m2 house in the center of the town of Gaucin is decorated in a rustic style.

At the entrance of the house is the living room with fireplace, the dining area and the kitchen, at the end of this room there is a room with a bathroom and another independent access to the street.

On the second floor there is a large bedroom with high ceilings, a bathroom.

Located near the two most important squares in the town and with its rustic character, it is a property with a lot of potential.
