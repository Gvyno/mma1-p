Exclusive Beachfront Penthouse in La Noria III

Located in the prestigious La Noria III urbanization, this spectacular beachfront penthouse offers panoramic views of the sea and mountains. Its brightness and modern design make it a gem, ready to live in or to be rented out.

This penthouse is a perfect combination of luxury, comfort, and strategic location. Ideal for those looking for an exclusive home on the Costa del Sol.
