Bright 3 bedroom penthouse in Los Pacos, Fuengirola

This lovely 3 bedroom, 2 bathroom penthouse is located in Los Pacos, 1.4 km from the sandy beaches and walking distance from Los Pacos padel courts. You can enjoy the sun all day on two terraces, one facing west and the other facing the morning sun. The renovated kitchen has a fridge/freezer, dishwasher, microwave,drinking water from the tap, oven and stove and extractor hood. The bathrooms have been completely renovated. All rooms have air heat pumps. The community has a beautiful pool area surrounded by a beautifully maintained garden. The building has an elevator and a parking space in the hall, as well as free parking in the community's public parking lot. The apartment has an LPO and a tourist license.
