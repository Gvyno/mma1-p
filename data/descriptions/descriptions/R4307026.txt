Fantastic top-floor apartment with a great terrace and beautiful views.

This spacious home is located in El Casar, one of the most sought-after communities in Benahavís village, where you can enjoy its fabulous facilities which are three outdoor swimming pools, an indoor heated pool, a GYM and a small sauna.
This desired complex is located a short walk away from the centre of Benahavís and all its bars, restaurants and amenities.

The apartment consists of an entrance next to an American kitchen that opens to a spacious living/dining room that has access to a large terrace where you can enjoy views of the mountains in Benahavís.
The comfortable bedroom has another access to the terrace and nice views as well.

This home comes with a car park space and storage room on the underground level.
