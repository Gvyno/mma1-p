Estrella de la Bahia en Casares Costa is a beautifull urbanisation well mantained with pool and children play ground. Setted up the hill you have nice sea views from the main terrace south-facing and sunshine all day long. In front of the 22sqm terrace is a small garden and a private parking space for one car. At the north side from the apartment you have the main entrance with small terrace and 30 sqm private garden. Walking distance to Casares beach is 950 meters with the famous beach restaurant La Sal.  Along the beach promenade you can easy walk to Sabinillas, La Duquesa Port or Estepona Shopping areas are very close and Finca Cortesin Golf Resort is just 2 km away. In 300 m distance is a beautiful park with play areas and football playground.  If you are not happy with your private parking place in front of your apartment you can buy underground parking in the urbanisation. Perfect for first home, second home or holiday rentals as investment. The apartment has tourist licence for short term rentals.
