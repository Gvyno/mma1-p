Opportunity for golf lovers!

LOCATION
This first floor apartment is located practically on the golf course in the beautiful Lauro Golf urbanisation in Alhaurin de la Torre. It's situated just a 10 minute drive to Alhaurin de la Torre and a 20 minute drive to the beach and Malaga airport.

APARTMENT
The apartment consists of 1 bedroom, 1 bathroom, an open plan kitchen and a living room with access to a terrace with views of the surrounding pine trees and golf course. There is plenty of parking in front of the building. The apartment can be reached with a lift, has fiber optic internet and has air conditioning in the living room. 

FACILITIES
The apartment enjoys the facilities of Lauro Golf, with its 27-hole golf course, restaurant, paddle / tennis courts, lawn bowling, golf school and lovely communal gardens and swimming pool. It could use an update but would be an ideal holiday home for golf lovers.
