A beautiful detached finca with olive plot located within ten minute's drive from the vibrant town of Alhaurin El Grande.

You enter the property through gates up a driveway to the house which has ample parking.

The entrance benefits from a wide covered terrace. Enter the house from here into a large living room with a feature fireplace and double height vaulted ceiling. The ground floor has a large modern fully fitted kitchen with a window archway to the dining area, a guest toilet, alcove office and staircase leading to the mezzanine floor with its master bedrooom, ensuite bathroom and walking wardrobe. 

The basement area has a further 3 bedrooms, one en-suite and another family bathroom.

The finca has been built to a high standard; features include double glazing, solar water heating, a feature fireplace and fitted wardrobes. It has town water and its own well and the property has all the documentation in order including the AFO licence.
