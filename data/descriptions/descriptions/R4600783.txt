***Close to the beaches of Casares*** Los Cortijos de la Bahia; Casares Costa is a quiet, gated community of townhouses, walking distance to the beach and not far from the local towns of Estepona, Sabinillas and Duquesa Marina. This is a spacious 3 bedroom townhouse situated in an ideal location within the development, close to the parking area and swimming pool. The Property is furnished and with air conditioning installed. The accommodation is over 3 floors: A living and dining area leads to the kitchen on the ground floor, the first floor consists of 2 double bedrooms and a family bathroom and the top floor has the master bedroom, en-suite bathroom and access to the roof solarium terrace. This is a great property for permanent or holiday living.
