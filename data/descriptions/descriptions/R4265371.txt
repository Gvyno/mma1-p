Top-floor apartment in Cerros del Águila

This beautiful apartment in Cerros del Aguila is located in a very quiet area. The apartment is spacious, with two bedrooms and two bathrooms. It has a fully fitted modern kitchen with an outdoor utility room.
The living room leads to a terrace, from where you can enjoy the views of the mountains and the golf course.
Furthermore, the apartment has a solarium on the upper floor of approximately 60 m2. 
The apartment has a private parking space.


Cerros del Águila is an area in Mijas Costa, a 10-minute drive from the beach. The urbanisation is quiet, with Spanish residents and newcomers of other nationalities. 
The area has a small supermarket, a few restaurants, and a large common area consisting of a playground, tennis court, and a swimming pool with a children's pool. The tennis court and playground are available all year round. 

According to the plan, a more extensive park called Gran Parque de Mijas will be built in 2025. It is going to be the biggest park in Málaga province.
