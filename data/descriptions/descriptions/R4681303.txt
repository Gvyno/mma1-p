This spacious 3 bedroom semi-detached house in Mijas Golf is bright and airy and full of character. The property, built in 1985, consists of entrance hall, fully fitted kitchen and utility room, lounge with feature fireplace and separate split level dining area, and south facing secluded terrace. On the first floor is a double bedroom with fitted wardrobes, second floor has a second double bedroom with stunning golf and mountain views and a modern shower room. The third level has a very private terrace and the top floor the master bedroom suite with en-suite bathroom and stunning views. This typically Andalusian property has an atrium running through the centre keeping the property cool throughout the summer months. The community has a large communal pool, well maintained gardens and community parking. This desirable location on the edge of two excellent golf courses is only 10 minutes to La Cala, 20 minutes to Marbella and 30 minutes to Malaga.
