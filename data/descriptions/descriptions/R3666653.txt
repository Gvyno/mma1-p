This southfacing 2 bedroom apartment is situated in the prestigous Los Arqueros Golf and Country Club complex. The apartment consists of 2 bedroom, 2 bathrooms, dining- living area, kitchen and a good size south facing terrace with views to the golf and sea. There is an underground parking included as well as a storage room. The comunity has nice gardens and a 2 swimming pools. Los Arqueros is a perfect location in the country side, at the golf and at only a few minutes to San Pedro and the beach.
