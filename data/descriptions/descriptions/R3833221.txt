Have you ever dreamed of living in the countryside and wherever the eye can see only mountains and vegetation? If you are one of those who need to live in full contact with Nature and love to grow your own fruits and vegetables, without a doubt, this is the property you are looking for.

This construction can be converted into a one-bedroom house with everything you need to enjoy the quiet life in the country.

It has electricity from the network and its own well.

If you want to live in the tranquility of the area and one step away from all the amenities that La Cala de Mijas offers, this is your place.
