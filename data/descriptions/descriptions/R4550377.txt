GREAT VILLA ON THE FIRST LINE OF GOLF
Magnificent semi-detached villa, located on the first line of golf in the exclusive area of Guadalmina, a few minutes from the shopping center, the clubhouse, San Pedro and all kinds of services.
The property stands out for its large size, distributed over four floors, it has a total of five bedrooms and four bathrooms.
The main floor has a large living room with fireplace, with direct access to the porch and garden area, it also has a large independent kitchen.
On the first floor the house offers four large bedrooms with three full bathrooms as well as terraces.
The upper floor has a fantastic solarium with beautiful views of the golf, the sea and the mountains.
The property has a basement where there is a bedroom with a bathroom, a laundry area and a large garage for several cars.
Externally, the property has a private pool.
An ideal property for clients who want to enjoy a large house with spacious rooms with an excellent location in one of the most sought-after areas of Marbella.
