Welcome to Capanes del Golf! This stylish 2-bedroom luxury apartment is located in one of the most exclusive complexes on the Costa del Sol, an oasis that offers incredible views of the El Higueral golf course (owned by the Marbella Club) and the surrounding mountains. Situated within a gated community amidst stunning, well-kept landscaped gardens, the complex offers five outdoor pools and a central wellness area with an indoor spa pool, sauna and gym.

With its modern and contemporary design, this apartment features a fully fitted kitchen with laundry facilities and a bright and spacious living and dining room perfect for relaxing. The large, sunny terrace offers panoramic views towards Benahavis and is the perfect spot for outdoor dining and enjoying the sun. 

The generously sized master bedroom which opens onto the terrace, has its own luxurious ensuite bathroom offering a shower and bath, while the second bedroom, perfect for guests or family, is adjacent to the second, well-equipped bathroom. This apartment also boasts an underground garage space and a good-sized storage room, providing extra space and convenience.

The gated nature of this complex ensures a safe and tranquil environment as well as access to the wide range of on-site top-notch amenities. The communal pools and lush, tropical gardens are the perfect places to unwind and enjoy the fantastic weather year-round. The exceptional location in the Golf Valley ensures you are near golden sandy beaches, gourmet restaurants, and exclusive shops and boutiques.

This apartment is more than just a home, it's an "extraordinary living experience" an opportunity to live the dream lifestyle that's not to be missed!
