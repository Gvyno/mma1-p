Low floor located in the center of Estepona, near the soccer field, a step away from shops (Aldi, Mercadona), cafes, bus stop, 15 minutes from the beach in a quiet neighborhood.
The house has 3 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, a very spacious living room and kitchen and a laundry room. Its total size is 90 m2.
It is ideal for the elderly too, they do not have to climb stairs and it has access for people with reduced mobility.
There is no reserved parking space that belongs to the property, but being in a quiet residential area, there is always a space close by.
