Located in the popular and newer style building of El Mero, beachside of the A7 in San Luis de Sabinillas, lays this second floor key ready apartment. Finished to a high standard throughout the property hosts a large semi open planned kitchen upon entrance, with quality appliances and an exterior patio with laundry facilities. The living room is spacious enough for both living and dining and offers access out onto the well sized covered terrace.

There are two double bedrooms, the master of which offers an en-suite bathroom and access out onto the terrace.

There is centralized air conditioning throughout the property and an intercom system allowing easy access for guests.
