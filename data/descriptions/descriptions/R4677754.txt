Semi-detached house for sale in El Tomillar de Nagüeles
Great corner townhouse with 4 bedrooms and 4 en-suite bathrooms, located in the prestigious El Tomillar de Nagüeles urbanization, Marbella's Golden Mile. Located a few meters from the Royal Palace of 'El Batatal', residence of the royal house of Abu Dhabi in Marbella, and right at the foot of Sierra Blanca. Very bright, private and with fantastic views.

Very quiet residential area surrounded by greenery, just 5 minutes by car from the center of Marbella. The complex has a beautiful communal pool, changing rooms and bathrooms for the owners' use, a sauna and a large gym.

It consists of 3 floors plus a semi-basement floor. On the ground floor we find a large entrance, a guest toilet, an independent kitchen equipped with a separate laundry room, a large living room with two areas, a dining area and access to the porch with a garden area.

On the first floor, there are 3 exterior double bedrooms en suite with built-in wardrobes and terraces with sea views.

On the second floor, there is an exterior double bedroom en suite with a built-in wardrobe and terrace with sea views.

Finally, the semi-basement has a large multidisciplinary space that could be another bedroom, games room, library, wine cellar, etc. Here is also access to the garage for 3 spaces.
