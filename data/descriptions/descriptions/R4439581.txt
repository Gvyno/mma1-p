Close by you can find many outstanding golf courses here in Benahavis, including La Quinta, La Zagaleta, Los Arqueros Golf, Guadalmina and Los Flamingos.

Only a few minutes drive you find the village of Benahavis occupying privileged position surrounded by peaks on the foothills of Serranía de Ronda and only a few KM´s from Marbella and Estepona, who their Municipality border with, make Benahavis an authentic artistic and touristic treasure. 

Benahavis is a simply delightful mountain village overlooking the Guadalmina River and according to the BBC Holiday Programme 'as close to paradise as you can get ! One of Andalucía's best kept secrets Benahavis is just 7 km into the mountains from the main coast road, just west of San Pedro de Alcántara and only a few minutes from Marbella and Puerto Banus. The village itself is a delightful pueblo, with narrow cobbled streets, that still keep the original Moorish layout. There are pretty houses, some shops and a simply amazing choice of restaurants. It is known locally as the 'dining room' of the Costa del Sol.
