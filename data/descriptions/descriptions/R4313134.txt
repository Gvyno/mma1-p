Luxurious villa with excellent qualities and wonderful views of the sea. Located in one of the best villa areas in western Estepona, in a well-kept and very quiet urbanization. The villa consists of 4 large bedrooms, all with en-suite bathrooms (the master bedroom has a dressing room), 4 bathrooms, a fully equipped kitchen, an ironing room and a 30 m2 basement. Large terrace with wide views of the sea and the mountains.
Private garage for 2 cars.
