This modern 2 bedroom + 2 bathroom apartment has a spacious layout of living room and open kitchen, with an adjacent spacious terrace. The bedrooms have unsite modern finishes.
Via the stairs you go to the roof terrace, where you have a beautiful view of the sea - Gibraltar - the coast of Morocco. Here you can enjoy the sun all day long and use the jacuzzi.
The complex has a nice communal swimming pool with plenty of space for sunbathing.
Location is a few kilometers from the beach, Estepona 5 km, Malaga airport 85 km.
