Los Naranjos de Marbella is a popular residential complex within walking distance of Puerto Banus and the popular Aloha College, as well as a Mercadona supermarket and several cafes.

This is a two bedroom penthouse with a roof solarium which benefits from all day sun and has wonderful views of La Concha.

The apartment itself is access via a staircase and is situated in the middle of the development, with the nearest swimming pool just a one minute walk away.

The accommodation is all on one level, with stairs to the first floor roof terrace.

The accommodation is as follows :

Lounge / dining room
Kitchen with separate utility room
Two double bedrooms
One bathroom
One shower room / guest cloakroom

The gardens are very well maintained and there are numerous communal swimming pools.

This is an ideal second home or holiday apartment with the potential to generate significant income. Alternatively, it could be made into a very nice permanent residence.

The apartment has been priced accordingly, taking into account that it could benefit from updating and redecoration.
