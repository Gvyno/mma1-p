Enjoying an elevated location in Buenavista, this villa boasts fantastic sea views. The contemporary design of the villa features bright and open living spaces, seamlessly connecting the indoor and outdoor areas. The ground floor comprises the living and dining area with an open plan, fully fitted kitchen. Floor-to-ceiling windows lead to the terrace with an impressive saltwater pool and a chill out-area at the lower part of the plot. There is also one bedroom, a bathroom and a laundry room on this floor. The upper floor comprises the main bedroom with en-suite bathroom and a further bedroom and bathroom. Owners will be able to customise the additional space on the lower floor, which can be converted into a gym or a cinema room. The modern villa will be built to the highest specifications, the buyer will be able to chose from a range of tiles, kitchen worktops and other details to customise the interior. 

Completion expected quarter 3/2025
