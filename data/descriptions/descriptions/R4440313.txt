Two bedroom family home located on a popular complex. 

This townhouse has been renovated on the ground floor to create an open plan family friendly space. The extended kitchen features an attractive granite breakfast bar and plenty of storage with space for a dining table and chairs. The living room has a working fireplace and leads out to a covered terrace and private west facing garden. There is also a cloakroom/guest toilet on this floor of the house. 

Upstairs the very large bedrooms are serviced by two full bathrooms. There is a private balcony leading off the master bedroom overlooking the garden and Mediterranean beyond. The second bedroom has pretty views over the community courtyard.

Located in a prime residential and golfing area, this property is two minutes drive or 15 minutes walk from all the shops and restaurants in Benavista. The beach is a further five to seven minute walk. Estepona, San Pedro, Puerto Banus and Benahavis are all accessible within 15 minutes by car and the two closest airports of Malaga and Gibraltar can be reached within an hour's car journey.

The beautiful complex has two large swimming pools and picturesque stream. Private underground parking spaces are available separately if required.
