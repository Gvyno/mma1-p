Torrequebrada (District Benalmádena Costa. Benalmádena)
Urbanization and residential area located next to the sea, with chalet-type homes and low-rise apartments. There are also several hotels in the area. It has all kinds of equipment and even has the International School. Much of its extension is occupied by a golf club. Its beaches include Las Yucas, Torrevigía, La Viborilla and Torrequebrada. Its main roads are the Carretera de Cádiz, Ronda Golf Este street and Las Palmeras avenue. The Hondo stream and the Pantano stream cross through this area. The luxurious casino located in the Hotel Torrequebrada stands out.
