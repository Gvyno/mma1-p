Superb south facing three bedroom apartment in the well-known and gated community Marbella Real. This prestigious community is in the heart of the Golden Mile with well-kept tropical gardens, a children's playground, five communal swimming pools, gymnasium, sauna and paddle courts. The property is just a short walk to some of the best beaches in Marbella, restaurants, shops and all leisure amenities. The property has a spacious living and dining area with access to a private terrace; a fully equipped kitchen; master bedroom with en suite bathroom and terrace overlooking the pool; two guest bedrooms; and a guest bathroom. There is also a private solarium with independent access from the 3rd floor of the block with fantastic sea views. Other features include fitted wardrobes, hot and cold air conditioning, a private parking space and a storeroom.
