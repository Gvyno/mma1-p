Apartment in Avenida de España, Estepona 

This spacious apartment on Avenida España is a gem on the beachfront, with direct access to the pedestrian extension of Estepona's promenade. It offers three spacious bedrooms, a complete bathroom, an elegant entrance hall, and a fully renovated and equipped kitchen. The spacious living room connects to a fully glazed terrace/balcony with views to the majestic Sierra Bermeja mountain range. In addition, two of the bedrooms have their own elongated balcony, also fully glazed. 

All the bedrooms are bright and have built-in wardrobes, and are connected by a short hallway, maximizing usable space and minimizing unnecessary corridors. The lounge terrace is spacious enough to accommodate numerous guests, making it the perfect place to enjoy al fresco dining with stunning views. 

The rear balcony of the bedrooms offers partial beach views and overlooks a quiet street perpendicular to the coast. The building has a lift and a consolidated and friendly community of neighbours, which adds extra value to this property. Furthermore, the location of the building, next to the charming neighbourhood of low houses of the Virgen del Carmen hermitage, guarantees quiet and charming summer evenings. 

This is the ideal place to enjoy coastal living with all the comforts, surrounded by natural beauty and a welcoming atmosphere. Don't miss this opportunity to make this apartment your new home!
