Modern corner penthouse in the stunning Cortijo del Golf complex in the Atalaya/El Paraiso area. This elegant and bright penthouse apartment has views to the swimming pool area and the beautiful gardens of this gated complex. Located in the immediate vicinity of several golf courses, beaches and walking distance to various restaurants and shops. 
You are welcomed by an elegant entrance that leads to the open plan living room and kitchen. From here you enter the huge wrap-around terrace of 97 square meters facing south and west and which lets you enjoy sun or shade as you wish. The master bedroom is spacious with built-in wardrobes, en-suite bathroom and direct access to the terrace. The guest room is a good size with space for a double bed, built-in wardrobes and its own bathroom. This modern apartment comes fully furnished, excluding paintings and personal belongings, which allows for immediate enjoyment of its fine atmosphere. In the garage that can be reached via the elevator there is a large storage room immediately next to the spacious parking space.
