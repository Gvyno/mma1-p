Large 2 bed & 2 bath room apartment with good size living room & open concept kitchen in El Soto, La Mairena
It is the original show flat for the complex, bigger built size than many others in the urbanisation, as well with a very generous covered terrace and direct access via a private gate to the manicured tropical gardens and pool areas. 
There are 7 pools (1 is heated), Padel tennis & Gym with restaurant on site.
Owners of the Urbanisation are entitled to use the 9 hole par 3 golf course El Soto de Marbella for free.
Stunning panoramic views towards Mijas & Fuengirola, golf & scenic mountains with sea in the distance. 
Included in the price is underground parking and a 9m2 storeroom.
