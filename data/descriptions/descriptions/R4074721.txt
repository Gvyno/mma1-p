Upper floor level with a master bedroom en-suite, a terrace with stunning views to the sea and two guest bedrooms with a bathroom. The lower basement level has a purpose built bar, lounge with kitchenette, 2 bedrooms and a bathroom. Garden with barbeque area and roof solarium with sea views. Ideal family home or investment property. Within easy reach of the Torrequebrada club house and golf course.
