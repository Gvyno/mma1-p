Fabulous three-bedroom townhouse situated within a picturesque development on the Casares Costa. Located just a short walk to the beach, local beach restaurants, and general amenities (850 metres, 11 minutes walk). Communal facilities in this enclosed and gated community include private parking, two swimming pools and extensive tropical gardens. The property offers three bedrooms, plus an unusually large private garden and patio area with plenty of room for a dining table and chairs, as well as several sun loungers. Built over three levels, the ground floor comprises of a bright and airy living room with a fireplace, dining area, and a fully fitted and equipped kitchen. This level has direct access to the private garden and patio, plus a further patio and private off street parking can be found to the front of the property. The first floor has two double bedrooms and a family bathroom, whilst on the second floor, you find the master bedroom and en suite bathroom. Further features include; a private garden, and private parking for two cars. An absolutely perfect property for anyone wishing to live life without a car!
