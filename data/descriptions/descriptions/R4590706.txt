Spacious corner townhouse within a gated and secured family-friendly community at approximately 5kms from Puerto Banus and the beach, and only 200m away from the Turtle lake and its surrounding park. The property offers a large lounge/dining room with fireplace and large modern kitchen on the ground floor and 2 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms on the first floor. The master bedroom as very large, with several wardrobes and its en-suite bathroom. There are 2 terraces, one by the pool and another South-East private terrace with open views.
