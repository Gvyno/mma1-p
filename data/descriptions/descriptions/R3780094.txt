A newly built area and the largest neighborhood in Marbella. The perfect area for golfers as it is surrounded by 5 Golf large Courses. Due to the elevation, Nueva Andalucía is privileged with spectacular sea and mountain views. Only a 5' drive away from the nightlife center – Puerto Banus – yet far enough to enjoy the peaceful.
