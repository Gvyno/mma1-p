Beautiful house just 1 km from the Andalusian village of El Chorro.

This house is situated on a plot of 5.787 m2 and has very good access.

On the ground floor there is a fully equipped kitchen with oven, microwave, hob and extractor, dishwasher and a fridge / freezer.
The other side of this room is the salon with a beautiful floating fireplace.

At the end of the corridor there is a spacious master bedroom and there are 2 more bedrooms and a large bathroom with walk-in shower.

From the outside terrace there is a separate "guest" bedroom with an en-suite bathroom via a spiral staircase to the first floor.
This terrace is also very suitable to enjoy with a delicious breakfast in the morning or a dinner on a sultry summer evening.

Outside there is a nicely landscaped garden with an 8x4 swimming pool with a large sun terrace.
The largest part of the plot is fenced and offers sufficient privacy.

All facilities such as water, electricity and internet are available.
