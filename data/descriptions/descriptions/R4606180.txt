This stunning semi-penthouse apartment has direct sea views and is located in one of the most sought after, immaculately maintained apartment complexes in Estepona right by Playa del Cristo beach. The large open living room with dining area opens up onto the large balcony with direct views to the sea overlooking Gibraltar and Africa and electrical awnings to provide much needed shade in the height of summer. The apartment has four bedrooms, two bathrooms and a cloakroom. The master bedroom has an en-suite bathroom and direct access to the balcony. The independent kitchen is fully fitted with an adjacent utility room for laundry. The property is fully air-conditioned. The development also has a small gymnasium and games room for the use of residents with table tennis, pool table, and table football. An underground private parking space with storage unit and a community swimming pool in the garden area which has a gate allowing direct access onto the beach. It is an amazing apartment close to the port of Estepona and directly on the beach.
