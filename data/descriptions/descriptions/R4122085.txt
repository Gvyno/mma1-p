This up-and-running freehold Hostel is located in the heart of Fuengirola, only 650 meters from the train station and 300 meters to the beach. 
The occupancy is close to full all year around, and many of the guests are regulars, returning year after year. 
Within the complex, the guests can enjoy two large swimming pools and communal green areas.
At the present, the hostel has a 7,8 rating at Booking.com with very positive reviews from the guests, where many comment on cleanliness and good condition of the hostel and its rooms, as well as closeness to all amenities like beach, marina, shops, train and restaurants. 
An on-site laundry facility saves expenses a great deal for the business.
All rooms are located on a single floor and enjoys access with elevator and stairs.
The owners are happy to aid in the transition for a new owner, providing training and contacts.
