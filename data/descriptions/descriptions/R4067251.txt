The facilities of this magnificent kindergarten of 483 m2 plus a patio of 420 m2, have 3 classrooms for 2 year olds, 2 classrooms for 1 year olds and 1 classroom for children from 0 to 1 year old, in total 6 classrooms and a generous exterior and interior patio. All classrooms are equipped with age-specific materials and toys. The atmosphere is bright and warm.
The facilities are completed with a multipurpose room, 1 bathroom specially adapted for children, 1 bathroom for teachers and another one for babies.
1 kitchen, 1 office and a dining room.
Fully equipped and operational.
With space for 94 children. 
Special importance is given to safety and cleanliness. All the facilities correspond to the strict safety regulations indicated by the Ministry of Education.
