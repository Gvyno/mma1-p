This apartment is located in the upper part of Calahonda and forms part of a small, gated community with a pool and mature tropical gardens. From the south facing terrace you enjoy fantastic unrestricted sea views. The apartment has a nice living/dining area with direct access to the sunny terrace, a separate fully equipped kitchen with adjacent a laundry area, the main bedroom with ensuite bathroom and access to the terrace, a guest bedroom and a guest bathroom. Both bedrooms have fitted wardrobes. This apartment makes an ideal holiday home.

There is ample parking in the complex. Within walking distance you find a few restaurants and a bus stop which takes you to La Cala de Mijas, a shopping centre and the beach. Calahonda has got several supermarkets, banks, restaurants, bars, a 9 hole golf course and a lot more facilities. Malaga airport is 25 min drive and Marbella 15 min.
