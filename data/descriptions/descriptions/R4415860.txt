A lovely, spacious 3 bed, 2 bath ground floor apartment in the secure gated community of Los Naranjos De Marbella.

The property consists of a large lounge with open fireplace, with a separate dining area off to the side, a fully fitted kitchen, separate utility/storage area, 2 guest bedrooms sharing a shower room and a large master bedroom with en-suite bathroom.  Each bedroom has lots of wardrobe space, particularly the master bedroom.  The property also boasts a spacious South facing terrace, with sufficient space for a dining table and sun loungers and is accessible via the dining area.  The property also has an integrated aircon system throughout.

There are multiple swimming pools in the community, with the closest to the apartment being approx. 50m away.  Parking is outside, but private for the community.

The community is very conveniently located for access to Puerto Banus, which is approx. 5 mins drive.  There is also a Mercadona supermarket and café located right in the middle of the community, within walking distance of the apartment.

Malaga airport is approx. 40 mins drive to the East and Gibraltar approx. 50 mins drive to the West.
