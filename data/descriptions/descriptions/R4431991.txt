Spacious garden apartment in Nazules, a modern residential complex in Altos de Puente Romano, a prestigious area on Marbella's Golden Mile situated within walking distance from the famous Puente Romano hotel and the beach. 
The apartment comprises a good-sized living/dining room with access to a large partially covered terrace with dining/seating area and sunbeds; separate fully fitted and equipped kitchen with laundry room; 3 bedrooms, one of them en-suite and the others sharing  bathroom; guest toilet. In addition the property comes with a spacious sunny garden. There is 1 parking space and a storage room situated in the underground communal garage. The community offer a fully equipped gym and swimming pool. 

This fully gated residential complex was completed in 2018 and boasts a communal swimming pool, a paddle court and a small gym.
