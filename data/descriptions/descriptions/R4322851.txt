Wonderful 5 bedroom townhouse available in Álora. This townhouse is much bigger than it initially looks. From the outside it gives off the impression of being a small cozy country townhouse, but when you walk inside you realize just how large it really is. Distributed over two floors and 234 square meters this property has enough space for anything you may need.
The spacious kitchen is fully fitted and includes a dining area and a fire place. There are two comfortable lounges, one upstairs and one downstairs and there is also a complete dining room to make the most of. All of the five bedrooms are spacious and comfortable. The outside space is also extensive, allowing for a large terraced area to enjoy the sun, as well as large carport and barbeque area. From the outside you can also access a large storage area. The property also boasts two complete bathrooms and a utility/laundry room. 
The property looks over the gorgeous countryside and it is situated opposite a bus stop for easy travel to and from the main town. To drive into town will take you less than five minutes. To walk will take 20 minutes, and it is a wonderful walk to enjoy, surrounded by the breathtaking countryside of Málaga. This townhouse is also only half an hour drive away from the famous Caminito del Rey and a 40 minute drive from Malaga airport. Having such a major tourist attraction so close makes this place an ideal investment opportunity, although it is also perfect for a family wishing to enjoy the tranquility of the Spanish countryside.
