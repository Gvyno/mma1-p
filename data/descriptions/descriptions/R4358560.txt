Bright and sunny apartment in one of the best beachside urbanisations in Elviria. This third floor apartment is situated in Hacienda Playa and is a 4 minute walk to the beach and is walking distance to the Elviria shopping centre with all its bars, restaurants and supermarkets. The apartment offers three bedrooms with two and half bathrooms, a fully fitted kitchen with utility area, marble flooring throughout, double glazing and an ample terrace overlooking the communal garden and the swimming pool.
