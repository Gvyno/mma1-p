Situated in one of the most sought-after developments in Guadalmina Baja/Casasola and just 750m to the sea. This spacious two-bedroom first-floor property has a good-sized semi-covered terrace that enjoys a south/west-facing orientation and overlooks the beautiful pool and lush tropical gardens. The entrance hall leads through to the large living area and a modern, fully fitted kitchen with Siemens appliances, in turn, this leads to a separate utility room with access to a rear terrace from which you can enjoy the morning sun. The master suite has access to the main terrace and comes with an en-suite bathroom. The second bedroom is also spacious and has access to a rear terrace. The apartment has full underfloor heating, electric blinds, underground parking, and a storage room. The complex is fully gated with 24h security guards. There is a tennis and padel club just a 3 minute walk away as well as football facilities. San Pedro de Alcantara is a 4 min drive to the east.
