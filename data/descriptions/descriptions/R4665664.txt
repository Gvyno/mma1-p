In addition, the villa has an outdoor dining room, perfect for enjoying al fresco meals and gatherings with family and friends. The location of this villa in Nueva Andalucia offers direct access to the golf course, as well as a wide range of services and amenities, such as restaurants, shops and nearby beaches.
