Large townhouse with fantastic panoramic views facing southwest!!

This townhouse has a lot to offer, when you enter from the entrance level you are greeted by a nicely renovated open kitchen and a cozy living room with a fantastic terrace that has incredible views of the sea and Torreblanca. On the same floor there is also a laundry room and a guest toilet. 
On the upper floor there is a bedroom with fitted wardrobes, a complete bathroom, and a lovely large terrace to enjoy the perfect barbecue evenings with stunning views.
Downstairs from the entrance level there are 2 more bedrooms with one bathroom. At the bottom there is an extra bedroom and a large room that can be used as a lounge or cinema room. There is also a large sized storage room and direct access to the communal pool. The townhouse includes a private parking space right outside the door and its sold fully furnished.
