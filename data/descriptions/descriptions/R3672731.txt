Stunning country house with wonderful views, with a profit of € 30,000 from the own avocado plantation, per year. 
The construction area of ​​this house is 362m2 built, it has 158m2 basement, 90m2 loft and 114 m2 main floor.

From the living room, there is another staircase with access to the basement, where there is a room, a bathroom with shower, a storage room and a kitchen.
From the pool there are stunning views to the mountains, an outdoor kitchen with a barbecue and a large table to join together with the family and enjoy the nature.
In addition, there is a covered garage for 2 cars.
The house was built in 2007, it has its own well, it is completely fenced and has easy access, it is located 3 min by car from Coin in a quiet area and only 25 min from Marbella and 25 min from Malaga.
