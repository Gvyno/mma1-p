This duplex apartment is located in a secure and established urbanisation in Las Lomas del Marbella Club.
The urbanisation has a communal pool and garden, and has a 16 hour concierge.
The property consists of a nice living-dining room on the entrance level, a spacious kitchen with utility room with access to the garden and a guest toilet. From the living room there is access to a very nice terrace with mountain views facing east with morning sun. Downstairs there are 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms, one of them en-suite. All bedrooms lead out to the private garden ideal for spending time outside and ideal for children or pets to play.
