Villa located in Casares village, with spectacular views of the sea and the mountains.
The house is located on a corner and faces south, so it has a lot of light and privacy.
The house is built on three floors, with very good quality construction materials.
At the entrance of the property there is a very nice terrace with a fountain which  gives access to the house, on the main floor there is a large fully equipped kitchen, a large living room with a fireplace facing south with beautiful views of the sea and the mountains, two bedrooms which share a fully equipped bathroom, a laundry area and a large terrace facing south, with a barbecue area, being an ideal area to live outside and enjoy the climate we have on the Costa del Sol.
On the first floor there are two large bedrooms that share a fully equipped bathroom, and a large terrace facing south, with beautiful views of the sea and the mountains.
On the floor minus one, there is a large garage for several cars with a storage area, a hall and a bedroom.
It is a very charming villa, ideal to live all year round, or a nice holiday home.
It is located 10 minutes walking from the center of the Casares village, where there are all kinds of services, bars, shops, restaurants... 15 minutes by car from Casares beach, 20 minutes from the city of Estepona, 40 minutes from Marbella and 60 minutes from Malaga airport.
