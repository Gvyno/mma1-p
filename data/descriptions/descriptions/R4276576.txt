Stunning frontline golf villa in Mijas.

When you enter from the gate, you already feel a sensation of space and a well kept garden, where you also have your 2/3 parking spaces in front of the house.
The garden wraps around the whole house, with a lot of plants, palm trees and some covered terrace spaces.

Entering the house, you will find the open space living room, that opens out to the entertainment area on the terrace.
On the ground floor you will also find 4 bedrooms, and a utility room.
On the upper level there is 2 bedrooms with bathrooms ensuite.
On the lower level, you have a guest apartment or a "teenage" area, with ensuite bathroom.

A few of the headlined features to mention is:
* Heated floors in most of the house
* Heated swimmingpool
* Possibility to make a smaller 7th bedroom.

The villa is a must see.
