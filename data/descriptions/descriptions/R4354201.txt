Beautiful townhouse in the heart of Mijas.

It has three floors; the first floor has a kitchen, living room, and a small sitting room, going up to the first floor there are three bedrooms and a complete bathroom, each floor has its own balconies and terraces, on the third floor you have a large solarium where you can enjoy the magnificent views of the village; Sea and Mountain.

Mijas Pueblo continues to be a major attraction for both national and international tourists due to its typical Andalusian white village beauty.
