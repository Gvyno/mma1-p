The house is equipped with the latest solar panel system that covers the electricity needed to 100%, overproduction energy is sold to the energy supplier.
There is also a huge basement which could be used for any purpose.
There is a separate apartment of 20m2 in the house, with a livingroom, a bedroom, a bathroom and a little kitchen.
