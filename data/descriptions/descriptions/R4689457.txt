Two bedroom, two bathroom top (1st) floor apartment in popular area very close to El Paraiso Golf Course. 

The apartment has two terraces, one to the south east and the other to the north west ensuring all day sun for those who want to enjoy sitting out. 

Inside, the rooms are well proportioned. There is a separate kitchen adjacent to the dual aspect bright living / dining room which has a working fireplace and leads out to the terrace overlooking the sea, El Paraiso Golf and La Concha mountain. Additionally, there are two double bedrooms, one shower room and one bathroom making this the ideal property for family holidays or golfing breaks. 

The pool nearest to the apartment offers the most stunning views of any pool in the area and the gardens throughout the complex are immaculately cared for by the gardening and maintenance team. Members of El Paraiso Golf will also benefit from the direct access on to the course from just along the road from this apartment. 

Other amenities nearby include Bel Air Tennis Club, grocery shop as well as restaurants and supermarkets just a few minutes drive from the property.
