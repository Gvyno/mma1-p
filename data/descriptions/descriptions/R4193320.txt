This contemporary villa is located in the exclusive neighbourhood of La Quinta, and it sits in a privileged hilltop from where you can enjoy with astonishing panoramic views towards the sea, mountains and city. 
It's distributed over 3 levels, it has 5 bedrooms and 6 bathrooms, the impressive primary suite offers breathtaking sea views, with a private terrace and luxurious master bath. The villa has a spectacular rooftop, an infinity edge pool and a private spa centre including an indoor pool, sauna and Turkish bath.
