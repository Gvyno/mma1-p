This place is located in Alozaina, 8 km. from Yunquera and 30 minutes from Ronda.

It has 6,700 meters of land with fruit trees. It has water and electricity from the town hall, as well as a well with its quart for greater security. It also has a 1,000 liter cistern.

The house is 215 meters, distributed over 2 completely independent floors. Upon entering you have a porch with a very large table to enjoy with the family.
The first floor has a living room, kitchen, 3 double bedrooms and a bathroom, all exterior.

The second floor, which is accessed by an external staircase, making it completely independent, has 6 bedrooms, a bathroom and a kitchen.

Outside we find a very large space with a barbecue area, bread oven and a pool.

The property needs care and reform, but it has great possibilities to enjoy it with the family or use it as a rural house.

It has several hiking trails, a few minutes away is the source of the Jorox river.

The views of the mountains are extraordinary.
