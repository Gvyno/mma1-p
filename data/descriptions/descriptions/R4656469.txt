This exquisite duplex penthouse in the prestigious Los Naranjos de Marbella community boasts 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, and two terraces for picturesque views of the mountains, pool, and gardens. Situated in the fifth phase of the development, the rooftop terrace, facing south, offers an all-day sunny retreat. The apartment features an open-plan kitchen, chic decor, and includes all furnishings for effortless living. Residents enjoy a secure, gated community with 24/7 security and communal parking, creating a serene and convenient living environment.

Nestled in Nueva Andalucia, Los Naranjos de Marbella offers an idyllic escape with modern amenities. The urbanization features 8 pools and a variety of 1 to 4-bedroom residences, including exclusive new apartments blending traditional Spanish charm with modern comfort. Within walking distance, residents find a supermarket, hot yoga studio, and the Manolo Santana tennis club for leisure and wellness pursuits in a vibrant and friendly neighborhood setting. This prime location is only 5 minutes from Puerto Banus and within walking distance to amenities and golf courses, offering a perfect balance of convenience and luxury living.
