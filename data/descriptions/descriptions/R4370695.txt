Where is El Casar?

This stunning four-bedroom townhouse offers a unique opportunity to experience luxurious living in one of Sotogrande Costa's most desirable neighbourhoods.

El Casar in Sotogrande Costa is an exceptional residential area that offers a desirable luxury lifestyle with convenient access to various amenities. One of the remarkable advantages of living in El Casar is its proximity to a wide range of cafes, shops, supermarkets, and the charming Pueblo Nuevo town, all within a pleasant 20-minute walking distance.

The area benefits from excellent connections with both Malaga and Gibraltar international airports. Malaga Airport provides a gateway to the world, connecting residents to many global destinations. Meanwhile, the proximity of Gibraltar Airport, located just 20 minutes away, offers an additional option for travel convenience.
