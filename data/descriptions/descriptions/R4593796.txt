A bright and immaculate two bedroom, two bathroom corner apartment with stunning panoramic sea and mountain views just a few minutes walk from amenities and Centro Plaza.

Situated in a peaceful position in popular gated community Senorio de Aloha, this corner apartment benefits from triple aspect and a terrace from almost every room allowing lots of natural light to flow through.

This lovely home comprises a living and dining room with a working fireplace, an upgraded fully fitted kitchen, two double bedrooms, two bathrooms, 3 terraces, a utility area and a private underground parking space.

The main terrace has an outdoor lounge and dining area to enjoy the incredible La Concha and sea views. 

The master bedroom is fitted with built in wardrobes, has an en-suite bathroom with a bath and shower, and also its own private wrap around terrace to enjoy the afternoon sun. Furthermore there is a main/guest bathroom with a bath and shower, and a second double bedroom with fitted wardrobes and its own private terrace.

Not only is this well maintained complex perfectly located just a few minutes drive to Puerto Banus and surrounded by the best golf courses in Marbella, it offers three community swimming pools, a childrens pool, perfectly manicured gardens and a private padel court for the residents.

This is a must see apartment - an ideal holiday home and fantastic investment opportunity for a buy to let.
