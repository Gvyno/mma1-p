Cozy ground floor apartment with a beautiful view of the Mediterranean Sea.

The apartment is situated elevated and secluded in the Los Arqueros urbanization. 
The residence consists of a living room with access to the terrace, which is partially enclosed with glass walls that can be fully opened in the summer. In winter, they serve the purpose of being a delightful conservatory, where you can spend warm moments enjoying comfort and views.

From the living room, there is an entrance to the kitchen and utility room. The two rooms are separated with a corridor from where each room have access to separate bathrooms.

Parking and storage room are included with easy access from an elevator.  The community has 2 swimming pools and a children's pool.

In walking distance, you will find Los Arqueros golf club. Here you will find 2 restaurants, fitness facilities, padel courts rand tennis courts, a bowling alley/sports bar, driving range and a beautiful 18-hole golf course.

Benahavis municipality is known for being very well organized and with a high level of service, and especially low property taxes compared to surrounding municipalities.
