Lovely renovated apartment with a very enjoyable covered terrace and amazing views towards the coast and the sea. Located within the established gated community of Las Terrazas de la Quinta, a short walk from the club house of La Quinta Golf. The apartment is situated on the first floors, southwest facing with afternoon sun. Consists of open plan living room and kitchen, master bedroom with shower-room, guest bedroom and separate bathroom. Parking right outside the building. The community has 2 swimming pools and is situated frontline to the golf course of La Quinta, thus giving a feeling of tranquility with its green surroundings, and also openness with great views and at the same time privacy on your terrace. Excellent holidays apartment for golfers and sun-seekers!
