Beachside duplex in a very well-known urbanization on the Golden Mile, next to fabulous promenade and the Plaza del Mar leisure centre. 

The complex has 24-hour security, beautiful and well-kept community gardens, a gym, a large swimming pool and sauna. It has a privileged location, just a few meters from one of the best beaches in Marbella - Playa de la Fontanilla, close to all services, public transport and the center of Marbella within a short walking distance. 

The urbanization has its own water reservoir, which ensures independence from potential drought. The urbanization will soon be painted, which will give it a fresh and new look. 
The apartment consists of 2 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, both en suite, and a guest toilet. 

The entrance is on the first floor, which composes of a living room with fireplace and an American kitchen with a small dinning corner, both connected through a large window. The extensive living area has a direct access to the terrace, equipped with a sun blind to protect you from the sun. There are stunning sea views, swimming pool and community garden view. The upper floor comprises bedrooms and bathrooms as well as a small terrace. 

Additional features are A/C and marble floors. The price includes an underground parking space and a spacious storage room, both with the direct access, via an elevator, to the floor where the apartment is located.
