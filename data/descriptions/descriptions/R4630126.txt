Ascending the spiral staircase to the first floor, you'll discover an additional living area with an open fireplace, perfect for unwinding in the evenings. With the convenience of being within walking distance to the town centre and less than a 30-minute drive to Málaga airport and the stunning beaches of the Costa del Sol, this country home offers the perfect balance of rural tranquillity and urban accessibility.
