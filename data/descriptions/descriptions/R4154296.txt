Azata Golf Villas is a new exciting project comprises of 45 detached and semi-detached villas in a unique location, surrounded by lakes and the greens on the Azata Golf course. The properties have been designed in a modern, Mediterranean style, they are all spacious and built to the highest of standards. 

They are distributed over two floors and the terraces all enjoy fantastic views over the golf and lake. This project has properties with 3 to 4 bedrooms and 2 to 4 baths. Terrace sizes from 45 M2 and gardens sizes from 400 M2.
