Very spacious corner apartament, originally 3 bedrooms and changed to two bedrooms, located in a gated urbanisation with two large communal swimming pools, a large garden and communal parking. This flat has a large living room, two bedrooms and two bathrooms, spacious kitchen with utility room/storage room. The property has a large terrace and wonderful panoramic sea views. The floors are marble, there are fitted wardrobes in the bedrooms and the kitchen is fully equipped and furnished. Lovely Andalusian style urbanisation with many gardens and green areas. It is situated only a few meters from the famous restaurant "Miel y nata" in the upper part of Calahonda, and close to the dog park and bus stop. Only 5 minutes drive to the shopping centres and the beach.
