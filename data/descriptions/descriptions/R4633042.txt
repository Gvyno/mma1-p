This extraordinary and luminous apartment is situated in Higueron West 217, considered the best and most sought-after urbanization on the Costa del Sol. Boasting expansive gardens, walking and cycling paths, and communal pools, this location provides an unparalleled living experience. The apartment faces South East, offering breathtaking sea views and abundant natural light—a perfect orientation with morning sun and afternoon shade.

The living room and its 28 m2 terrace seamlessly merge, thanks to the large floor-to-ceiling glass doors. The terrace, overlooking a verdant area adorned with cork oak trees, invites residents to enjoy picturesque nature walks. 

Upon entering, you are welcomed by an entrance hall leading to a spacious living-dining room paired with a fully equipped open-plan kitchen. A separate utility room, two bedrooms, and two bathrooms with showers complete the layout. The master bedroom features an en-suite bathroom and opens to a generous private balcony, perfect for relishing the sunset. This balcony provides views of the communal pool and play area. 

The kitchen and living room connect effortlessly to the primary terrace, a 28 m2 space furnished with a dining table and lounge seating. The apartment is entirely new and has never been occupied. Included in the package are two parking spaces and a storage room.

Adjacent to the urbanization is the Sports Club Reserva del Higueron, offering a spa, gym, padel, and tennis courts, along with a café and multiple restaurants. Conveniently close is the Carvajal train station, facilitating easy commuting to the airport, Malaga, or the city of Fuengirola. This property promises not only a luxurious living space but also access to a range of amenities and convenient transportation options.
