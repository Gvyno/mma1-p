This prime investment opportunity is situated in the prestigious Casasola area of Guadalmina Baja, offering an exceptional frontline golf property. Comprising two seamlessly connected semi-detached villas and two garden plots since 2007, this architect-designed residence showcases innovative spatial solutions and employs top-notch construction materials.

Spread across three levels, the ground floor boasts two expansive living rooms with a fireplace, a dining area, a kitchen with a breakfast space, laundry and storage rooms, a guest toilet, and a staff bedroom with an en-suite bathroom and a private entrance. Opening to a generous covered terrace and the garden, this level is perfect for both relaxation and entertainment.

The first floor accommodates three bedrooms, two with private bathrooms and one with an en-suite bathroom. The master bedroom is impressively spacious, featuring two bathrooms (for him and her), ample built-in wardrobes, and a dressing room. Furthermore, the master bedroom can easily be transformed into two independent bedrooms, each with its own en-suite bathroom.

The third level introduces a covered solarium-studio with its own bathroom, providing a versatile space for various activities. The property is equipped with a central gas-heating system, ensuring year-round comfort, and the use of quality construction materials safeguards against humidity issues.

Among the additional amenities are covered parking for multiple cars, a designated space for a golf buggy, a barbecue area, an inviting outdoor swimming pool, and a generously sized garden plot. The property's strategic proximity to the beach, golf courses, schools, public transport, and shopping facilities further enhances its appeal and value.
