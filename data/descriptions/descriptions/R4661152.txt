A well established residential development in the up and coming area of La Campana in Nueva Andalucia. Half way between Puerto Banus and the charming town of San Pedro de Alcantara the area is well known for its the array of amenities you find walking distance from the property. The accommodation is well appointed and spacious comprising 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms (one en suite) a large lounge/dining room a kitchen/breakfast room and a small balcony. Other features include an allocated underground parking space, a store room, air conditioning and open mountain views.
