Duplex 2 bedroom penthouse in the quiet urbanization Botanical Park in Benahavis with two terraces.
Very bright and spacious living room with fireplace and access to the south oriented terrace. 
The separate kitchen is equipped with hob & oven and washing machine. 
The master bedroom has access to the terrace. The bathroom is next to the bedroom on the same level. 
When going up the stairs in the living room you arrive to a very big mezzanine open space that is used as second bedroom.
From here you also have a large panoramic window and access to the second big, also south oriented terrace.
The apartment comes with an underground covered parking and a private storage room.
The urbanization "Parque Botanico" is well known for its fantastic communal facilities. 
There are 2 outdoor swimming pools, a very well-kept garden and a children's playground. 
Inside you find a true sports complex with an olympic (25m!) indoor swimming pool, jacuzzi, sauna, and spacious gym.
The complex has 24 hours security.
