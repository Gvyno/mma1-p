Welcome to your new home in Calahonda!

This charming apartment offers a privileged location in the Calahonda Park development, where comfort and serenity blend perfectly.

Location:

Located in Calahonda, this residential gem offers a unique living experience. Enjoy spacious green areas, communal parking for your convenience, and two sparkling pools inviting you to relax under the warm Costa del Sol sun. Furthermore, various access points from different streets make mobility easy and convenient.

Apartment Features:

This stylish apartment welcomes you with two entrances, one through the main door and another from the lovely terrace. Inside, you'll discover a living-dining room with an open kitchen, creating a cozy space for entertainment. Its two bedrooms feature en-suite bathrooms, offering privacy and comfort. Additionally, the apartment includes an additional toilet, a porch for relaxation, and a terrace.

Additional Details:

Just 240 meters away, you'll find the beach and a supermarket, providing you with the convenience of coastal living and easy access to supplies. Air conditioning in the living room and main bedroom, along with a fan in the second bedroom, ensure an ideal climate in any season. The price includes all furniture, facilitating your move and allowing you to enjoy your new home from day one. Quick access to the highway from the development provides convenient connection to the region's main roads.
