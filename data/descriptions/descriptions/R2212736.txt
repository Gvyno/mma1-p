On this occasion, Stand Inmobiliario is offering rural estate, la Finca Menaya, in Alhaurin de la Torre. 
Situation: Irragated and dry land situated in la Finca Menaya, Municipal Term of Alhaurin de la Torre, just 800 metres from the town and 10km from Malaga airport. 
Inside the estate constitutes an area of 2,931 square metres and a 5 metre wide road.
The estate has two entrances in and out between the East and West, both of which are closed with an iron gate.
Water: The estate has its own water well and furthermore has 110 minutes a week of irrigated community water from the well of Funseca.
Electricity: In la Finca Menaya there is a light transformer yielding 100Kw.
