This 2 bedroom 2nd floor frontline golf flat is located just 5 minutes from the beach in the gated complex Las Terrazas de Santa Maria Golf in Elviria, Marbella.

The complex has 4 swimming pools and is in a quiet and peaceful location, yet only 10 minutes walk from Santa Maria Golf Club and 20 minutes walk from Elviria commercial centre where you will find all sorts of amenities including bars, restaurants, banks and shops.
The flat is sold with Italian furniture, fibre optic internet and electric blinds.
The flat includes an underground parking space and a tiled storage room.
