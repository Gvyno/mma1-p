Two story townhouse in a lovely street with perfectly manicured palm trees in the town of San Martin Del Tesorillo, Cadiz. 

The townhouse has two floors. Entrance hall, stairway room, large living/dining room with kitchen on ground level. 

Two bedrooms and fully fitted bathroom in top floor. 

From the living area, you can access the patio, with a toilet and stairs to the roof terrace with another independent room. 

Lovely town near Guadiaro river and walking distance to amenities including bars, pharmacy, cafes and restaurants.
