The property has been decorated and completely restored with modern finishings, and a change of dimensions to incorporate more space. Upon entering one is immediately present with sea views beyond the spacious living & dinning rooms with its tall ceiling. The open plan kitchen sits well slightly above the living areas, annexed to it is washroom and a utility room that could conditioned as a guest quarter or a 4th bedroom. The three bathrooms and guest toilet each have a practical and unique design. The second floor houses two guest rooms and the master bedroom, of which includes en-suite, terrace with sea views, and study space. The top floor is designed as a chill out area with kitchenette, bar, two terraces, bathroom and open living room.
