This detached villa sits in the countryside a short walk to village... it could not be in a better location!

Property Details:

As soon as the electric gates open to welcome you to this villa, you are wowed by the views of the both the countryside and the mountains.  As you drive down to the villa you will see a wooden storage room on the right and on the left the separate casita with private terrace, slightly elevated in order to enjoy those views!

The main house is surrounded by landscaped gardens and sits beautifully on the mountain.  Double arched wooden doors open to the fully fitted rustic styled kitchen with direct access to the shaded terrace that runs along the front of the property.

The kitchen feels spacious with centre island and plenty of storage.  A staircase leads from this room to one of the 3 bedrooms in the main house.  This bedroom could quite easily be the master bedroom with adjoining bathroom and double french windows opening to a private terrace.

The lounge sits in the centre of the property and has a beautilful hand crafted feature chimney breast with wood burning fire.  This room also had double patio doors leading terrace.  A hallway leads from the lounge to two double bedrooms and the guest bathroom.   The master bedroom with ensuite bathroom has patio doors leading directly to the swimming pool.   Bedroom 2 has patio doors leading to the front terrace with views of the mountains.   

The casita sits to the side of the property with one bedroom and a bathroom.
