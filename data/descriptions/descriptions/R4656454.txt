Outstanding Townhouse for sale in the heart of Benahavis pueblo.

As we walk downstairs we come across a fully refurbished living area: bedroom with a on suite bathroom & shower, as well as a garage with space for 2 cars.

On the first floor we have a master bedroom on suite with access to a west facing terrace with fabulous views of Benahavis Village.
On this floor we also find another two bedrooms & plenty of storage space. 

Enjoy the benefits of living in one of the wealthiest villages in Spain: Benahavis & all the activities the Town hall have to offer for the younger generation.
