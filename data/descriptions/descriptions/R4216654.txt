Wonderful 2 bedroom, 2 bathroom duplex penthouse located just located a couple of minutes from Estepona Marina.  This property is located in tranquil area of Estepona centre and is very bright as it is located on a 6th floor. On the lower level, we find a spacious kitchen and laundry room, both bedrooms equipped with lovely windows overlooking the town and large wardrobes, as well as both bathrooms, one of them en-suite.  The living room leads onto a covered terrace that boasts a spiral staircase that takes you to a 45m2 roof top terrace.  On the solarium there is plenty of space to cover a part of it and use as a kitchenette, gym, extra bedroom or cinema room.  This area is the perfect chill-out space.
Less than 5 minutes' walk a beautiful beach, some of the best bars and restaurants in town, as well as several gymnasiums, supermarkets, pharmacies and shops.   
The property comes with a garage and storage room included in the sales price.
The complex has a communal swimming pool.
