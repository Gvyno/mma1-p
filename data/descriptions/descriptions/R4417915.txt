This is a beautiful country property comprising of 2 individual houses each with their own gated entrance, and accessed by a concrete road only 2 minutes drive from the main road into the beautiful Andalucian village of Ojen and 10 minutes from Marbella. The larger house consists of 2  large bedrooms, 2 full bathrooms, an open plan kitchen and sitting room, a traditional bodega bar, pump room, storage room, garage and swimming pool with beautiful views.  There is plenty of outdoor terracing and gardens for those wanting to be self sufficient. The smaller house has 2 bedrooms, 1 full bathroom, a traditional kitchen with utility room plus a cosy sitting room with beautiful vaulted, beamed ceilings and open fireplace. Outside the terracing runs the length of the house for plenty of outdoor space and there is an open water deposit that could be converted into a natural swimming pool.
Set in an area of outstanding beauty, these two houses lend themselves to use as a larger main residence and a guest house, for rural tourism usage, or a mix of private and tourism use.
Entire plot is just over 17.000m² ideal for keeping horses or growing your own produce.
