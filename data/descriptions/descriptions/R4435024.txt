Alhaurin el Grande is a fabulous location for anyone wanting to enjoy outdoor country living. Only 25 minutes away from the beaches and Malaga or Marbella center. The charming active pueblo offers more less all you you need, nice shops, cafes and restaurants and good environment with nice people and Spanish atmosphere. 

Fantastic location, easy access in and out to the pueblo. Walking distance everywhere in town. This property is located in the beautiful and relaxing part of Alhaurin el Grande. Parking on the street is easy. 

Andalusian style townhouse in Alhaurin El Grande which has a quirky layout. This is a 4-5 bedroom, 2 bathroom property with numerous patio and terrace areas, great views of the Guadalhorce Valley and even a small vegetable plot. 
This house needs a new owner who has an adventurous side when it comes to doing some reform work. It is habitable as it is but to turn this rough diamond into a gem you will need some imagination.

On the first floor you have 1 bedroom and modern renovated bathroom. Spacious salon with dining area, with lot of natural light. Next to the bedroom you have a lot of space to renovate cozy kitchen and relaxing area. A lot of options to make this spacious area useable and functional. Another bathroom in the kitchen area. From kitchen you can access to a amazing private patios, in different levels. Not even mentioning the roof terrace, where you have a storage room and little bit more up, you have a hobby room and the vegetable plot.

From the living room you go up, where you have 3-4 bedrooms. 4 rooms in total, one share the entrance door for two rooms. Also you have little cave room on the patio levels, great place to have a little siesta during hot summer days.
This property is necessary to visit, see the charm and possibilities to make this as you dream home.
