Set on a hillside overlooking the blue Mediterranean sea the Las Olas resort is the reference for luxury in Riviera del Sol, Mijas Costa. This exciting individually designed penthouse with it's sweeping elegant curves and high ceilings offers a unique living experience. A home where inside and outside co-exist in perfect harmony. The expansive main terrace is divided into three areas, seating, entertaining/sun-worshipping and reflection with glorious views across a green expanse and down to the sea. There are two storage rooms on the terrace, very useful  for terrace paraphernalia when not at home. Internally there is a large principle reception room with generous dining alcove, a modern fully equipped kitchen with breakfast bar and terrace and three ensuite bedrooms with fitted wardrobes all with direct access to a terrace. In addition to extensive well stocked gardens the resort enjoys two large swimming pools (one for the little ones) with ample sunbathing areas, a well stocked fitness room and a seasonal cafe. The property is fully airconditioned, benefits from two parking spaces and an additional storage room in the garage area. Located close to transportation networks, Miraflores Golf and the many other golf courses of the province, La Cañada shopping and Marbella, the glorious stretches of beach and the dunes of Cabopino and the historic city of Málaga. In short the very best location to enjoy the magnificent infrastructure, lifestyle and culture of the Andalucian coast.
