Fantastic apartment located in Torreblanca, in a quiet urbanization with a community pool and common areas. The path, which is right next to the house, leads directly to Torreblanca beach, which takes about 15 minutes to walk. There is also free public transport that takes you to Los Boliches and vice versa every 10 minutes. The apartment is located on the first floor with an elevator. It has 2 double bedrooms with a built-in wardrobe, a complete bathroom with a bathtub, a toilet, a living room with a fully furnished American kitchen and a 6m2 terrace where you can enjoy splendid views of the mountains and the sea. Marble floors and insulating windows that insulate from the outside temperature. Surrounded by a playground and an animal park, it is the ideal place to live in contact with nature. Come visit it!
