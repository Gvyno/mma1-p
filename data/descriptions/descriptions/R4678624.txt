Andalusian Gem with Breathtaking Vistas!

Nestled in the sought-after enclave of Buena Vista, where the essence of Fuengirola, Benalmadena, and Mijas Pueblo converges, lies this splendid townhouse, offering a harmonious blend of modern comfort and traditional allure.
Upon arrival, you're greeted by a serene ambiance, with the main floor seamlessly connected to outside parking, while a garage, accessible through a secure entrance, ensures both safety and convenience.

Step inside to discover a spacious entrance including a guest toilet, and from here a charming kitchen with beautiful Andalusian mosaic tiles. The kitchen boasting magnificent mountain panoramic views, overlooking the idyllic landscapes of Mijas and Fuengirola. Adjoining this heart of the home is a spacious dining area and living room, bathed in natural light, with access to a covered terrace, perfect for al fresco dining.

Ascend to the top floor, where a sun-drenched roof terrace awaits, equipped with an awning and offering unparalleled panoramic vistas, ideal for soaking in the Andalusian sunshine.

Venture to the lower level, where tranquility reigns in the form of four generously sized bedrooms, two bathrooms, and access to the enclosed private garden. 
Among these, a master suite awaits, boasting an ensuite bathroom, fitted wardrobes, and double doors opening onto the garden. Two additional ample bedrooms with fitted wardrobes provide ample space, while a versatile room can serve as a cozy bedroom or an office.

The garden, bordered by a gate, ensures privacy and tranquility, offering a serene sanctuary where pets can enjoy freely in the private surroundings.

From the property it is only a 2 min walk on the quiet residential street to the communal pool, that can be enjoyed in the surroundings of a well maintained garden.
