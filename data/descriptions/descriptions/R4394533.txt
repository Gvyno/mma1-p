El Mirador de Calahonda a gated complex with a mix of apartments and penthouses with communal gardens with a large swimming pool; located next to La Siesta Golf a 9 hole, par 3 golf course; opposite the Club del Sol tennis club that offers 12 tennis courts, 4 padel courts, 4 squash courts, a large gym, changing facilities including a sauna and jacuzzi and classes and tournaments; within a short drive from the toll road motorway; a 10 minute walk from "the strip" with a mix of bars and restaurants; a 20 minute walk from the beach, beach bars, Calahonda has a variety of supermarkets, various shops, banks, cafe bars & restaurants.
