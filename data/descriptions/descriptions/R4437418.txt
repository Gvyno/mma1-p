A beautifully presented ground floor apartment in Calanova Golf, La Cala de Mijas, featuring two bedrooms and two bathrooms. This residence is part of a secure gated community, boasting both a terrace off the lounge and a terrace at the front entrance. Inside, you'll find a fully equipped kitchen, an open-concept living and dining area, along with two spacious double bedrooms and two bathrooms, one of which is an en-suite. Enjoy stunning views of the gardens and golf course, and take advantage of the extensive amenities, including two exquisite pool areas, lush tropical gardens, and padel tennis courts. The property also comes with an underground parking space and a storage room, all included in the price. The main terrace was installed with glass curtains at the beginning of this year, giving you the feeling of another living space during the winter months.
