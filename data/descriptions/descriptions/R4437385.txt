COUNTRY HOUSE IN MONDA

This property offers you tranquillity and nature but with all the comforts.

You will be able to disconnect from everything, you will be able to organise family meals that will last all day and night if you wish. Your children will enjoy the nature, the swimming pool, creating unforgettable memories. You will have your own vegetable garden, your own chickens as well as all the fruit trees (mango, orange, avocado...).
Thanks to its solar panels you will save electricity so maintenance costs are minimal. In other words, you will be self-sufficient.
Moreover, you won't have to drive on narrow and bad roads thanks to its proximity to the highway.
