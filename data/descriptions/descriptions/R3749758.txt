This bright large four bedroom villa which has been recently renovated is located in a tranquil area in Riviera del Sol on the costa del sol.

The villa briefly consists of an entrance hall, large lounge/dining room with a beautiful feature fireplace, a separate fully fitted kitchen, also on this level there is one double bedroom with fitted wardrobes and a large bathroom. On the second level there are three double bedrooms 2 ensuite bathrooms and a further separate family bathroom..
The lounge is bright and leads onto a large terrace from here you have access to the gardens and private swimming pool.
The villa also has a very large 180m2 basement which could be developed into more living space if required.
The villa comes fully furnished, with hot and cold air-conditioning and double glazing throughout.
