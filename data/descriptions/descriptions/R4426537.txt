3 bedroom apartment with amazing views over golf to the sea at Los Flamingos, Benahavis

Hoyo 19 is a community of contemporary style apartments standing in beautifully maintained, mature gardens overlooking Los Flamingos Golf Course and the Villa Padierna Hotel.  The community has 24 hour security, a choice of 3 swimming pool and paddle courts and is the perfect base for a permanent or holiday home.  From here there is easy access to down to the beaches and to all the amenities of the New Golden Mile. Marbella, Estepona and Puerto Banus are all easily accessible by car.

The view from the large west facing terrace is one the main features of this exceptional apartment. It overlooks the Los Flamingos Golf Couse, Villa Padierna Hotel, the lake, the Mediterranean Sea and coastline to Estepona and Gibraltar.   Access is via a lift from the parking garage and main portal.  The entrance hallway leads through a well proportioned sitting and dining room which has bifold doors out to the covered terrace which has a decking finish and glass balustrade to make the most of the view.  There is a separate fitted kitchen with integrated appliances including oven, hob, dishwasher and fridge/freezer together with a separate utility room with washing machine.

An inner hallway with an extensive range of fitted wardrobes leads through to the master bedroom which has a dressing area with fitted wradrobes and direct access to the terrace.  There is a luxury bathroom en suite.  The two family bedrooms both have fitted wardrobes and share a bathroom.

Hot and cold air conditioning is installed together with double glazing.  A garage parking space and a large store room are included in the sale.
