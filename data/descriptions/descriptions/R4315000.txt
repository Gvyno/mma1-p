Contemporary four bedroom, south facing villa in Puerto del Capitan, Benahavis, only a short drive to the beach, Puerto Banus and all leisure amenities. The villa is in an elevated position with wonderful sea and mountain views. There is a large bright living area with a fireplace and access to the partially covered terraces, garden and infinity swimming pool; a fully fitted kitchen with island; the master suite and three guest suites. Other features include hot and cold air conditioning and marble floors.
