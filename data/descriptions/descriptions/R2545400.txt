Palatial villa located at the top of Paraiso Alto (Benahavis) and offering unbeatable panoramic views across the local countryside, golf course, mountain range and across to the Mediterranean. The current owners built the property to suit their family's needs with much care and attention to detail following stringent German building methods and furnishing it with ornate and high-quality luxurious furniture, fixtures and fittings.
