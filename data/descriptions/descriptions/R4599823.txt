Apartment with Private Garden in La Reserva de Marbella.
This property boasts a built area of 110 sqm, situated on a plot of approximately 300 sqm with its own private garden. Featuring a spacious living room, 2 bedrooms with direct access to terrace and garden, a newly equipped kitchen, 2 large bathrooms, a sizable terrace with garden space, garage, and storage room.
With marble floors and both heating and cooling air conditioning.

The complex offers accessible facilities for people with reduced mobility, featuring three beautiful communal pools, paddle tennis courts, recreational areas, and jacuzzis. It includes a concierge service and lovely communal garden spaces.

The gated community has a barrier entry and security guard, along with a bar-restaurant on-site.
