For sale two industrial buildings located in Llano de La Cruz, a hamlet belonging to Ronda / Arriate, with good access by several routes, including the road to Seville.

The warehouses are composed of a single plant, and have their own office. They consist of 512 meters built according to cadastre, are built on a plot of 1,000 meters, with ample parking area, loading and unloading area, and warehouse.

In each of the rooms there is good ventilation, with windows to the outside, and the office has air conditioning. It has drinking water point, only would have to be discharged.

For more than 20 years it was used as an artificial stone and plaster factory.
