Immaculate ground floor apartment is located in Riviera del Sol. This 95 m2 two bedroom two bathroom apartment, built in 2003, is located in small gated community of Cumbres de Riviera. Entrance is into a Hallway leading through to a spacious and bright lounge/diner. There are two Bedrooms, both with fitted wardrobes, the master has en-suite bathroom and there is a separate guest bathroom. The lounge leads onto the large south facing private terrace with garden views. The kitchen is fully fitted. The property has hot/cold air conditioning and marble flooring throughout. The urbanisation benefits from communal pool and communal underground parking and storeroom. Walking distance to all amenities including restaurants, bars, shops and golf.
