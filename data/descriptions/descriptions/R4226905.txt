The Law of Impulse for the Sustainability of the Territory of Andalusia (Lista), allows building on rustic land with certain conditions, such as not inducing new constructions in the form of settlements, the distances between them will be arranged according to the circumstances of each municipality taking into account certain criteria. Construction on rural land in Andalusia is subject to the prior authorisation of the local council of each locality under a building permit and the constructions can be for residential use as well as infrastructure for industrial use, as long as the criteria for the preservation of the rural land and the landscape are fulfilled.
