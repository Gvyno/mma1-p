This wonderful townhouse is located in Condes de Iza, privet residential development situated in Nueva Andalucía. It provides 24 hours security, community pool and gardens, just a few minutes from Puerto Banús, shops, sports facilities and a range of amenities. 

The townhouse has been renovated. It has 4 bedrooms, 3 full bathrooms and a toilet, large terrace on the ground floor with access from the spacious living room plus a large solarium.
