Very large townhouse located just a few metres from the Town Hall. On the ground floor is a beautiful spacious entrance, large, fitted kitchen, living-dining room with fireplace, 3 bedrooms, and a bathroom. On this floor there is access to the terrace where there is a large utility room, patio and nice views over the Guadalhorce valley. The covered patio could be enclosed if needed for storage space, for example.

The first floor has 4 bedrooms, a bathroom and access to the attic, which is currently a large open space which could also be used as a playroom or extra storage.

To the rear of the house at street level, there is an entrance and access to the ground floor, which offers the possibility of making a garage for several cars as the surface area is the same as the floor of the house.

The property is well located in the heart of town, 5 minutes from shops, pharmacy, and schools. Ideal for a large family or to create a Bed & Breakfast or a boutique hotel.
