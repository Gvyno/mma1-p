Single storey Country House with two bedrooms, a bathroom, open-plan kitchen, living room with a wood-burning fireplace.

The property benefits from a covered terrace of about 15m², and a fully fenced plot with swimming pool and storage room. There is both town water and community well water (the community well has a monthly fee of €30).

The house has good access, and the village of Villafranco del Guadalhorce is within walking distance.

Note: The property has some cracks in the wall of the living room and the storage room.
