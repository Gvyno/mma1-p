Three bedroom apartment in Elviria with sea views 

Corner apartment with 3 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms in an enclosed urbanisation with 24 hour security.
This apartment is located on the second floor. Very practical layout with a living-dining area, separate kitchen with adjacent a laundry, three bedrooms with two bathrooms en suite and a separate bathroom. The apartment has an open sunny terrace and is south facing. Quiet location within the complex with very well maintained gardens, several swimming pools and offering 24 hour security at the main entrance gate. This is one of the few three bedroom apartments in the complex. From the sunny terrace you can enjoy views to the mediterranean sea, mountains and garden. 

Santa Maria Village is located in the very popular area of Elviria with its beautiful sandy beaches, wide choice of restaurants, Santa Maria Golf, Nikki Beach Club and Don Carlos hotel.
