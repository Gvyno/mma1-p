This luxurious villa in Guadalmina Baja, is situated just 50m from the beach. Being close to Puerto Banus with upscale amenities and exclusive shops but living in haven of tranquility provides residents with the convenience of nearby shopping, fine dining and golf courses. 
The villa spans over a plot of 2.200 m2 which encompasses the house, pool and a carport for 2 cars and 2 uncovered spaces. 
Impressive hall with guest toilet and salon, leading to the main living area with high ceiling, separate dining room, fully fitted and equipped kitchen. The rooms have access to the covered terrace and a garden with a swimming pool.
With a total of 6 bedrooms and 6 bathrooms and staff apartment, the house is a beautiful gem.
Cinema (not in use) and basement make the house complete.
Furthermore the house is fitted with double-glazing, marble floors, underfloor heating, air conditioning hot and cold.
