This well located family townhouse situated in a quiet area with less traffic, features a private patio and terraces with surrounding town and countryside views. It is located in the historical town Álora, 30 minutes from Malaga. 
This property consists of 3 double bedrooms and 3 bathrooms with a good sized family bathroom. On the ground floor there is a spacious garage and several large storage rooms. A well sized lounge and dining room is leading onto a terrace overlooking the town and mountains. The well-equipped large kitchen also leads onto the private patio. On the second floor there is the outside kitchen/ barbecue area, surrounded by plants and a outside WC. On this floor there is a second kitchen and living room which also leads to a second terrace. This townhouse is very well located within walking distance of full amenities, for example, hospitals, schools, hypermarkets and sport centres. The town of Álora is only 35 minutes drive from Malaga city centre and the airport.
