If you are dreaming of a home with a magnificent view in a quiet environment, then this house is for you!

A beautiful country house with a lot of charm and nice finishing touches. When you open the door you will discover a homely living room with fireplace, open plan kitchen and pantry. Also on this floor are two bedrooms, a bathroom, and a utility room. Upstairs is a large bedroom with a high ceiling.

The garden, surrounding the house, is planted with trees, and there are beautiful spaces where you can enjoy the view, enjoy the peace & quiet or simply take a nap under the hundred-year-old olive tree. The access to the swimming pool area is via a few stairs. This relaxing spot boasts an infinity pool with a 180º view over the valley and towards Málaga, whilst to the side of the pool is a lovely covered terrace where you can enjoy the last rays of sunshine.

This is a house with a lot of character, various places to relax, a true little paradise!
