A cozy and bright 2 bed apartment nestled in the tranquil Mijas Golf community. This frontline golf apartment has a west facing terrace with lovely golf course views from the two bedrooms. The kitchen connects with the light, bright living room area. You can step out from here to the east facing terrace which has spectacular mountain and green countryside views. There is ample wardrobe and cupboard space inside the property plus an underground parking space and also additional space in the storage room. You also have the use of the lovely communal swimming pool and gardens to enjoy.
