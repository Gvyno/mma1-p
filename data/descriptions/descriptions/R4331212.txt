Chestnut and cork oak farm, almonds, tangerines and many fruit trees over 22 hectares in El Valle del Genal. Easy access from the highway.
Two springs with a pool and a drinking water well.
A small house of 80m2 on two floors with two bedrooms on the top floor, on the ground floor 1 bathroom, kitchen/living room. A perimeter of 1 hectare around the house is fenced for a vegetable garden.
Spectacular views, very close to the museum town of Genalguacil. 45 minutes from Estepona, the Costa del Sol and 1 hour from Ronda.
