Spacious 3 bed apartment in the center of Marbella, strategically located in second line beach, in a building built in 2001 that has a communal pool on the roof with 360º panoramic views.

This property is a eighth floor with southwest orientation. It has a built area of 139 m². This apartment combines the proximity to the Mediterranean Sea, the beaches and the promenade of Marbella, with the privilege of being in the heart of the city, next to the main avenue, all kinds of services, the Alameda park and the picturesque old town.

It offers qualities such as Lumon glass curtains on both terraces, electric blinds in the bedrooms, air conditioning and marble floors.

The property also includes two storage rooms and two parking spaces for your comfort.
