Set high in the hills between Tolox and Alozina is this beautiful country property with stunning views down the Guadalhorce valley toward Málaga. Access is very good with 800 meters of concrete road and the last 300 meters being a good quality dirt track easily accessible by car.
The property was built in 2011 and is in excellent condition throughout. It sits on a plot of 2981m2 with a selection of olive trees. The terrace is the perfect spot to enjoy the far-reaching views on offer with very little passing traffic as it´s one of the last properties down the lane.

You enter the home into the open plan living area, with fully fitted kitchen and breakfast bar. In the corner you have a lovely log burner perfect for those chilly days which doesn´t happen too often. Furthermore, you have three double bedrooms, one with en-suite and family bathroom. The property has been tastefully decorated throughout and is in excellent condition. Outside there is a stunning pool area, low maintenance which also boast the stunning views on offer. There is a concrete driveway which leads to a single car port and small storage shed.

Other features include town water, fully fitted kitchen and a small olive grove. The property is fully registered with AFO.

If it´s peace and tranquillity you are looking for then this is the property for you. There are many walks on your doorstep including the beautiful Charco del Pntón Alto which follows the edge of the Rio Grande with stunning clear water rock pools and waterfalls.
