Housing with a lot of outdoor space in an urban area.

In a quiet and natural urban environment with panoramic views, a children's play and sports area with easy parking.
Three floors, three patios and two roof terraces. 220 meters built and 159 meters of patios.
Ground floor 86 m2, with a large hallway, body of the house, bedroom, living room, kitchen, bathroom with laundry room and patio with fountain.
First floor 80 m2, three bedrooms, bathroom and roof terrace 17 m2.
Second floor 54 m2, three bedrooms and 14 m2 roof terrace.
First patio on the ground floor with panoramic views of the Veracruz hermitage. Second patio with beautiful views of the town and La Peña, with a flower bed. Third patio of 75m2 with trees and flowerbed, with access to the outside to a municipal green area and view of the Peña de los Enamorados.
