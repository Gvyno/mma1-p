This property is located in Torreblanca, in a quiet and comfortable urbanization very close to leisure areas, shops and the sea. Upon entering the house we find a bright and spacious living-dining room with a fully equipped American kitchen integrated into the living room, creating an open and diaphanous space. The living room has direct access to a private terrace where you can enjoy sunsets with views of the urbanization. It has two double bedrooms with built-in wardrobes. One of them with a private terrace and a full bathroom with a shower. The house has a large private garden where you can sunbathe and organize evening parties. Its two community pools and garden areas make it a fantastic place to live surrounded by vegetation and tranquility.
