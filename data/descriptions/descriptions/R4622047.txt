San Roque Golf:  4 bed/3 bath villa located on the highest point of San Roque Club affording views to the sea, mountains and golf course. Spanish patio leading to hall , lounge, dining room, kitchen and bedrooms. Off the kitchen is a drying area and a small terrace ideal . The garden room is fully enclosed with glass "curtains" and  wood burner. The master suite is on the first floor off a landing which could be used as a small office.The swimming pool is surrounded by a decorative stone balustrade.
