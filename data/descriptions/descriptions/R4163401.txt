This villa is truly something out of the ordinary, with several renovated parts and endless possibilities depending on your needs.
Its unique location on the corner plot gives you the privilege of enjoying views both to the east, with La Concha, and to the west, overlooking the entire valley, the mountains and Gibraltar.
