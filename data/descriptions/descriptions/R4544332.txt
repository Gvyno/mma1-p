Finca for sale in the padrón Estepona with views of the sea and the mountains, located just 10 minutes by car from the beach in Andalusia, Estepona, Costa del Sol, near Marbella, and Puerto Banús.


This wonderful rustic property is located in the area known as the padrón Estepona, it has very good access by paved road, it is in a very quiet area where you can enjoy living in nature and at the same time it is very close to all services.
It has a plot of 10,050 m2 and within it there is a house built with a total of 355 m2 built and a warehouse of 30 m2. And it is distributed over three floors. Ground floor: Large living room with fireplace, fully equipped kitchen with access to a covered porch with access to the garden and two large bedrooms and a bathroom. First floor: it has two bedrooms and a bathroom, the master bedroom has a large bathroom en suite and has access to a large terrace from where you can enjoy wonderful views of the sea and the mountains. Basement floor: this floor is used as a warehouse and workshop area.
Exterior: The farm is completely fenced, it has a good pool with a barbecue area, a room for gardening tools, it has a plantation of avocados and other fruit trees, it has its own well water and water from the irrigation community.
