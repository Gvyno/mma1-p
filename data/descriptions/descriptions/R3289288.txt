This villa has recently been completed, decorated and furnished. Located on the Golden Mile, Marbella, it is just a few steps away from the beach and Marbella's sea promenade which leads you to Marbella and Puerto Banus.

The impressive main facade is enhanced by travertine marble slabs as a frame to the main entrance. The whole construction is a contemporary concept with clean and simple spaces allowing any type of decoration. On ground floor kitchen/ dining and living room are integrated and boast large panoramic windows and sliding doors which attract the outside to the inside. A wine cellar, bedroom/ office with bathroom and dressing room plus Guest WC complete the distribution on this level.
