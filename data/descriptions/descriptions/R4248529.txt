Las Lagunas is a town of Mijas, in the province of Malaga. It is also known as Mijas Costa, although this name is also used to designate the entire southern area of ​​the municipality, including the towns of La Cala de Mijas and Calahonda.

Las Lagunas does not have precise official boundaries, but generally it is considered part of Las Lagunas to the territory delimited to the south by the Fuengirola river, to the east by the border with the municipality of Fuengirola and the A- 7 and to the north and west by the AP-7, covering both the original nucleus of Las Lagunas and the neighborhoods of El Juncal, Las Cañadas, El Albero, etc., the urbanizations of El Coto, La Sierrezuela, El Lagarejo and Campo Mijas.

Las Lagunas is the most modern, commercial area of ​​Mijas, concentrating more than half of the population of the municipal area with 48,682 inhabitants.
