Fantastic townhouse in Los Pacos, Fuengirola close to all amenities for a comfortable living. The property is distributed in 4 floors. In the basement there's a garage with access to the ground floor. The entrance has a beautiful patio, and inside there's an elegant large living room and dining area, a guest toilet and a fully equipped kitchen. From the living room you reach the huge 54 m2 terrace, where you can enjoy morning sun and it has possibility to build a pool and a BBQ area. The first floor has got 3 bedrooms all with enough space for double beds, and the master bedroom has unsuited bathroom and walk-in closet. On the top floor is the 4th bedroom and a 20 m2 roof terrace with spectacular mountain views. The area is very popular and it's nice and quiet and has all the amenities like supermarkets, bars and restaurants. Los Boliches beach and the train station are less 20 minutes by walk and Malaga Airport 25 minutes by car.
