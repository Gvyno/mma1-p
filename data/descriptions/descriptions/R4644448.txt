This is a beautiful first floor apartment sitauted in the "Bahia de Banus" development, within a few minutes walk of the beach.

Puerto Banus is around a 15 minute walk from the development, with various beachside restaurants along the way, as well as the Guadalpin Hotel, which has various restaurants and bars.

The apartment is furnished and presented in excellent condition, so is ideal for the discerning client.

There is a modern open plan Kitchen adjoining the main living room / dining room, which is light and bright with dual aspect windows. There are patio doors onto the large terrace.

Both the master and guest bedroom have en suite bathrooms and there is also a guest cloakroom.

A private parking space and store room is in the garage below.

Communal swimming pools and security on site.
