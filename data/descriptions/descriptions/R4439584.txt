Great south-east facing apartment with beautiful open sea views in the upper Calahonda. The apartment has a nice cosy living-dining room with fireplace leading to a covered terrace with fantastic views. The kitchen has a good size and it is fully fitted and semi opened onto the dining-room with a separate laundry room. The master bedroom with electric shutter has an ensuite bathroom with bathtub and there is a second renovated bathroom with shower for the other bedroom and also accessible for visitors. The apartment has the A/C installed with an individual meter and payable to the community every semester. The community offers a very large swimming pool ideal for swimmers and with sun all  day, a communal leisure area next to a pond where you can relax and exchange books with neighbors...There are 2 communal gated parking areas for residents plus extra spaces just outside the Urbanization reserved for residents. A few bars and restaurants are just at the back of the Urbanization, crossing the road.  Next to the Urbanization you can walk to a bus stop that will take you to the commercial center in Fuengirola,to  a nice playground for children and to a big dog park where you can take your pet for a run if you have one! You can be on the beach of Calahonda in a 5mn drive.
