Located in the hill of Los Monteros and just a short drive away from the beach and Marbella centre, this renovated villa has breath-taking sea views with both contemporary elegance and traditional architectural elements. 

This villa is built with the highest quality materials and featuring lofty ceilings. Upon the entrance hall it leads you to the spacious living room with direct access to the terrace, 
The open-plan kitchen are fully equipped with top appliances from Gaggenau and Miele. 
On this level, there are two bedrooms, both with en-suite, guest toilet, study, and open kitchen/dinning room/lounge
On the upper level is the master bedroom with an ensuite bathroom, walk-in closet and a private terrace.
On the lower level there is an additional separate apartment for guest, a sauna room with its own bathroom, so does the gym, there is also a large storage room that also houses the pool and waterfall filtration systems.

Furthermore, it features solar panels for energy efficiency, fireplace, central air conditioning, and a garage for 3 cars..
There are also a well equipped outdoor kitchen, large pool, well maintained gardens, Jacuzzi, and charming gazebo.
