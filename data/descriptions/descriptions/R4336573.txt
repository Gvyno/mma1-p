Beautiful apartment in the beautiful neighborhood of Los Boliches at 140m from the beach walking.

Thanks to the location you find all services within reach, supermarkets, restaurants, the market Los Boliches, bus stop, train to Malaga, etc..
Easy access to the highway, being 20 minutes from Malaga International Airport and 20 minutes from Marbella.

The apartment has three large bedrooms, two bathrooms (one en suite), independent kitchen with access to a large covered terrace, living room with access to another terrace closed with glass curtains where you can take advantage of the good orientation to the Mediterranean Sea. The whole house has double glazing which makes it a quiet home without noise from the street.

The urbanization is quiet and well maintained, has paddle tennis court, swimming pool and green areas.
