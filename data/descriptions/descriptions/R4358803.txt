New to Market -  Located in the exclusive El  Soto de Marbella complex, this beautiful duplex penthouse apartment comes with 3 large terraces offering stunning panoramic sea and mountain views of the surrounding area.

Situated on the second floor, on entering the apartment you have a large and bright living room dining area and semi open plan fitted kitchen and utility room. The living room leads out directly to the first of its three terraces, with plenty of room for outside dining and enjoying the Andalucian sun as well as fantastic sea and mountain views. On this level you have two good sized guest bedrooms and a very large shared shower/bathroom. Upstairs leads to the propertys second terrace with views of the communal area as well as the huge bright and airy  en-suite master bedroom with its fireplace and its own very big private terrace offering more incredible sea and mountain views. The interior of the apartment has recently been painted.

There is plenty of storage space throughout the apartment, air conditioning in each room, electric shutters as well as a private underground parking space and store room. The communal gardens and community pool are immaculately kept and residents can access the El Soto de Marbella leisure centre with its tennis and padel course, 9 hole golf course sauna and Spa. The club house has a bar and restaurant open year round.
