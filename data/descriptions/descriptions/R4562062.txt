The building is located in the Capuchinos area, an 8-minute walk from the University of Málaga and 13 minutes on foot from the Historic Center of Málaga. Built in 1960, it has been recently refurbished, transforming it into a new building. It faces east and comprises three floors.

The ground floor has a main entrance of 12m² and a 64m² apartment. The apartment consists of a spacious 33m² living room with an integrated kitchen and access to a private 12m² patio. It has two bedrooms measuring 4m² and 11m², and a 4m² bathroom.

The first floor features an independent apartment. It includes a large 30m² living room with an integrated kitchen and access to a 4m² terrace. There are two bedrooms measuring 5m² and 12m², and two bathrooms of 3m² and 5m².

The second floor houses an independent apartment. It is arranged with a 25m² living room, an integrated kitchen, and access to a large 21m² terrace. There is one 11m² bedroom and a 4m² bathroom.

Each apartment is fully furnished and equipped. The property is sold with a tourist license for immediate operation.
