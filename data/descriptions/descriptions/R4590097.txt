Beautiful townhouse with two bedrooms, a bathroom and a toilet in the El Albañil urbanization (Mijas)
A very quiet and well-kept urbanization, located in Campo Mijas, consisting of 54 apartments and 34 townhouses in total.
The community has extensive gardens with a private pool, tennis court and children's play area. Sitting at the foot of Mijas, El Albañil has beautiful views of the Sierra de Mijas and the coast. Just 5 minutes from Fuengirola by car and 25 minutes from Malaga airport.
