A fabulous 3-bed duplex penthouse in La Perla with stunning sea views.

Stunning apartment with lift access in the very private urbanisation of La Perla de la Bahia, front line beach on the coast of Casares. 
From the urbanisation, you have direct access to the Blue Flag Casares beaches, the famous La Sal, Beach restaurant and the Finca Cortesin Beach Club. You can walk along the promenade and beach to neighbouring San Luis de Sabinillas with its numerous bars, restaurants and amenities or take a 5 minute drive up the Casares road to some of the best restaurants in the area. Within 10 minutes you can be in the typical whitewashed village and enjoy the fabulous views.

If you fancy a day out shopping, Marbella is only 20 minutes away, and Gibraltar is within commutable distance. You will also be spoilt for choice if you are a passionate golfer, there are numerous golf clubs within the vicinity including the Finca Cortesin Golf Club, just 5 minutes away.

Casares is a truly special, unspoilt area of the Costa del Sol, and this apartment is a must-see.
