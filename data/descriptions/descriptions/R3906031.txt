CLASSIC ANDALUCIAN STYLE VILLA 
Extravagant frontline golf villa, beautifully designed in a classic Andalucian style with all the luxuries and features of modern life. Accommodation consists of two en suite bedrooms on the ground floor with a separate sitting area. A beautifully detailed Clive Christian kitchen with a cathedral ceiling and decorative wooden beams; includes an island and informal dining room, complete with antique marble fireplace. The first floor comprises two double bedrooms with private terraces overlooking the garden, pool and golf course. The master bedroom boasts a private sitting area, dressing room, sunny terrace and large en suite. The ground floor accommodates a wine cellar, integrated bar, home cinema room, gym, sauna, steam room and kitchenette with direct access to the lower level of the garden. Accessed from an external entrance is a separate apartment with en suite double bedroom, sitting area and kitchenette for guest or staff. Luxury top qualities include central heating, separate air-conditioning hot & cold in bedrooms, iroko double glazed windows, stone & wood flooring and domotic system.
