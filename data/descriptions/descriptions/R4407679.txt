Classical style family situated in a large corner plot in the prestigious Monte Halcones off the Ronda road in the municipality of Benahavis. 
The villa is built in 2006 and is immaculately presented. Built over 2 levels and oozing a sensation of being at home.
LAYOUT > Entry level comprises a beautiful arched hallway leading onto to an impressive living room with double height ceiling with adjacent dining area. The room also features a large open fireplace. The dining area continues into a fully equipped kitchen with separate utility room. Also on this level are 2 bedrooms en-suite, cloakroom for guests. This level leads into the manicured garden with swimming pool and gazebo. 
The hallway staircase leads up to a gallery with lots of space for an office or a library featuring chesterfield armchairs and a coffee table for the afternoon cognac. To the opposite site of the gallery are further 2 bedrooms en-suite, both with private open terraces, one in a South orientation with sea views, the other is Northwest facing and perfect for the sunsets.
SPECS include under floor heating throughout, air-conditioning hot & cold, double glazing, etc. 

In addition to the villa a self-contained guest apartment is available, along with a large 3-car garage.

The villa enjoys easy access to Nueva Andalucia, also known as The Golf Valley, including San Pedro de Alcantara, which is a thriving, full-service town with all services and facilities close to the buzzing yacht marina Puerto Banus, including the beaches. Also, in the near vicinity are amenities and restaurants in Monte Halcones and in the picturesque La Heredia, including a brand-new health centre within 2 minutes' drive of the villa.

The picturesque village of Benahavis, widely renowned for cuisine, is within 10 minutes' drive of the villa.
