Wonderful 2 bedrooms apartment in one of the most luxurious gated urbanisations, Buenavista de la Quinta. Situated close to the Club House and Hotel, and with unbeatable panoramic sea views and very close to Monte Halcones commercial centre. This spacious, high-quality elevated ground floor apartment offers a large living with dining area, opening onto a south facing terrace with breathtaking panoramic sea, golf and mountain views, 2 good size en-suite bedrooms, one of which has access to the main terrace, guest toilet, and an independent kitchen fully fitted with Siemens appliances, with adjacent laundry room and access to a terrace. Some of the features include home automation system, underfloor heating in the bathrooms, hot and cold air conditioning, individually controlled in each room, electric shutters, fitted wardrobes, safe box, etc. The apartment comes with underground parking space and storage room.
