Marein Village is a gated community of 28 townhouses. This modern development has been designed for permanent living near to all services and amenities.

The corner unit has 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms plus a guest toilet and private terrace. Spacious living room, fully equipped open kitchen with laundry area are situated on the entrance floor. Upstairs 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. Independent air conditioning, porcelain floors of 120×60 and pre-installation of home automation. 

One parking space in the underground garage.

Marein Village has a perfect location, just 1.5 km away from Puerto Banús and the beaches.
