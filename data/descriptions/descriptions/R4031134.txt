A great location, right by the sea.
This Andalusian style townhouse has 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. The house has 2 floors and a solarium on the third floor.
On the first floor there is a living room with a dining room, a fully equipped kitchen, a guest bathroom and a patio with a barbecue. On the second floor there are 3 bedrooms and a bathroom with a bathtub.
In one of the bedrooms there is a hidden door that leads to the third floor - to the solarium area. The rooftop solarium has a beautiful space for sunbathing, dining under the sky or playing sports.
The patio features a covered grill and space for dining and more. The townhouse is located next to the bars, restaurants and a few steps from the beach.
There is a charming Andalusian style throughout the property that blends perfectly with the location of La Cala de Mijas.
