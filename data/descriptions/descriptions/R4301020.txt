Spectacular contemporary off-plan villa located in the renown Cascada de Camojan in Marbella. The property is distributed over three levels, and comprises, on entrance level: entrance hall with double height ceiling; spacious living-dining room with access to the covered porch, mature gardens and infinity swimming pool; fully fitted kitchen with center island, breakfast area and storage room; separate dining room; three en-suite guest bedrooms; and a guest toilet. Upstairs: en-suite master bedroom with dressing area and private terrace enjoying panoramic views to the Mediterranean sea and over the coast. Basement: games room; fully fitted gym; Spa area with sauna, turkish bath and bathroom; three en-suite guest bedrooms; staff apartment; private garage for 6 cars; and a guest toilet. Outstanding villa located in the heart of Marbella, close to all amenities and within a short drive to Puerto Banus. Completion: February-March 2024.
