Situated within a large residential complex with manicured gardens and several swimming pools, just a few minutes from the famous beaches surrounding the Port of Cabopino we find this spacious duplex penthouse, ready to move into. The property is in very good condition, it has a large living room with access to a first terrace with open views to the countryside and east facing, a fully fitted kitchen that could easily be opened to the living room and two bedrooms with fitted wardrobes and independent central air conditioning. The master has an en suite bathroom and there is another nice guest bathroom.  From the terrace there is access via a spiral staircase to the huge solarium where all options are possible: jacuzzi, summer kitchen, another bedroom etc.. The property is sold with a good underground parking space and a large storage room. Outside there is a beautiful swimming pool surrounded by gardens with jacuzzi. Its large green and landscaped areas with flowing rivers and beautiful ponds make its aquatic facilities a place for relaxation, fun and sport.
