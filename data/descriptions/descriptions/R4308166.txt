Not only is it a golfer's paradise, but it also enjoys an elevated position with beautiful views and an ideal location. This is an idyllic spot for living your Mediterranean life to the fullest, being close to amenities yet being tucked away for some tranquility. The unit comes with a garage space and storage unit.
