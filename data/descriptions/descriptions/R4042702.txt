Magnificent finca with pool in Casares, Costa del Sol, 9 km from Estepona, 1 hour drive to Malaga airport and 30 minutes drive to Gibraltar
The beautiful finca with pool of 30.000 m2 is located in the nature park in Casares, 8 km from the beach and 10 km from shops.

The house is surrounded by terraces and a 1000m2 fenced garden with fruit trees (orange, lemon, grapefruit) and palm trees.

The house has all the machines for the maintenance and care of the garden.
