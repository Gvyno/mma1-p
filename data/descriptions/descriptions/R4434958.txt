In the living room with fireplace, we find a small room at the back that serves as a reading room and library, also with access to the patio.

The rustic-style kitchen is fully equipped, has a fireplace and communicates with the patio. But it also has a large area to be used as a dining room.

On the first floor there are 3 en-suite bedrooms with dressing room and closets, one of them with an independent exit from the house.

The second floor presents the master bedroom with an en-suite bathroom and also a dressing room.
