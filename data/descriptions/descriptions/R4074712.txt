Property Details:

This country finca in Casarabonela boasts lovely views of the rolling countryside. The property has good access, a short drive from the main road. 

The property itself is laid out over two floors, you enter into a open plan living area with fully fitted kitchen. The bathroom is also on this floor. Upstairs are two consecutive double bedrooms. A shady porch wraps around the property and there is space to instal a temporary pool.

Outdoor area and Land:

The land is vast and usable with rolling hills and flat areas, perfect for livestock or agricultural use.
