Great plot with golf views and sea views from 1st level located in a quiet gated street in La Alqueria.

The plot has a proposed project with a modern designed villa by renowned architect Carlos Lamas. The price includes the licence and the project.

La Alqueria is an excellent area with luxurious villas, Atalaya golf on the doorstep and 5 minutes walk to an international school and to the closest supermarket.

The beach of San Pedro de Alcantara is a short drive and the town center offers an abundance of shops, cafes, bars and restaurants.
