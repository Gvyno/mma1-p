Country home with over 17,000m² of land planted with olive and avocado trees and with spectacular views of Alhaurín el Grande and the mountain range.

The property has 3 bedrooms, kitchen, large bathroom and living room with fireplace. It benefits from a large outdoor area with 2 storage rooms, barbecue, covered terrace, water well and a nice pool.

The property is situated in an ideal, very private location with beautiful views and close to all amenities.

The plot has about 240 olive trees, around 15 years old, various types such as Hoja Blanca, Carviñel and Marteña.
