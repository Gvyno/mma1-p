This immaculate second floor apartment is located in Miraflores. This 104 m2 two bedroom two bathroom apartment, built in 2003, is located in small gated community of Miraflores de Riviera. Entrance is into a Hallway leading through to a spacious and bright lounge/diner. There are two Bedrooms, both with fitted wardrobes, the master has en-suite bathroom and there is a separate guest bathroom. The lounge leads onto the west facing terrace. The kitchen is fully fitted. The property has hot/cold air conditioning and marble flooring throughout. The urbanisation benefits from communal pool and allocated parking. Just a short distance to all amenities including restaurants, bars, shops and golf.
