This penthouse in Las Lagunas offers an exceptional location, strategically located close to shops, restaurants and schools. Its proximity to the new Mijas park, the developing area and the new hospital make it an attractive option for those looking for comfort and accessibility. Just a 20-minute walk from the beach and in the vicinity of a shopping center, this place combines residential tranquility with proximity to services and entertainment.

The property consists of three large bedrooms with built-in wardrobes, two full bathrooms and a fully equipped independent kitchen. In addition, it has a laundry room. The living room connects with a beautiful and spacious terrace, perfect to enjoy with family and friends.

A notable feature is the possibility of installing a staircase to access an 80 meter solarium, offering additional space to relax and enjoy the sunny weather. Additionally, the property includes an underground parking space and a generous storage room to meet storage needs.
This property is available for viewings, providing the opportunity to explore all of its amenities and features first-hand.
