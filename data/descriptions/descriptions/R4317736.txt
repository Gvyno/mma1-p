Charming typical Andalusian style semi-detached house at the bottom of Calahonda. It has been completely renovated with new floors, new openings, new kitchen, air conditioning installed, etc. It is surrounded by a terrace, tiled on one side and wooden on the other side with direct access to the pretty communal gardens and swimming pool. This way you can enjoy the sun whenever you want or rest in the shade if you prefer. On the ground floor there is a beautiful living room with fireplace and a modern open kitchen and also a toilet with shower. Upstairs are the 2 bedrooms and a full bathroom with tub. The master bedroom opens onto a south-facing terrace with a pleasant open view where the sea can be seen through the trees.
From the house you can walk to all amenities and the beach.
Calahonda offers you all the necessary services at your fingertips, a family and quiet atmosphere all year round, just 10 minutes from Marbella and Fuengirola.
