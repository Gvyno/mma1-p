Just five minutes from the beach, in the heart of the emblematic neighborhood of Los Boliches, is this spacious apartment that has everything to start enjoying the hectic lifestyle of the Costa del Sol.

Its interior has a bright and spacious living room with air conditioning and heating, a fully equipped independent kitchen, three spacious bedrooms with fitted wardrobes and two bathrooms.

Located on the fifth floor, the property stands out for its comfortable terrace offering magnificent panoramic views of the city and overlooking the Parque del Rosario. Another attractive point of the property is its  community pool.

 It has easy access to all services, schools, shops, church, transport (train and bus), the best restaurants in the area and the fabulous Fuengirola promenade. Additionally, the exterior is adapted for people with reduced mobility.
