This impressive apartment is located on the first floor of a beautiful gated community in La Quinta, Benahavís. The apartment has a distribution of two bedrooms and two bathrooms, providing a spacious and comfortable space for your home.
One of the most outstanding features of this apartment is its beautiful closed terrace, facing west, which allows you to enjoy impressive panoramic views of the area. From the terrace you can appreciate the sunset and enjoy a calm and relaxed atmosphere.
The urbanization offers two community pools and impeccably maintained gardens, creating an oasis of tranquility and well-being for its residents. The pools are perfect for cooling off on warm days and for enjoying unforgettable moments with family or friends.
In addition, the gated community offers a safe and quiet environment to live. La Quinta is one of the most exclusive areas of Benahavís, surrounded by beautiful golf courses, just a few minutes from the beaches and with easy access to all the necessary services and amenities.
