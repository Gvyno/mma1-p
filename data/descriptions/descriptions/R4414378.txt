Beautifully refurbished 3 bed, 2 bath apartment in the sought after urbanisation of Los Naranjos, Nueva Andalucia.
The property is spacious and bright with a newly fitted open plan kitchen and dining area. Situated within beautiful gardens and access to community swimming in one of the many pools within the grounds.
Los Naranjos is ideally located within 2 mins. walk to shops, pharmacy etc. and next to the Aloha Golf Club.
By car it will take a 4 minute drive to the famous Puerto Banus and an 8 minute drive to the centre of Marbella.
