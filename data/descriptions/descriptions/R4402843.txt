A lovely two bedroom middle floor apartment in the urbanisation Hacienda del Sol. 
Hacienda del Sol is a gated community with 24h security, located at 200 meters walking distance to the beach. Into the community, you will have access to 6 pools, a gym, an outside jacuzzi and 2 paddle courts, which make it the perfect location as your holiday home. 
The apartment is located at the beginning of the urbanisation and consists of a large entrance with good sized living room and its access to the East facing terrace, overlooking the pool. 
There are two large bedrooms with 2 bathrooms. The master bedroom has fitted wardrobe, a Juliette Balcony, as well East facing and has an ensuite bathroom with shower and bath. 

This apartment is sold with an underground garage and a storeroom.
