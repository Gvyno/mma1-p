Immaculate front-line golf, South-West facing three-bedroom apartment situated in one of the best gated and secure complexes in Guadalmina Alta, within a short distance from San Pedro town the beaches, in Marbella West.  
This spacious and bright corner apartment was completely refurbished in 2018 including a heat pump system to provide economical underfloor heating and air conditioning throughout. Situated on the first floor it enjoys beautiful golf and garden views. 
The accommodation comprises of entrance hall, and a generous living/dining room leading to a huge wrap-around covered terrace with a glass enclosure, providing all-year-around comfort, and lovely views over the golf course. The newly renovated open-plan fully fitted kitchen also has golf views from the window. The master bedroom is an en-suite, and two guest bedrooms share a bathroom. One of the guest bedrooms has direct access to the terrace, providing a lovely space to sit and read or paint the views.  Other features are, porcelain flooring throughout, guest WC and one underground car parking space.
